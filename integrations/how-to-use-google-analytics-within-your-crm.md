# How to Use Google Analytics Within Your CRM

Google Analytics is a powerful tool to track traffic on your website and is optional. A Google Analytics account can be created for free by going to the Google website, [https://analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com/)

Google will provide a tracking code. Copy your tracking code from the Google Analytics website and paste it into the Tracking Code box. Do the same if you have a Site Verification Key.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ecf603a2-bafe-4cb0-95e3-aadecdc50fc7)

Click **Save** at the bottom of the page. Google will begin tracking traffic on your website. Now, you'll be able to use these analytics to find out who's visiting your website, how often, and when you generate the most traffic.
