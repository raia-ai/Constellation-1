# Date Synchronization | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   To synchronize the same date on your system, go to “Preferences” under the drop-down icon having name of your account.

  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5daf29d6-7f81-42dc-ae40-9be8784fd4c3)

*   On preferences page, scroll down and there you will see the “Referral Date Range” option.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/7bac63a1-6c1a-462f-9318-5e9220e1a4b5)

*   Click on “Referral Date Range” and select the available options from the drop-down to set your date accordingly.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d4421644-4c02-4557-8b86-a58a36c4d5e1)

*   Select the date according to your requirements.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/57d8dd3b-8935-4c15-a6ef-16cc82e93143)

*   By adjusting the date, you will see it being synchronized in sections and set across your system. i.e. Clients, Autopilots, Overdue etc.
