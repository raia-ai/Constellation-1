
# Broker Center Integration via Chrome Plug-in

*   To integrate Broker Center via Chrome plug-in, first install/add the plugin-in extension in your Chrome browser with the below link.
    
    Link: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/relocation-referral-impor/nhipeejphhagobmbggbmeciphhdgajea
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6159adf9-5c3a-4944-a6a2-0c6445e55c50)

*   Now go to the Broker Center website and log into your account by entering the credentials.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/4abcfbad-8a29-43f1-9666-bfbd53951e8d)

*   Click on “Referral Mgmt.” and select “Open/Ref Number” from the drop-down menu on the left-hand side of the website.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e3383f97-37ba-49e0-acdd-ab5bdb24e91d)

*   Enter the referral number and click on the “Open” button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fda35358-3927-44f0-8fb0-3d9a566137ff)

*   View the required Referral lead by clicking on Referral # and expand it by clicking on the “View details” option.

                                            ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ec954994-7977-4170-a447-be6c23c5029d)![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/76781b6c-467e-43f7-9f0d-72e857d5aa81)

*   Now click on the Constellaion1 Broker Center plug-in/extension, click the “Import Broker Center Referral” button, and wait for the data conversion, once completed you will be prompted to the Constellation1 Relocation website.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fbed2224-d988-4d42-9fa4-1a3df74b6836)  

*   Review the details and add or edit them before clicking the “Save” button. Clicking it will convert the Broker Center lead as a referral in Constellation1 Relocation.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e9ebed68-01f0-4287-b52b-6059255e60d5)
