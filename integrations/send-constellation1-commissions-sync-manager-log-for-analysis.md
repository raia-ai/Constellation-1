# Send Constellation1 Commissions Sync Manager Log for Analysis

This article details the steps required to provide the Constellation1 Commissions Sync Manager Log to a member of the Customer Experience Team for analysis by our Development Team.

Maximize Constellation1 Commissions Sync Manager and click anywhere on the blue Constellation1 Commissions Sync Manager v2.0 title.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2b024f2f-9ffe-4840-b754-74d35d2810fa)

A new window will open where user will find the "logyyyymmdd".  Users can copy/paste the appropriate log to their desktop or navigate to its location to attach to this ticket.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ed31d881-d2a1-46a0-ab1c-5314df16072f)
