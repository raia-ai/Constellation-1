
# Adding a Referral to a transaction

This article will serve as a reference for when adding and paying out a referral on a transaction.

If the contract states that you are obliged to pay a referral on a transaction, depending on which side of the transaction, the Referral is added on the Commission Tab of the transaction.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9e9e420f-2681-4249-b4e9-33d7b33cb38b)

To deduct Referral "off the top"

On the appropriate side that the referral needs to be added to, select **+Add Referra**l. Example above shows referral on the Selling Side

As usual, search on the referral's name and select from the list if they appear, otherwise select **Add New Contact** and add the contact as appropriate

**Please note**: if you have tax enabled in your program, the applicable tax will NOT be applied to the referral unless the appropriate tax number is entered in their contact file

If referral amount is a percentage enter the percentage in the adjacent field. 

If it is a flat amount,  click on the percentage button to change to a dollar sign and enter the dollar amount accordingly

To **delete** the referral, select the ellipsis on the far right of the referral and select **Delete**

To **configure** the referral, select the ellipsis on the far right of the referral and select **Advanced Options**

*   Options such as selecting the Referring Franchise if appropriate, including Unshared and Share Non-Commission Income, the Calculation Basis and whether it is exempt from Franchise Fee may be selected and then select **Save**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e474b494-5b26-41a0-94d8-b845cb4c2c67)

**To add Referral being deducted from Pre-Split amount**

Should the agent be contributing to the referral's disbursement and it is being deducted from the pre-split amount, navigate to the agent details screen by selecting the Agent Details button ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5701266f-6a9f-4723-9e3c-f97164254494) to the far right of the agent on the transaction and select **+Add Referral** and add the referral as above, selecting from the search if the contact exists or adding appropriately.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a40e1d54-af03-46ad-8ebf-9dfe19a0678e)

The referral will then be paid at closing as appropriate either through integration with QuickBooks® or your Accounting system or by a third party if Commissions Disbursed by a Third Party is selected
