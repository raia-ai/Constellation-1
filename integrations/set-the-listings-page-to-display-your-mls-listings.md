
# Set the Listings Page to Display Your MLS Listings

The Listings Page Feature allows you to display listings from various sources. Your MLS listings can be viewed on your website by creating a listings page and choosing the type of MLS Property.

**Step 1:**

1.  Log in to Top Producer® Websites at [https://www.topproducerwebsite.com](https://www.topproducerwebsite.com/)
2.  Click My Account
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%201.jpg)
    
3.  Click Third-Party Integrations
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%202.jpg)
    
4.  Under 'Enter your credentials to display your MLS listings' enter your Agent ID.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%203.jpg)
    
5.  Under Board Name/City, click the drop list and select your MLS.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%204.jpg)
    
6.  Click Save Integrations.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%205.jpg)
    

**Step 2:**

1.  From the main menu click Edit Site, or choose Edit My Pages from the Quick Tools section of the main page. The Website Manager window opens.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%208.jpg)
    
2.  Click Manage My Pages. The Manage My Pages window opens.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%202.jpg)
    
3.  Hover over the Listings Page from the menu. Click the pen icon to edit the page.  The Edit Page window opens.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%206.jpg)
    
4.  In the Types of Listings section, select _MLS Property_ from the Select the type of listings to show dropdown menu. If you have not already integrated your MLS, you’ll be prompted to do so here (Step 1).
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-Listings/TWS-Listings%207.jpg)
    
5.  Click the Preview button if you’d like to preview the page in a new window.
6.  Click Save Page to save and publish the page.

If you have any questions or require additional assistance, please contact support by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner of your Top Producer Website control panel, or email [support@topproducer.com](mailto:support@topproducer.com)
