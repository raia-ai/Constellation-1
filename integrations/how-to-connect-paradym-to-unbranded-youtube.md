# How To Connect Paradym To Unbranded YouTube

This article reviews how to connect your Paradym account to an Unbranded YouTube channel&#x20;

Want to effortlessly boost your search engine optimization (SEO)? YouTube is the second search engine in the world. Only second to Google. Google owns YouTube. As a result, you make it easier for users already looking for you, find you. However, if you want to post videos without your company branding information, this tutorial is for you. &#x20;

As always, the following instructions involve logging into your Paradym online account. Head over to Paradym.com, click My Account. Log into your account. Then, follow the instructions below Steps A - L.&#x20;

(A). Log into your Paradym account, Select your name at the top right for a drop down menu, and select "Settings."

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/cdbc172c-baeb-4c26-a33f-caa3308fed0d.png)

(B). Click, “Connections,” under Social Media. Alternately, you can also click ”Change” under Social Connections.&#x20;

![Graphical user interface, text, application
Description automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6326d405-acc8-4642-846c-d4d548b63b5f.png)

&#x20;(C). Click the plus sign (+) next to "YouTube (Unbranded)."

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/fdaa1289-0788-404f-9e9e-5c8d631a5282.png)

(D). NOTE: YouTube (Unbranded) will NOT connect to the same channel you connect under your regular YouTube connections. They MUST be two different channels &#x20;

(E). Next, Select An Account You Want To Connect; Notice your options you can select from. Select your choice.

![Graphical user interface, text, application
Description automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d72e3af4-fba5-41f5-bbc1-33fb23415ae4.png)

(F). NOTE: You will chose the channel you want Paradym to post too. The profile will say YouTube under it. If you DO NOT see this, Paradym Support (phone number located at the end of this article) will help you set a channel up. A channel set is necessary for Paradym listing syndication to YouTube.&#x20;

(G). Select "Allow" &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e1be89a6-d6d6-4204-bbcd-f8967cf32f0b.png)

(H). YouTube Settings: &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/7a2fa7cd-57a4-4cc1-bdad-8f45cb1b80dc.png)

(I). Notes and Options:

1\. If you have any existing tours prior to connecting to your YouTube channel, you have the option to send them to YouTube all at once. &#x20;

* Just checkmark the option= "You have “X” active tours that have not been posted. Post these tours?” &#x20;

2\. Delete my videos for sold tours. &#x20;

* Videos will automatically delete from your channel 36 hours after a tour is marked "Sold." Saving you time..

3\. Notice:  "Use tour cover photo as YouTube video thumbnail."

* This will set your first photo, usually the front exterior, as the lead photo of your video. &#x20;
* Therefore in order make this active, Locate=  ” \* Requires your YouTube account to be verified. To verify your account, please visit this link.” &#x20;

\>> Click the provided “link” and follow prompts. &#x20;

4\. Once verified, hit "Save."

(J). YouTube V.S. YouTube (Unbranded) &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/52e0acb4-d659-4db6-80c5-a35906084991.png)

1\. YouTube

* Will display company branding and agent contact information.&#x20;
* You can resubmit changes. &#x20;

2\. YouTube Unbranded &#x20;

* Simply displays photos. &#x20;
* You cannot resubmit changes. &#x20;
* Call Support for assistance.&#x20;

(K). NOTE: You CAN 'NOT' connect the same channel to both YouTube and YouTube (Unbranded) Paradym connections. It will NOT work.  &#x20;

* You will need to dedicate a different channel to each Paradym connection. &#x20;

(L). Please call Support at 800-873-0700 option 1 for further assistance. Support is available Monday - Friday from 9am ET to 8pm ET.

Thank you!
