# Constellation1 Commissions Limitations | Constellation1 Customer Hub

This article highlights some of the requirements and features not supported in the Constellation1 Commissions system: 

*   Companies servicing different states or where there is multi-ownership and therefore have unique TAX ID/EIN numbers must purchase separate licenses for each TAX ID. The company will also have to create an additional file in QuickBooks® to map the TAX ID accordingly.

Listings

*   Active Listings can be included via import however there is minimal support for listing management within Commissions (reports and other related functionality). If companies require listing management they should be recommended the purchase of Listing Manager.

Commercial Transactions

*   Commercial transactions (industrial, retail etc), Leases, Property or Mortgage Management, or Time shares--Commissions can support commission calculations for these types of transactions but reports are unavailable.

Commissions and Fees

*   Commissions currently supports up to 13 commission plan levels (12+1) and 6 transaction related fees.
*   Mere Postings for commission paid in advance is not supported
*   Three way split of commission (40/30/30) is not supported
*   Fees included in commission calculations but not actually received by anyone in the company are not supported. For example Brokerage received $5,000 but pays out $4500.
*    Manager Override bonuses are calculated on the number of transactions (ends) or by anniversary/Calendar Year.

General Ledger

*   General Ledger number field in Commissions and QuickBooks® supports up to 7 characters.
*   Separate expenses General Ledger® for each agent is not supported.
*   Commissions supports unlimited number transaction types for reporting purposes but integrates with a limit of 3 additional Commission Income Accounts and 3 additional Agent commission Expense Accounts.
*   Commissions does not support any bank sub-accounts in QuickBooks®.
*   QuickBooks®- Modules
*   Payroll module is supported if agents entered as Employees. Users will manually run Unscheduled Payroll being paid from Operating Account.
*   Commissions will not offset an Accounts Receivable (A/R) invoice to a landlord/seller for the payment of commission for any type of transaction (commercial or residential). The integration is for A/R for agents only.
*   Un-deposited Funds for Agents Receivables, Commissions must have an A/R account in order for users to deduct invoices expense from Agents Closing Checks.

Taxes

*   Only 1 Tax calculation is supported (i.e. GST or HST or PST); the exception is City or State Tax (one per company profile) which can be supported in addition to this tax calculation.
*   Tax calculations do not cross levels based on sales price nor can be set up as a flat fee - always a percentage.
