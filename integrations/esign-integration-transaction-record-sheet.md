# eSign Integration > Transaction Record Sheet

This article details the information required to configure and use eSign Integration.

If you would like to add eSign Integration to your account for Transaction Record Sheet signatures, please email [commissions@constellation1.com](mailto:commissions@constellation1.com) and a member of our Team will be happy to assist.

Before you begin, please ensure the email address attached to your Company is the best email for all eSign communication.  To do this, on the left navigation bar select **System Settings / Company**.  If you change the existing email, do not forget to save your changes.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/a8cbc28c-d7a7-48ff-91c3-68c4ad765a49.png)

Only Non-Agent/Administrative Users can be selected as the Company Contact to Sign for eSign Integration. For additional information please click here; [Add, Edit, Delete or Deactivate Non-Agent User.](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/17343576)  In addition, canceled transactions are exempt from eSign Integration.&#x20;

To initiate the signing session, navigate to the Commission tab of the transaction, edit the Agent and select the Transaction Record Sheet icon.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/af2add0a-1462-4e3b-8379-1f1d496f6e1a)

You will be presented with the following Transaction Record Sheet window;

Toggle ON, Send via eSign for signature(s), select the template from the drop down and by default, the logged in user will automatically populate as the Company Contact to Sign but can be changed if required.&#x20;

The Agent(s) attached to the transaction will automatically display as the Agent to Sign and cannot be changed. If there is more than one agent attached to the same end of a transaction, each agent will receive their own copy of a Transaction Record Sheet to sign.

Select **Send**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/534c975b-f2ce-4216-b1ae-b1279732e642)

If the Company email has been changed since the last time a Transaction Record Sheet was sent for signature, you will receive the following prompt.  Make your selection accordingly.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/08e37e66-c931-4ddd-b2f9-49c9c06146ca)

Once sent, you will receive a confirmation prompt and the Documents tab of transaction will provide you with the Status.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/11365074-f88e-4d52-ac89-8639475914d9)

The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will receive an email comparable to below with a subject of Signing invite: and include the address and city of the transaction.  The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will select Review & Sign button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0c3680dd-7136-4348-a284-93a5d04d13bc)

The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign can Decline, Contact Sender or Agree and Start Signing.

If Declined, the Documents tab of the transaction will provide you an updated status and the Company email will receive a notification as well.  If you wish to resend, you must cancel the session from the Documents tab of the transaction and start again.&#x20;

Contact Sender will open your default email application with the Company email automatically populated.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c1422cb8-56e6-4b1f-8a6d-918f87d19e13)

If the Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign agree, there will be a signature line and date box at the bottom of the Transaction Record Sheet where they will click in the field to auto populate.  If multiple contacts were selected, there will be multiple signature lines and dates.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3af0c40d-90eb-4d61-b647-bf2fbe1af8b7)

Once the signature and date are populated, the Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will select Done at the top left of the window.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fdff9228-e864-42d4-8441-8d8e30685fc9)

The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will receive a confirmation prompt, select YES and the the Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will be presented with the following;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3cfc56d3-e12a-4add-8107-d64df2350bd2)

The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will receive a subsequent email indicating that their signed document is ready for download. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/73848dcf-74dc-4a3d-8a4c-3411060c2ac3)

The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign have signed the document, the The Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign will receive a final email comparable to below.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ee3fa579-c051-48f1-85e8-865b75a5f107)

The Documents tab of the transaction will display an updated status and you will be able to download the documents for your records. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/35381914-21bd-47b3-8593-52362cff955d)

The zipped folder will be saved to the "Downloads" folder of your PC/laptop and contains both the signed Transaction Record Sheet as well as a Certificate Of Authenticity.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5721ac5c-5bdf-42e5-8db5-bce7b97819ca)

**Please note, you cannot resend a request if not received. You will need to cancel the request and reinitiate the signing session for the Agent(s) and Company Contact to Sign.**&#x20;

**eSign Sessions sent for the month can be found on the** [**Billing Report.**](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/23453494)
