# My Profile | Constellation1 Customer Hub

The My Profile section in Documents allows Agents, Brokers, and Site Administrators to manage their personal information, Team settings, and other Preferences. This guide will walk you through the steps to access and update My Profile.

*   **Accessing My Profile:**
    *   Use your credentials to log in to the Documents system.
    *   Click on the My Profile option located in the drop-down in Select at the top of the Dashboard.
        *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2ccb803d-00d7-458f-99ea-d4e77556601e)

*   **My Profile Information:**

*   **Assistants/Team Management: (Not available to Site Administrator)**
    *   If you are part of a Team, you can manage your Assistants or Team settings from the My Profile section:

*   **Preferences: (Not available to Site Administrator)**
    *   The following options are available to configure under Preferences:
        *   Default View: Tile or Grid.
        *   Sort Order: Ascending or Descending.
        *   Sort Column: Column used to apply Sort Order (Date Created, Closing Date, Property Address, and Contact Name).
