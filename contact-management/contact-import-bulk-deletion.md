# Contact Import - Bulk Deletion

*   Log in to rDesk CRM
*   Click Contacts on the left menu
*   Click Contacts in the submenu for contacts
*   Where it says "Filter By" Choose "Advanced Filters" in the dropdown menu; there's two ways you can filter for these folks. As you said, the import date is one, and the "Source" is the other.
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d922fd80-7cc3-4c04-87c5-7253718de6ed)

**Filter by Date Created**

*   Within the advanced filters click the words "Date Created"
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2c45acbf-9d60-42c0-895a-92a92ef02198)
*   Then choose the radio button for "Specify Date Range"
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fc754239-804e-475e-9990-2c4ee615c638)
*   Put your start and end date
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a83c979a-b05f-4880-b817-67b70f10ff4a)

**Filter by Source**

*   Within the advanced filters click the word "Source"
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/df747663-8df4-4ab3-8ec1-751f05d7c9bf)
*   In the "Filter By Source" menu, scroll to rDesk - ManualImport
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e9d4f1e8-4cdf-4c87-bfe4-c6516f2a633d)

**Next Steps for either lookup method**

*   Click Search
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/31e34b17-6e6c-4778-a012-345b276f1c85)
*   Select all contacts by clicking the checkbox on the left side of the header row
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a4d0d50c-0160-4392-9e10-a3fcfaf07c50)
*   Click the dropdown for "Bulk Action"
*   ![image.png](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9ef7e6bb-ba3a-4237-af3c-2a6e18b8c031)
*   Chose "Delete Selected"
