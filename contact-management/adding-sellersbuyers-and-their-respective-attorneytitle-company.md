# Adding Sellers/Buyers and their Respective Attorney/Title Company

The purpose of this article is to provide the steps to add sellers/buyers and their respective attorney or title company.

Navigate to Transaction and select EDIT&#x20;

Select NEW icon&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18028463)

Within Seller/Buyer Detail window, select SEARCH icon &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18028464)

Lookup name OR ADD, once highlighted, select OK &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18028465)

To add additional contacts such as the Attorney/Title company follow the same steps as above.  &#x20;
