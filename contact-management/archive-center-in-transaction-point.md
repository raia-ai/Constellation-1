# Archive Center in Transaction Point

**Archive Center**

The Archive Center allows you to download transactions, archive them to your computer, and/or burn them to a Compact Disk. The files are saved as a readable .PDF file. You can then provide the file to agents and clients. Before you can use Archive Center, you must download the TransactionPoint Archive client application.

**Note:** Site Administrators can choose whether or not Agents have the ability to archive files. If you do not see the Archive Center link, contact your site administrator.

**Downloading and Installing the Archive Utility**

**Note:** If you have not installed the current TP Archive Utility, follow the steps below to install or update the software. This must be done before you burn CDs.

1.  Go to your Transaction Point website and login. From the Main Navigational Toolbar, select

**Transactions**, Click on **Archive Center**.

2.  In the Archive Center, click on the link: **TP Archive Utility** to download the software.

  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/f1b7adb9-c633-4d67-956e-0705a93f70bc.png) 

3.  Click on **Run** in the window shown below:  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e6523011-d4c0-4c94-a1a7-0a592104caf5.png) 

The screen below is shown to indicate that the software is installing on your computer:  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/2897102d-9af7-44c0-9fef-b77c11001b7c.png) 

4.  Click **Run** in the Security Warning window (shown below) that pops up.  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e5f5f009-ecbc-4116-b0cf-5e40d81e69b5.png) 

5.  Click **Next** in the TP Archive‐InstallShield Wizard window (shown below) that pops up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/250bdfa0-993c-402c-ae3f-002725a33e0f.png) 

6.  Type your name and company name, select **Anyone who uses this computer (all users)**, and click **Next**.  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/aab3124c-8000-40c5-9723-03813951ab4a.png) 

7.  Click **Install**.
    
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/3b78580f-d3d7-4cf4-bf64-ec59fcec8a47.png)
    

The **Installing TP Archive** window appears.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c315a58d-8f1a-4225-8768-f8bbc5d2e141.png)

8.  When the **InstallShield Wizard Completed** window appears, click **Finish**. You are now ready to archive transactions**.  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/31074950-cf09-4876-8b96-9e03ef268edc.png)** 

**Archiving Transactions**

**Note:** Before writing the CD, the TP Archive utility ensures that there is enough room on the CD. If there is not enough room, a warning appears.

1.  To archive a transaction, go to the **Archive Center** and select transaction(s) by clicking the box to the left of the property address.
2.  Select the type of archive you wish to execute:  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/16a4fb4e-34ea-4ff2-b272-35a8035de63c.png) 

**Full** ‐ Allows you to archive the entire file, including the communications log and private and public documents, for the Broker’s or Agent’s records.

**Public** ‐ Allows you to archive a limited view of the file (no communications log or private documents) to be given to participants in the transaction.

**Custom** ‐ Allows you to archive a customized list of documents and activities. Customized archive is available when you upload documents directly to TransactionPoint or fax documents into TransactionPoint using the fax cover sheet. This feature can also be made available using defaults set in the Activities section by the Site Administrator. Additionally, you may archive multiple transactions at one time by selecting the check boxes to the left of the transaction address.

**Buyer** ‐ The Buyer archive will include transaction details, key dates, transaction contacts, public activities, order summary, public documents and any private documents where the Buyer has viewing access.

**Seller** ‐ The Seller archive will include transaction details, key dates, transaction contacts, public activities, order summary, public documents and any private documents where the Seller has viewing access.

3.  Insert a blank CD.
4.  Click **Open**.

The TP Archive Write window appears.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/053b8f86-297a-49d1-b4b8-c8456e5fb574.png)

**Note:** If this window appears, click **Cancel**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/93fa1fa0-ff0c-49ec-a9db-5b6fa8e10074.png) 

5.  Click **Yes** to reload the CD.  
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/5a949cae-136b-4bc4-8e10-532d3be890e6.png) 

6.  Choose **Close Archive CD using TP Archive**.
    
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/8edf525e-d0b2-4472-b1f0-15336e52c711.png)
    

7.  Click **OK**.

8.  Click **Yes** to confirm your choice to close the CD.
    
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/85b16dd4-52d7-45ef-808a-a66f4877482f.png)
    
9.  To view the CD, close the CD drawer. The CD launches automatically.

**Opening an Archive .PDF File**

1.  Insert the CD into your CD‐ROM drive. The TP Archive Center page appears with a hyperlink for each address on the CD.
2.  Click the address you want to view. The **Property Information** page appears.
3.  Click **Transaction Summary Report**.
    
    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4de8aad8-b529-4f1e-b681-fed164d9d7fe.png)
    

You can now print the summary.

**Customizing Archive Options**

**Including a Document Using a Fax Cover Sheet**

This feature is available in TransactionPoint anywhere that you can create a fax cover sheet. Once you choose to create a fax cover sheet, the following Fax Cover Sheet page appears. Check the **Include in Customized Archive** box; the faxed documents will be included on the Customized Archive CD you create in the Archive Center.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dff96493-7d15-468e-b1d7-cff6a4ff94a4.png) 

**Including a Document Using the Upload Feature**

You can include a document in your Customized Archive when you upload the document directly from your computer.

1.  In the Transaction Documents window, click **Upload**.

(You can click on the “plus” sign to expand the Documents section within the

**Activities/Orders/Docs** tab)

  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/630a80f1-9c72-4e49-938c-4e259c4fd339.png) 

The Upload Document window appears:  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/54e98535-ff78-4eee-81ef-1f700fd89033.png) 

2.  Click **Yes** next to **Include in Customized Archive**.
