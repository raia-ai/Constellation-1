# Accounting - Removing Credit Card Payments from Bank Batches

The purpose of this article is to provide the steps needed to remove an agent's credit card payment from a bank deposit batch. 

In instances when an agent's credit card was charged in error, you must first contact CSIPay and request that the agent's payment be removed from the submitted credit card batch.  Then follow the steps below, to make corrections in the program:   

*   Navigate to **Bank > Processing > Batch Deposits**.
*   Select **Cash Account** from dropdown.
*   Highlight the bank batch that includes the payment you would like to remove.
*   Click **Edit**.
*   Highlight the payment you would like to remove.
*   Click the red "**X**" to remove the payment line item from the batch.
*   Click **Yes** to the pop-up.

Example of pop-up below:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b3418e54-ac06-431f-9e5f-9ca451594f02)

*   Reduce the **Total Deposit** amount by the amount of the payment that was removed, to ensure the "Amount Left to Distribute" is $0.00.

Example shown below:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ccf4f912-00a8-45e3-9982-46cc81ca4b2f)

*   Click **Save**.
*   Navigate to **Bank > Processing > Non-Commission Deposits**.
*   Find the **Non-Commission Deposit** record for the agent.
*   Highlight the record, then click **Delete**.
