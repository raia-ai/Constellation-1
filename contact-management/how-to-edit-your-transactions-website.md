# How to edit your Transactions website

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/760f2ce8-6827-41f4-ad0f-84c235883fdc)

*   You can edit your Transactions website by logging in to your Admin account
*   Once logged in, you can change/modify the following:
    1.  **Company Info**
    2.  **About Us**
    3.  **Service Providers**
    4.  **Company Links**
    5.  **Color Theme**
    6.  **Logo**
    7.  **TSP Page Banner**
*   To access these options, log in to your Transactions Admin account, go into "Edit Web Site" under "Site Settings", and click on the "Edit" option for the relevant field/category that you want to update.
*   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0b64b4ca-9b50-4808-93d7-33803be36c5c)

*   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1cc0e64c-d7d4-4612-b0f3-dcad31ff6e94)

*   **Company Info**
    *   The information entered on this page will be displayed on the "Contact Us" page of the system's home page.
    *   The fields labeled with (\*) are required.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/379548de-0ff1-4a62-af9d-eafd4119a141)

*   **About Us**
    *   Use this screen to enter descriptive information about your company.
    *   The "About Us Title" and the "About Us" text fields support both plain text and HTML.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c92451e2-47b7-4f54-bfd4-dc807bd85e93)

*   **Service Providers**
    *   Use this screen to add or edit information about your preferred service providers.
    *   When adding a new service provider, fill in the "Service Provider" and "About Service Provider" fields and click on "Add".
    *   The "Service Provider" and the "About Service Provider" text fields support both plain text and HTML.
    *   When editing an existing service provider, first click on the service provider from the service provider list and click on "Update" to save the changes.
    *   When removing service providers, check the corresponding check box by each of service providers and click on "Remove".
    *   To clear the fields click on "Clear".
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/bf6dcc0a-a175-4c1a-80af-9658a2b8a3cf)

*   **Company Links**
    *   Use this screen to add or modify your Company links.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/56f57e26-8a17-4e79-9a51-868fcf2b5774)

*   **Color Theme**
    *   Select a color theme from the "Select Theme" drop down list.
    *   Once a new theme is selected, a preview is displayed in this window for your reference.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/af555a99-4565-42ea-8c98-145fa9352887)

*   **Logo**
    *   To upload a new company logo or replace an existing logo, click on the "Choose File" button.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/01b3fdcf-6a0c-4503-8466-249b8a19a465)

*   **TSP Page Banner**
    *   To upload a new banner or replace an existing banner, click on the "Choose File" button.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/59f646e8-30a0-4f95-9352-27e70c1db337)
