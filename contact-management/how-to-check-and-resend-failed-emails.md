
# How to check and resend Failed Emails

If your emails fail to deliver when sending e-signature requests or session invitations, follow these steps to review and resend them from the dashboard.

### **Step 1: Log in to eSign**

1.  Visit the eSign website.
2.  Enter your credentials and click **Login**.

### **Step 2: Navigate to the Dashboard**

1.  After logging in, you'll land on the Dashboard.
2.  The Dashboard provides metrics for Total Sessions, Delivered Emails, Failed Emails, and more.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dcdc8e20-f026-41fd-ba35-46fa5c7d2faf.png)

### **Step 3: Access the Failed Emails Section**

1.  Scroll down to the Failed Emails panel on the Dashboard.
2.  Here, you can see columns for:
    *   Email Address
    *   Session Name
    *   User
    *   Email Type
    *   Date
    *   Error Type
    *   Reason
    *   Actions

If there are no records, the system will display "No data available.".

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/5da628f3-f8d7-4d56-9bc1-aca7b7ae3660.png)

### **Step 4: Filter Failed Emails (Optional)**

1.  Use the Search Bar to type a specific email address or Session name.
2.  Select the Failure Type from the dropdown to refine your search further.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/813471c7-5235-4ca9-baa6-d24ecf28f695.png)

**OR**

3\. You can filter by Date Range if you are looking for a particular Session from a different date.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/82673e2f-f4f0-4cfc-8967-8a46186415e2.png)

### **Step 5: Remove from Blacklist**

1.  Once the results are visible based on your selection and you can view the undeliverable email address, you can click on the Action button and select Remove from Blacklist.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b8c8eb85-e507-48b6-9ac6-f0f5ea59cca1.png)

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e9f2c8a2-a15b-48a3-8d8b-6c8b44d85cff.png)

You can now resend the Session invite to the Recipient.

### **Additional Tips:**

*   Ensure that the Recipient's email address is correct.
*   Please inform the Recipient to not mark emails from eSign as Spam.
*   Check for any temporary server issues or undeliverable errors in the Reason column.
    *   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/3f64a174-f4c9-41eb-96d9-dbddc396fd6f.png)
*   For bulk email failures, contact eSign Support at [eSign@constellation1.com](mailto:eSign@constellation1.com) for assistance.
