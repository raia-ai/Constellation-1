# Adding IDX Pages to Your Top Producer Website

**Step 1:** Get the URL for Your IDX Site

You will need a frameable IDX URL from your IDX vendor to add an IDX page to your website. You may use the IDX vendor of your choice, including your MLS.

As steps on locating the frameable IDX URL differ, please consult your IDX vendor if you are unable to locate your IDX URL.

**Step 2:** Add the IDX Page to Your Top Producer® Website

1.  Log in to Top Producer® Websites at [https://www.topproducerwebsite.com](https://www.topproducerwebsite.com/)
2.  Click Edit My Site.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%201.jpg)
    
3.  Click Manage My Pages.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%202.jpg)
    
4.  Click Add Main Page.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%203.jpg)
    
5.  Click 3rd Party IDX. The New IDX or New 3rd Party IDX screen appears.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%204.jpg)
    
6.  Enter the Page Name, SEO Title, SEO Description, and SEO Keywords.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%205.jpg)
    
7.  In the 3rd Party IDX Link section, paste your frameable IDX URL.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TWS-IDX/TWS-IDX%206.jpg)
    
8.  When done, click Preview to take a look, then click Save Page.

If you have any questions or require additional assistance, please contact support by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner of your Top Producer Website, or email support@topproducer.com
