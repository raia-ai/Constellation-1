# 7/22/2025 - Important Update: Greater Southern MLS Data Feed Transition

We are writing to inform you of a significant update regarding the **Greater Southern MLS data feed (OSN GreaterSouthern)**. The Southwest Louisiana Association of REALTORS data that was previously included in this feed has been removed by the source system. **This data is now accessible through its own dedicated feed (OSN SouthwestLA)**.

The new OSN feed has been enabled for your access. Should you have any questions or require further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.
