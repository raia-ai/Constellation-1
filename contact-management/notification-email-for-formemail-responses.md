# Notification email for Form/Email responses

*   To get notified via email when a new form and email response is received in your system, go to the “Users” page under the drop-down icon having the name of your account.

                                       ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9f08c307-62e8-419c-b327-18946a6fc436)

*   On the Users page, edit your user details by clicking on 3 dots menu … under the Actions column.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1de6b954-fae4-4c3a-a520-c20277f03128)

*   On the User edit page, go to the Contact tab and enter the email address on which you wish to receive notifications. Then, click the “Save” button to update your record.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/38f194ac-f2c2-437e-8b75-4dc7bb1ca7f9)

*   Go to the “Preferences” page under the drop-down icon having the name of your account and update your notification settings.

                                                  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/809da2c0-d37c-4360-9487-74ede6ae7a58)

*   Make sure to enable the “Notify Email Response” and “Notify Form Response” buttons on the Preferences page to receive email notifications for Form/Email responses.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5d42cee5-c5b3-42c1-acef-6601bf2340a4)
