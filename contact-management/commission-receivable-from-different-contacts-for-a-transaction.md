
# Commission Receivable From Different Contacts for a Transaction

This article provides the steps to configure a transaction when different contacts pay a portion of the Commission Amount ensuring accurate financial records in your Accounting System once integrated. 

Navigate to applicable Transaction / Commission tab

In the screen shot below, the Commission Amount is $75,000.00 which means that the amount that each contact may pay cannot exceed the Commission Amount. 

Select the appropriate contact in the Commission Receivable From field. The drop down displays the same contacts found on the Contacts tab of transaction. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f9f2504b-4e47-4830-abcf-dd0a98a7dd35)

Once selected, you can edit the contact's amount, date, etc. or delete if you selected the incorrect contact.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/dece1940-fffb-400e-b48f-0a752178fa78)

Select Edit icon to edit the details as needed and select Update. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/13ff27ae-5cbe-41c7-97a2-1ce6f062da87)

To add the next contact and amount, select the ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a78e661f-aa09-4cee-b1c8-e5f9a4489bbe) icon, complete the details as needed and select Save. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e0ecfce6-a46e-4d53-9540-8ac656f502b0)

Once you are finished entering contacts and amounts, save the transaction.

You are now ready to close the transaction where you will see the amounts as configured display on the Review Closing Report. 

If accurate, select Process. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/bd345981-8b1c-47f0-b911-b236825e43f8)

Once integrated, you will see two separate deposits as configured which will now be accurate when you reconcile your bank accounts monthly. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/cefe563e-a9a6-49c2-af68-00ac67844c6e)
