# Viewing Market Snapshot Reports and Listing Alerts

Top Producer® X CRM allows you to conveniently view and manage your Market Snapshot® reports and listing alerts, so you can easily showcase your expertise and stay top of mind by providing consumers with hyperlocal, hard-to-find info on the real estate market.

**Viewing Snapshots in the Snapshots Dashboard  
**  
Click on **Marketing > Market Snapshots** in the main menu to open the _Snapshots_ dashboard. Here you’ll find a comprehensive list of all snapshot reports sent.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/ViewMSLA/View%20MSLA%201.png)

 Use the _Sent between_ and _Report status_ filters to find the snapshots you desire. Click **Stop** to stop a snapshot report, **Start** to start a stopped report, or **Edit** (migrated boards only) to edit the report settings.

Click on a snapshot report to see full details, including search criteria, next scheduled date, and report statistics.

Report statistics include:

*   Email Opens – # of times the email was opened.
*   Email Clicks – # of times the link in the email was clicked.
*   Email Inquiries – # of times the customer emailed you from the report.
*   Report Views – # of times the Market Snapshot report was viewed.

**Viewing Listing Alerts in the Snapshots Dashboard**

Click the **Listing Alerts** tab in the Snapshots dashboard to view listing alerts that have had at least one customer interaction.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/ViewMSLA/View%20MSLA%202.png)

 **Note:** If you don’t see any listing alerts here, please check that listing alerts are enabled under **Settings > Market Snapshot.**

Use the _Sent between_ filter to find listing alerts in a certain date range. Click on a listing alert to see full details, including property address, description, and alert statistics

Alert statistics include:

*   Email Opens – # of times the email was opened.
*   Email Clicks – # of times the link in the email was clicked.
*   Email Inquiries – # of times the customer emailed you from the listing alert.
*   Page Loads – # of times the listing alert page was load.

**Viewing Snapshots in a Contact Record**

Open a contact record and click the **Snapshots** tab to see a list of snapshot reports you’ve sent to the client.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/ViewMSLA/View%20MSLA%203.png)

 Click **Stop** to stop a snapshot report, or **Start** to start a stopped report.

**Viewing Listing Alerts in a Contact Record**

Open a contact record and go to **Snapshots > Listing Alerts** to see a list of listing alerts you’ve sent to the client.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/ViewMSLA/View%20MSLA%204.png)
