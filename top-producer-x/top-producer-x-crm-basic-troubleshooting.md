# Top Producer X CRM Basic Troubleshooting

Here are some basic troubleshooting steps you can try to help resolve issues in Top Producer X CRM.

*   Log out of Top Producer X, log back in, try steps again.
*   Close browser, reopen browser then try steps again.
*   Clear browser cache, temporary files, try steps again.  The following links will step you through how to clear cache/temporary files for each browser:
    
    Chrome: [https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop)  
    Edge: [https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/edge/learning-center/how-to-manage-and-clear-your-cache-and-cookies](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/edge/learning-center/how-to-manage-and-clear-your-cache-and-cookies)  
    Firefox:  [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)  
    Safari: [https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)
    
*   Restart PC, desktop, laptop, or Mac system.  Log into Top Producer X at [https://crm.topproducer.com/](https://crm.topproducer.com/), then re-try steps.
*   For login issues, make sure you are logging in at [https://crm.topproducer.com/](https://crm.topproducer.com/)  This is  the only login page for Top Producer X CRM.
*   If Top Producer X is logged in on multiple devices/systems, log out of all instances, then log back in.
*   Log out of Top Producer X, then open an Incognito (Chrome)/InPrivate (Edge)/Private window (Firefox).  Log into Top Producer X at [https://crm.topproducer.com/](https://crm.topproducer.com/).  Re-try steps.

If none of these steps resolve the issue, please contact support by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner of your Top Producer X CRM, or email [support@topproducer.com](mailto:support@topproducer.com)
