
# Top Producer X CRM Mobile: Add a Shortcut to your Home Screen

**Top Producer X CRM Mobile: Add a Shortcut to your Home Screen**

**How do I add TPX to my mobile phone?**

  
You can easily add an icon for Top Producer Mobile on your device’s Home screen. This will give you quick access to Top Producer Mobile with one touch!

1.  Using the browser on your device, go to [https://crm.topproducer.com](https://crm.topproducer.com/) and log in.
2.  When you are logged in to Top Producer Mobile, follow these steps:
3.  **iPhone (Safari)**: Tap ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPshare.png) > then tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4.  **iPhone (Chrome – bookmark only)**: Tap ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPellipsis.png) > then tap . (This adds a bookmark to your Bookmarks)
5.  **Android (Chrome browser)**: Tap ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPellipsis.png) > then tap **Add to Home screen**

  
**Tip for iPhone Users  
**  
It’s easy to save your password in Safari! After entering your password on the login screen, tap **Passwords**.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPiphone.png)

Then tap **Save Password**.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPiphone1.png)

If you have any questions or are unable to add TPX to your mobile device, please go to [https://support.topproducer.com/](https://support.topproducer.com/) and click the 'Chat Now' located in the middle and top right corner, by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner, or email support@topproducer.com.
