
# We’re having a hard time verifying that you aren’t a robot error on login

**We’re having a hard time verifying that you aren’t a robot error on login.**

If you are logging into Top Producer X CRM and see the following error message, the steps below will help get you logged in.

![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/Robot%20error/tpx-roboterror.jpg)

1.  Log into Top Producer 8i at [https://www.topproducer8i.com/](https://www.topproducer8i.com/) or if you have the Top Producer X login page open, click the **Top Producer 8i** button at the top of the screen to open the 8i login page.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/Robot%20error/tpx-roboterror1.jpg)
    
2.  Once you are logged in to Top Producer 8i, click the **TPX** button from the main menu, this will open Top Producer X in a new tab/window.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPX%20noniterable%20instance/noniterable%20instance%202.jpg)
    
3.  Once you are in Top Producer X, hover over your agent photo in the upper right corner and click **Sign Out**.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/Robot%20error/tpx-roboterror2.jpg)
    
4.  Log back into Top Producer X.

If after following these steps you are still seeing the ‘We’re having a hard time verifying that you aren’t a robot’ error message when logging in to Top Producer X CRM, please go to [https://support.topproducer.com/](https://support.topproducer.com/) and click the 'Chat Now' located in the middle and top right corner, by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner, or email support@topproducer.com.
