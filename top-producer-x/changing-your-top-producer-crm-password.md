# Changing Your Top Producer CRM Password

To reset your Top Producer® CRM password, follow these steps:

1.  Go to [https://crm.topproducer.com](https://crm.topproducer.com/). If logged in, click **Sign Out** in the top right.   
    **Note:** If you do not sign out of Top Producer X CRM, you will not be able to reach Step 6 as the **RESET PASSWORD** link from Step 5 will simply open your Top Producer X which is currently signed into.  If you are signed into Top Producer X on multiple computers, make sure you are logged out of ALL instances.
2.  Click **Forgot Password**? on the login screen.
    
    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/TPforgotpw.png)
    
3.  Enter your username and click **Send Password Link**.
4.  A password reset email will be sent to your registered email address.
5.  Click **RESET PASSWORD** in the password reset email.
6.  Set your new password as prompted and click **Reset Password**.

**Note:** This process will reset both your Top Producer® X CRM and Top Producer® 8i passwords.

If you have any difficulty resetting your password, please contact Customer Care for further assistance by going to [https://support.topproducer.com/](https://support.topproducer.com/) and click the 'Chat Now' located in the middle and top right corner, by clicking the 'Chat Now' bubble located at the bottom right corner, or email [support@topproducer.com](mailto:support@topproducer.com).
