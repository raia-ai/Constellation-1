# Sending a Market Snapshot Report

Send Market Snapshot reports to your clients to keep them in the loop on what’s going on in the market. You’ll provide hard-to-find info on the real estate market, including new listing and sold data. First, log in to [Top Producer® X CRM](https://crm.topproducer.com/).

1.  Click the **Compose** button and select Send Snapshot, or click the **Send Snapshot** button in the _Snapshots_ dashboard or the _Snapshot Reports_ tab of a contact record.

    ![](https://na3.files.teamsupport.com/TSData/WikiDocs/2472652/images/TopProducer%20-%20Wiki%20KB%20images/MS-Send%20MS/Picture1.png)
2. Enter _Contact details, Address, Buyer preferences_, and choose the _Snapshot settings_. \
   &#xNAN;_\*\*_\
   &#xNAN;_&#x4F;ptional:\*\* For more detailed options (i.e. Half Bathrooms), click **Advanced**._
3. Click **Send Market Snapshot**.
