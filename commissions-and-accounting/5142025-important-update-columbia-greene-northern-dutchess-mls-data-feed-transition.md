# 5/14/2025 - Important Update: Columbia Greene Northern Dutchess MLS Data Feed Transition

We are writing to inform you that the **Columbia Greene Northern Dutchess MLS** data feed (**OSN ColumbiaGreene**) has been dissolved. According to our recent updates, the data previously available in this feed is now accessible through the **Hudson Valley Catskills Region Multiple List Service (Ulster County Board of REALTORS)** data feed (**OSN** **UlsterCounty**).

As part of this transition, the **ColumbiaGreene** data feed will be decommissioned on **Friday, May 23rd, 2025**.
