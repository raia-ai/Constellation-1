# Commissions Online Help and Product Tour

The purpose of this article is to highlight the Help and Product Tour functionality within the application. 

The Help icon is located at the top right corner of the application. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e8b9792e-92d2-4e23-9cc7-e21921d7aeae)

When selected, the Help menu will open and provide a summary of the module including easily accessible links to the relevant Knowledge Base articles and videos.   

To navigate to a different module, select Next or Previous and to close the Help select the "x" at the top right of Help.

Please note, some modules have a scroll bar, so you may need to scroll to see the Related Knowledge Base Articles links.
