# Accounting - Reversing Posted Unpaid Bills

The purpose of the article is to show how to reverse a posted unpaid bill.

To reverse a posted unpaid bill, navigate to **AP** \> **Processing** \> **Bills**.

*   Find the unpaid posted bill you would like to delete
*   Click **Edit**
*   Click the **Reverse Bill** button

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d3357de3-a5f6-4c11-9efa-7488ede53890)

Once you click the **Reverse** **Bill** button you will receive the following pop-up.  The pop-up informs you that the credit created to apply to the unpaid bill will be dated with the current processing date.  If you do not want the credit to be created with the current processing date, then click **Cancel** and change the [**Processing Date**](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/16221922 "Accounting - How to Change the Processing Date") before stepping through the above process again.  

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/51fe6fad-a293-4ec4-9b95-f355d3610707)

If you have the **Processing Date** set to what you want, click **OK**.  You will then receive the following pop-up.  Click **OK** to close the pop-up.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/056289a2-84f0-437f-a4a2-49473025a06c)
