# Accounting - Correcting Posted Manual Bills with Incorrect Vendor Class

This article outlines the steps that need to be taken when a manual bill has been posted to the wrong vendor class.&#x20;

When a manual bill has been posted to the wrong vendor **Class**, navigate to **AP > Processing > Bills** then click on the **Manual** radio button. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1534953a-ee71-4b0a-a8bf-b2312f6028e0)

* Find the bill in the left pane
* Highlight the bill

In the example below, the manual bill was posted to vendor **Class** 11115 in error; it was supposed to have been posted to vendor **Class** 11111:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3fc5d33a-6c71-44f5-bef3-29daa2298311)

* Click **Copy**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/7d1e4e06-9f65-4a0d-9180-9e51187f0181)

When you click **Copy**, you will receive the following pop-up:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/371779a4-2342-448c-a6cc-cc17aea28ced)

* Click **Yes** to the pop-up
* Change both **Amount** fields, in the copy, to negative values
* **Save**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5f11e44e-765e-4116-92f5-ca0c589bc254)

* Enter a new manual bill referencing the correct vendor **Class** 11111:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/461b3356-b12e-4e94-8d3f-796ddcc2bf90)
