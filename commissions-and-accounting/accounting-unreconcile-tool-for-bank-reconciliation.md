# Accounting - Unreconcile Tool for Bank Reconciliation

The purpose of this article is to provide the steps needed to unreconcile your last bank reconciliation. &#x20;

To unreconcile your last bank reconciliation, first close the **Bank Reconciliation** tab, then navigate to **Bank > Processing > Unreconcile**.

* Select cash **Account** from the dropdown
* Click **Unreconcile**
* Click **Yes** to **Unreconcile** pop-up
* Click **OK** to **Unreconcile** pop-up
