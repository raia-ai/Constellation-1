# Non-Sufficient Funds Escrow/Trust Deposits | Constellation1 Customer Hub

This article provides the steps when a Deposit is returned by the financial institution due to non-sufficient funds.

The best way to deal with Trust/Escrow deposit check/cheque that does not clear your Real Estate Trust/Escrow bank account, is to locate the specified transaction, select Edit and navigate to the Deposit tab.

*   Select the Add Deposit icon, select new and from the Action drop down select, NSF - Non-Sufficient Funds.
*   Complete the rest of the data entry as required, ensuring the date you enter here is the date that the check/cheque was returned by your financial institution.
*   When posted to Accounting, journal entries will credit the Real Estate Trust/Escrow bank and debit the Real Estate Trust/Escrow liability.
