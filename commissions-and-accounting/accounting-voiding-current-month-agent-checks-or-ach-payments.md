# Accounting - Voiding Current Month Agent Checks or ACH Payments

The purpose of this article is to provide steps for voiding an agent check and/or ACH payment, that was issued in the current month. 

The process for voiding an agent check and/or ACH payment will vary depending on whether the sale transaction is posted or unposted.  Instructions for both scenarios are listed below:    

**Sale Unposted:**

*   Navigate to **Sales > Processing > Void Commission Payments**.
*   Highlight the check in the left pane.
*   Click the **Void** button.
*   Read the pop-up.
    *   Note that it states that an off-setting agent record will be created in the sales(s) involved. 
        *   If the check included more than one closing, then an off-setting agent record will be created in multiple sale transactions.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2350f76b-0cb3-433f-8a37-42876f3e8fa9)

*   Click **Yes** on the pop-up.
*   Click **OK** on the pop-up shown below.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b94fd6ba-2439-4468-8a42-49acc10d6665)

*   Close the **Void Commission Payments** tab.
    *   If you do not close the **Void Commission Payment** tab, you will not be able to make edits to sale transactions.

To reissue the check, navigate to **Sales > Processing > Sale Transactions** and follow the steps below: 

*   Navigate to the **Closed** view.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0253afdd-08a2-42b2-96ad-8b8cb0b06a7a)

*   Highlight the original sale transaction in the left pane.
*   Click **Edit**. 
*   Navigate to the **Commission** tab.
*   Click the green plus sign, to add a new commission record for the agent.
*   Select the agent from the pop-up.
*   Click **OK**.  
*   Ensure that all values are correct.
*   Click **Save**. 
*   Click **Actions** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/dad14546-6b05-41dc-81d3-8c78134022fb) 

*   Select **Issue Payments**.  
    *   Alternatively, navigate to **Sales > Processing > Issue Commission Payments**.
*   Step through the payment process.

**Sale Posted:** 

*   Navigate to **Sales > Processing > Void Commission Payments**.
*   Highlight the check in the left pane.
*   Click the **Void** button.
*   Read the pop-up.
    *   Note that it states a **NEW** off-setting sales transaction will be created. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2958fb5b-25fd-4f8a-b424-4b226d79b588)

*   Click **Yes** on the pop-up.
*   Click **OK** on the pop-up shown below.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e9b5f0e2-5330-49c2-b2e8-2322a2aa29ef)

*   Close the **Void Commission Payments** tab.
    *   If you do not close the **Void Commission Payment** tab, you will not be able to make edits to sale transactions.

To reissue the check, navigate to **Sales > Processing > Sale Transactions** and follow the steps below: 

*   Navigate to the **Closed** view.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0253afdd-08a2-42b2-96ad-8b8cb0b06a7a)

*   Highlight the **_new_** sale transaction in the left pane. 
*   Click **Edit**. 
*   Navigate to the **Commission** tab.
*   Click the green plus sign, to add a new commission record for the agent.
*   Select the agent from the pop-up.
*   Click **OK**.  
*   Ensure that all values are correct.
*   Click **Save**. 
*   Click **Actions** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/dad14546-6b05-41dc-81d3-8c78134022fb)

*   Select **Issue Payments**.  
    *   Alternatively, navigate to **Sales > Processing > Issue Commission Payments**.
*   Step through the payment process.
