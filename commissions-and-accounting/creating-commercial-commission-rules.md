# Creating Commercial Commission Rules | Constellation1 Customer Hub

This article outlines a description of the commercial calculations as well as how to set one up.

*   **Creating Commercial Commission Rules**
*   Select Commission Rules > Commercial from the Set up menu and select New
*   Enter the necessary data as described below:
*   **Comm Rule** The unique label you assign this commission rule. (ex. Builder)
*   **Description** A brief description of the commercial commission rule
*   **Rent/SqFt.** Enter the dollar amount for the first, second, etc. year
*   **Net Rent Commission** Enter the percentage of the commission earned on the first, second, etc. year
*   **Selling Net Rent Commission** Enter the Selling percentage of the commission earned on the first, second, etc. year
*   **Selling Flat Commission** Enter the Selling Flat Fee of the commission earned on the first, second, etc. year
*   **Buying Net Commission** Enter the Buying percentage of the commission earned on the first, second, etc. year
*   **Buying Flat Commission** Enter the Buying Flat Fee of the commission earned on the first, second, etc. year
*   **Number of Months** Enter the dollar amount for any part of a year, applicable percentage and the number of months
*   **Under the Additional Configurations node**
*   **Click the box to enable Other Income Shared** if applicable
*   **Use % and/ or Fee allocated to Buying and Selling per year** if applicable
*   **Selling Total Commission Split Percentage** Enter the percentage of commissions owed to the Selling Broker, if applicable
*   **Buying Total Commission Split Percentage** Enter the percentage of commissions owed to the Buying Broker, if applicable
*   **Selling Total Plus Additional (Shared) Amount** Enter a flat fee dollar amount (can be positive or negative) for each side, it can be used in combination with a percentage calculation or used on its own as a flat commission amount. Enter the amount only without the $ sign.
*   **Buying Total Plus Additional (Shared)** **Amount** Enter a flat fee dollar amount (can be positive or negative) for each side, it can be used in combination with a percentage calculation or used on its own as a flat commission amount. Enter the amount only without the $ sign.
*   **Unshared Income Name** if applicable
*   **MLS /Transaction Fee Rule** Click the Look up button to select the MLS/Transaction Fee rule, if applicable, to be used for this commercial commission rule.
*   Select Save
*   **Editing/Deleting a Commercial Commission Rule**
*   Select Commission Rules > Commercial from the Set Up menu
*   Highlight the commercial commission rule you wish to change in the database box and double click on it
*   Select Edit and Make the necessary changes
*   Select Save
