# Cancelling a Partial Payment Transaction

The purpose of this article is to provide the steps to cancel a payment attached to an existing partial payment transaction.

To do this, navigate to Transactions in the left navigation bar and search for the transaction in question. 

From within the transaction ensure you are on the tab of correct payment. In the screen shot below, Payment 1 is the one we want to cancel.

Select Action = Cancel 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/97591e1b-0fcc-4615-946e-87ba4c01f8ad.png)

You will receive a prompt to ensure you want the cancel this transaction, select Confirm to proceed OR Cancel if this is not the correct payment and/or transaction. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a41b64ed-ab41-4c89-aacb-58eb46f2198b)

Once the payment has been cancelled, the commission amounts can now be reattached to a new payment OR reallocated to an existing payment for the same transaction.
