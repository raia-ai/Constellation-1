# Accounting - Entering AP Credits

The purpose of this article is to show how to enter a vendor credit and apply to an unpaid bill.

To add a vendor credit, navigate to **Accounts Payable > Processing > AP Credits/Payments**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/475fa25c-6b91-405e-8270-2683b44056fe)

* Click **Add** button
* Click green plus sign to filter for **Vendor**
* Select the vendor and click **OK**
* Enter **Date** you would like the credit to accrue
  * It is advised that you do not backdate the credit, doing so will change historical financial data
* Leave **Check#** field as is
  * **Check#** will auto-fill upon save
* Enter **Amount** of credit
* Enter meaningful **Comment**&#x20;
* In the **Credit Lines** section, click the green plus sign to add line item(s)
* Enter the **Amount, Account** and **Office.**&#x20;
  * **Project** and **Comment** fields are optional&#x20;
* Click **Apply Payment**&#x20;
* Click **Auto-Apply** or manually apply the credit to applicable bill(s)
* Click **OK** and **Save**
