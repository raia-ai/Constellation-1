# Accounting - Applying Agent Credits and Payments

The purpose of this article is to provide steps for applying agent credits and payments.

To apply agent credits and payments, navigate to **Agents > Processing > Agent Credits/Payments**.

* Click on the **Unapplied** column header twice to gather all values greater than zero to the top of the column.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/cfcef13c-e77d-4a12-b007-c30d825a60a3)

* Highlight the first record in the list and click **Edit**.
* Click **Apply Credit/Payment** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f0632b16-765d-401b-a391-ecef4f988629)

* Click **Auto-Apply** to apply the payment to the oldest agent charges or pick which agent charges you would like to apply the payment to, by manually typing in the payment for preferred charges.&#x20;
* Click **OK** then **Save.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20158008)
