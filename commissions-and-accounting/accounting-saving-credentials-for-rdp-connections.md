# Accounting - Saving Credentials for RDP Connections

The purpose of this article is to provide steps for saving RDP credentials again.  

If you find that your RDP credentials are no longer saved, which can happen after a Windows update or if you installed the RDP shortcut on a new computer, follow the steps below, so that the "Allow me to save credentials" and/or "Remember Me" check boxes are presented to you again.

*   Right-click on your current RDP shortcut.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20e66ff1-126e-40d6-8b6e-159c34fb9db0)

*   Click **Edit** on the **Connect** pop-up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2f0f800e-edc7-41ca-a898-f658fa50f509)

Note: On some computers, you may have to first click **Show more options** before you see the **Edit** option.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/48390580-720c-4dbb-b9c7-e1c65d9d2617)

*   Click **delete** on the **General** tab.

For security purposes, the **Computer** name and the **User name** have been removed from the screenshot below.  These values should still be visible on the **General** tab of your **Remote Desktop Connection**.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ccf5d275-dce2-4ccd-a3ca-1d75d0487ad6)

*   Click **Yes** on the following pop-up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6b23d19b-3918-4641-8d07-76b338b943a3)

*   Navigate to the **Advanced** tab.
*   Click the **Settings...** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9ec91b12-2c4f-4bbf-b614-e40716b9f4b2)

*   Click **delete** on the **Remote Desktop Connection** pop-up.

For security purposes, the **Server name** and the **User name** have been removed from the screenshot below.  These values should still be visible to you in the **Remote Desktop Connection** pop-up.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fe58a7f8-a218-40dc-9aff-9082b26fa5e1)

*   Click **Yes** on the following pop-up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1e8766e1-a53b-421c-a0a5-362d800b25b0)

*   Click **OK** on the **Remote Desktop Connection** pop-up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/180b0437-e847-40de-ad5d-f167a8b9590a)

*   Click the **Connect** button on the **Remote Desktop Connection** pop-up.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2be5e52e-7e3b-4e5f-abbb-46e2c38eee04)

*   Enter your **User name**.
*   Enter your **Password**.
*   Check the **Remember** me box.  
*   Click **OK**. 

For security purposes the server name has been removed from the screenshot below.

After you click **OK**, you will receive the following **Windows Security** pop-up. 

For security purposes the server name and user name have been removed from the screenshot.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3bc26ec7-9ce4-47e8-84a4-54d0ae1f331f)

*   Enter your **Password**.
*   Click **Remember me**.
*   Click **OK**.

After you click **OK**, you will receive the following **Windows Security** pop-up.  However, this time, the pop-up will include the server name and your user name.  For security purposes, the server name and your user name have been removed from the screenshot below.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d6e356ab-fe4e-40ba-b839-366b972a857b)

*   Enter your **Password**.
*   Click **Remember** me.
*   Click **OK**.

After clicking **OK**, your hosted desktop should load.  Close your hosted session, then click on your RDP shortcut to make sure you connect to your hosted session without having to enter your password.
