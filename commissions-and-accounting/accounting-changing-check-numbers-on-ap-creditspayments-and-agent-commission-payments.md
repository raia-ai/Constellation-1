
# Accounting - Changing Check Numbers on AP Credits/Payments and Agent Commission Payments

The purpose of this article is to show how to change the check number for accounts payable and agent commission checks. 

You can change check numbers as long as they have not been reconciled or posted, however, you will not be able to edit the ACH box once you have stepped through the issue payment process.  To change an AP check number, navigate to **AP > Processing > AP Credits/Payments**. 

*   Filter for the payment record
*   Click **Edit**
*   Change the **Check#**
*   **Save**   

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/735f9b46-6a5e-4082-ac52-654610da6082)

To change an agent's commission payment check number, navigate to **Sales > Processing > Sale Transactions**. 

*   Filter for the sale
*   Click **Edit**
*   Select the **Commission** tab
*   Scroll down to the **Agent Payment Details** section
*   Change the **Check Number**
*   **Save**
