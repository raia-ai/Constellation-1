# Accounting - How to apply Accounts Payable Credits and Payments

The purpose of this article is to show how to apply accounts payable payments and credits.

To apply accounts payable payments and credits, navigate to **Accounts Payable > Processing > AP Credits/Payments**.

* Click on the **Unapplied** column header twice to gather all values greater than zero at the top of the column.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20155961)

* Highlight the first record and click **Edit**.
* Click **Apply Payment.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20155746)

* Click **Auto-Apply** to apply the payment to the oldest bills or pick which bills you would like to apply the payment to, by manual typing in the payment for preferred bills.&#x20;
* Click **OK** then **Save.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20155747)

* Click on the **Credits** radio button to apply credits, repeating the process above.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20155748)
