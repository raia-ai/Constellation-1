# Generating Letters to Selling or Buying Brokerage

You need to print or email a statement to the Buying or Selling brokerage, here are the steps.

*   Navigate to Transaction > Commission tab 
*   Do not EDIT the Transaction 
*   Select the SELLING & BUYING COMMISSIONS icon to the right of Gross Commission 
*   Within the Transaction Commission Details window, you will select the WORD icon to the right of the displayed default letter template.
