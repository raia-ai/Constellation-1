# Conditions Tracking | Constellation1 Customer Hub

Commissions Online enables user to track conditions, or contingencies on transactions.  

**To engage Conditions within the program:**

*   From the left navigation bar, navigate to System Settings / Advanced Settings / Transaction Settings and ensure Enable Conditions Tracking is toggled on.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/8380a715-18e5-4150-a79b-38a1ab001602)

**Adding/editing conditions list**

Once Conditions is toggled on, a Conditions list appears on the System Settings / Advanced Settings / Field settings card.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3ddeb556-457c-47e8-b066-f429d4a02bda)

To add a new Condition, select the "+" button and a free form field will appear, add the name of your new Condition.

**Adding conditions to a Transaction**

When Conditions is enabled, user will notice two new fields appear on the Transaction Details tab of your transactions, **Conditions Status** and **Firm Date.**

*   Should a condition be in a status of **NOT MET**, the Conditions status will be noted as **Not Firm** and will be unable to close the transaction. 
*   Only when **ALL** conditions have been **MET**, will the status change to **FIRM** and the Firm Date be pre-filled with the latest condition met.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/703b428c-0748-4904-804b-0be255485b9d)

Once Conditions is toggled on, the Conditions card will appear on the Transaction Details tab.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5c4118f2-e761-42f8-8350-9fe113fa0aed)

In order to add a condition to a transaction, select **Add Condition**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2ec06986-aee2-455c-b006-37fdf0cff20c)

For each condition, select the condition from the Name drop down field, assign an expiration date and if the condition has been met or not, by selecting a status of **Met** or **Not Met** under Status and update the Met Date field if it has been met. Add any notes or description if needed.

*   The condition will now appear in the list under the Conditions tab.
*   Conditions will be visible on the My Workspace tab under Conditions Due.
*   To edit, or delete a condition, select the ellipsis on the far right hand side of the condition, and select **Edit** or **Delete** accordingly. 
*   **Delete** will the delete the condition entirely and **Edit** will display the following to update the status, expiration date and met date of the condition.  
*   Selecting **Save** and update your Firm date on the Property Card of the transaction details tab if applicable.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1ea8f976-6ef9-4ece-aebb-c61e19991f19)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c94bd5c7-99a3-4e55-ac47-64e1a8e45cef)
