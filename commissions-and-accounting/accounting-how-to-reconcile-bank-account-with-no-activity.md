# Accounting - How to Reconcile Bank Account with no Activity

The purpose of this article is to provide a way to reconcile a cash account that does not have any monthly activity.  

For a cash account that has had no activity during the month, there is no need to reconcile the bank statement; you can wait until the next month with activity to reconcile.

If you want to reconcile each and every month, regardless of whether or not there is activity, you can do the following:

*   Enter a journal entry for $0.01
    *   **Debit** and **Credit** the cash account $0.01

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b03e7c02-bb93-40f4-b980-80deec44cd66.png)

*   Retrieve the journal entry into bank rec
*   Clear the debit and credit, since they net to $0.00

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1dffcf7a-3bed-45e5-a016-c607dcf98e53.png)

*   Enter **New Bank Balance**
*   Enter **"as of"** date

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/04deb4ba-5782-4ff9-985a-b60ff554b874.png)

*   Click **Worksheet** button

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/70b5b3c6-edc3-49c4-9073-7705497bb0de.png)

*   Confirm **Bank Recon to GL Balance Difference** is $0.00

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/9e9ce432-4c22-465f-93d9-a15e324c8d40.png)

*   Close **Bank Reconciliation Worksheet**
*   Click **Reconcile**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/04b5e1c6-d2a5-46d4-8b46-82e5c6ade03f.png)
