# Accounting - Correcting Batched Deposits

The purpose of this article is to detail the steps to correct batched deposits.

* Corrections to batched deposits cannot be made if the batch has already been retrieved into the bank reconciliation.
* To remove the batch from the bank rec, put check mark in the **Selected** column to the left of the **Batch** line item, then click **Remove Selected**.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/725.png)

* Then navigate to **Bank > Processing > Batch Deposits**
* Select the **Cash Acct.** from the dropdown list.
* Find the **Rec#** for the batch, highlight it, and click **Delete**.
* Click **Yes** to the **Delete Batch Deposit** confirmation pop-up.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/726.png)

* Click **Add** if you would like to create a new batch.&#x20;
