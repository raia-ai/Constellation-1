### Viewing Market Snapshot Reports and Listing Alerts

Top Producer® X CRM allows you to conveniently view and manage your Market Snapshot® reports and listing alerts, so you can easily showcase your expertise and stay top of mind by providing consumers with hyperlocal, hard-to-find info on the real estate market.

Send Market Snapshot reports to your clients to keep them in the loop on what’s going on in the market. You’ll provide hard-to-find info on the real estate market, including new listing and sold data. First, log in to [Top Producer® X CRM](https://crm.topproducer.com/).

### Email Integration - Needs reauthorization
