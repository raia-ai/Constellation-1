
# Completely Removing a Contact Type from Top Producer 8i

**The following steps will take you through how to completely remove a Contact Type from Top Producer. Note that these steps currently need to be followed in Top Producer 8i.**

The first step is to remove the Contact Type from any contacts that have that Type applied to their contact records.

1\. Hover over Contacts menu and click **Search for Contacts**.  
2\. Click the **Select from List** link beside the empty Contact Type box.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2c3930e1-8943-4014-a068-a3022b3238a0)

3\. Click the box beside the Contact Type you want to remove and then click the Done button. The Contact Type **“Foreclosure”** will be removed in this example:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a0db2193-cf03-432c-9cf0-f6c9f4c2aaa2)

4\. Click the **Search** button find any contacts using that Contact Type.  
5\. Check the top box on the left side to select all the contacts in the list and then click the **Mass Update** option below.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c1b21c8f-c244-4406-ac03-96eb909e08bf)

6\. Click the **Remove** field to the right of the Contact Type option along the top, find and check the box beside the Contact Type you want to remove and then click the Done button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/588e3342-8b2b-4ab1-9e52-68595cdc15c8)

7\. Click the **Update Contacts** button below and then click Yes on the popup message.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fb4a99b7-897d-4848-97ff-9cf9259ba537)

**Removing a Contact Type from the “Master” list**

  
The second step is to remove the Contact Type from the main, or “master”, list.

1\. Hover over the Contacts menu and click **Add Contact**.  
2\. Click the **Select from/Modify List** link beside the Contact Type field.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c8d50807-41d1-4708-bc06-58406d809316)

3\. Click the **trash can** icon to the right of the Contact Type you want to delete and click Yes on the popup warning.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9a854635-e4b3-4950-8010-87280d1eff1f)

Click the **Cancel** button below to close the Add Contact screen when you’ve removed the Contact Type(s).
