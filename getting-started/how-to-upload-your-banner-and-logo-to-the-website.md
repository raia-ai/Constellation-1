# How to Upload Your Banner and Logo to the Website

Our Nestfully websites can be enhanced with a custom banner and logo! They'll allow you to personalize your pages with the information you desire.

  
**Note:** The option to change the logo may not be available for some companies.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f5ba7c21-8e87-483c-9000-0551604a2660)

To upload or select a new banner, click on the banner image in the left navigation. This will open the banner image library.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/38826bb7-75f0-4246-82aa-42554ca8c654)

In the pop-up window, click on the banner image you would like to use on the website or upload a custom banner.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fc9d06d9-116a-4da3-b24d-a28031aad8f1)

  
To add a logo to your website, click on the “logo” image on the left nav. Follow the same steps as you would for the banner images.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/26254d35-aa6c-47f9-a77e-e4463a10910d)

If you require any assistance with your website configuration and setup, our team is always here to help! You can reach us by email at [support@nestfully.com](mailto:support@nestfully.com).
