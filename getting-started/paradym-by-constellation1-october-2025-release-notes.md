### üè¢ Office Accounts

Office Accounts introduce a new way to manage Paradym at the office level. Each office now has its own login and account, giving brokers the flexibility to delegate local office management while still retaining full oversight. This new layer (Broker ‚Üí Office ‚Üí Agent) adds control, increases local visibility, and expands social exposure for agents‚Äô listings and content.

**Automatic Creation of Office Accounts**  
When a broker establishes and configures a new office within their account, the system automatically generates an Office Account. Existing Office Accounts are already in place for all current offices associated with the broker's account. Each time a new office is created, a corresponding Office Account is generated automatically. Brokers have the option to designate an office manager and provide login credentials either at that moment or at a later time.

#### What Brokers Can Do

*   **Send Credentials**: Invite an office manager with one click by sending their unique login credentials.
*   **Impersonate Accounts**: Log in as an office to view or manage settings directly, just like you can with agents.
*   **Override Controls**: Retain full control over branding, logos, preview videos, flyers, and disclaimers where needed.
*   **View Office Metrics**: Access dashboards with office-specific reporting and insights.

#### Quick FAQ

**Q: Do I need to set up Office Accounts myself?**  
No. Office Accounts have already been created for you ‚Äì all you need to do is send credentials to office managers.

**Q: How many Office Accounts do I have?**  
We‚Äôve automatically created a separate Office Account for each of your existing offices.

**Q: If I add a new office, will that create a new Office Account automatically?**  
Yes. Whenever you create a new office, a new account is automatically generated. You can then assign an office manager and send them login credentials.

**Q: Can brokers still control everything if needed?**  
Yes. Brokers retain override capabilities and can impersonate any Office Account.

**Q: Will agents notice a change?**  
Agents continue to work as before, but now benefit from increased exposure: agent tours and posts appear on their own channels, the office‚Äôs channels, and the broker‚Äôs channels.

**Q: Will this change how agents post?**  
No. Agents continue posting as usual. If an office broadcasts content with review enabled, agents may see a notification before posting.

**Q: Can one agent also be an office manager?**  
Yes, but they will receive **separate login credentials** for the Office Account.

**Q: Do office managers have access to broker-only settings (like billing or security)?**  
No. Office Accounts only include office-level dashboards, tours, and settings. Broker-level features remain with the broker.

**Q: How does branding override work?**  
Brokers always have top-level control. If a broker sets branding (such as a logo) and chooses to override, that setting applies to all offices and agents.

*   If the broker does **not** override, each office can set its own branding.
*   If the office sets branding and overrides, it applies to all agents in that office.
*   Agents can only control branding if neither the broker nor the office has overridden it.

This same Broker ‚Üí Office ‚Üí Agent override hierarchy also applies to preview video scene templates.
