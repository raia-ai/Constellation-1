
# How to Add an Email Activity to Your Action Plan

To add activities to the new Action Plan, click **Add Email Activity.**

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/8c862374-9c85-4a65-bcbb-f09e0967af8a)**

To create a new activity, select **Create a new email activity from scratch** from the dropdown list.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2aaa5df2-e783-499b-88e0-856a92ab2fba)

The **Create New Email Activity** overlay will display.

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b52d2d68-9f48-4f2c-8575-dd7dc99d4b64)**

Enter **Email Activity Title** (required) and the **Email Subject Line** (required).

**NOTE**: If you wish to have your company logo and photo displayed at the top of each activity, select the Display logo and my photo at the top of each email activity.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/de5b56fb-2058-4ec4-b555-6e543c4e115d)

**NOTE:** If this setting is checked, all the activities for that plan will include your logo and photo. Enter your content in the design box.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2666a765-bad6-461d-baf4-cc9c0fb67835)

Click the **Tokens** dropdown to pull in dynamic fields.

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/83df1697-2b71-4f24-9bde-955ba43decd3)**

Click **Save** to save the activity and add it to your plan. The new activity will also be added to the Manage Email Activities list.
