# Accounting - Completing a Bank Reconciliation (With Video)

The purpose of this article is to provide instructions on how to complete a bank reconciliation. 

Before beginning your bank reconciliation, go to **GL**\>>**Processing**\>>**Post the General Ledger**

*   Enter Ending Date equal to the last day of the month you are about to reconcile
*   Click **Show Exceptions** button

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0aa78e09-b3a0-466b-87f8-5e2d6c228fa0)

If there are exceptions, they will be noted in a grid at the bottom of the screen and you will need to fix the exceptions before proceeding with the bank reconciliation. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/78333fa9-9b15-4941-83f1-93efd82086dc)

If there are no exceptions you will receive the following message: 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/8b74d275-c8ac-4faa-81b9-321a338e0ed0)

If no exceptions are detected, navigate to **Bank**\>>**Processing**\>>**Bank Reconciliation**

*   Select the appropriate Cash Account from the dropdown list.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2fb89def-47be-4999-8725-2df4d775b4e6) 

*   Enter the **Retrieve Through** date that corresponds to the ending date of your current bank statement. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/8c98b2ec-ed8d-4458-a888-2f7de3eaefd4)

*   Click **Retrieve New** button. 

This will bring in all transactions that affect the cash account since the last reconciliation, up to and including the date you enter. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/65404890-8436-413b-9658-9eadcf6eafd0)

The **Prior Reconciled Balance** and the **New Reconciled Balance** are the ending balance from your last bank statement. These fields are not editable. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e7ac5e05-fa77-480b-975d-d9b40bc8deb8)

*   Enter the **New Bank Balance** and ending date from the bank statement; in the **as of** field. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0a66a6d8-9104-4680-9da2-7357d46fcba6)

The first time a transaction is retrieved into the reconciliation, you will see a check mark in the **New?** column. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2db860e1-1384-4c7f-a2d7-27b9a2fb9599)

Practice sorting and filtering the data; it will make the reconciliation process easier.  To filter for all deposits, enter “>0” in the filter.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ae4afd7b-895f-4faf-96aa-fe97f94006cd)

To filter for all checks, enter “<0” in the filter. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e2d14aee-735c-4686-a178-29d2ef00a579)

You can sort each column by clicking on the column label; it will toggle between ascending and descending order. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5dc3d246-2e7d-4e72-9bd1-18f014e460c5)

*   Mark off the items that have cleared the bank by putting a check mark in the Cleared column. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6395a817-6ab0-4202-add7-1fe53837ec9a)

Ultimately, the **New Reconciled Balance** should equal the **New Bank Balance**; resulting with a $0.00 (zero) difference. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6e27eb17-9638-45a4-ada8-e3d6b94af100)

*   Click **Worksheet** button (see screen shot above)
*   Verify the **Bank Recon to GL Balance Difference** on the last page of the worksheet is $0.00.  Any differences should be researched and corrected before hitting the **RECONCILE** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e70cfed3-81dc-4853-b3a0-57204c227281)

*   When finished click **RECONCILE** button 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6ec3eaf4-a555-4d0b-b469-df43a1884368)
