# How to create a Template

Templates allow eSign users to automate the document mapping process. This guide will walk you though a Template creation process:

## Step 1: Navigate to the Templates Page

1. Log in to your eSign account.
2. Locate menu on the left and click on Templates.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f7794774-892d-4ddc-80d8-8d19c7e3f722)

## Step 2: Create a New Template

1. On the Templates page, click the blue Add button on the screen's top right corner.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/825ee483-f78b-48ba-905e-bd400162496f)

## Step 3: Enter Template Details and Upload Documents

1. Fill in the required fields:
   1. **Template draft:** Enter a name for your Template.
   2. **Description:** Add a short description of the Template.
   3. **Document Link:** In the Document Link field enter your document code. The system will access the document by the code number then apply the template. _**This field is designed for enterprise customers and is dependent on our API to connect to custom documents.**_ You can skip this step if you you do not have an eSign enterprise setup.
   4. Scroll down to the **Documents** section.
   5. Drag and drop your file into the upload area, or click **Upload** to select a file from your computer.
   6. Upload your document. There are three options available for adding a document to a Template:
      1. Drag and drop your documents to the documents section.
      2. Upload – From your computer or local drive, navigate to the document(s) then select them.
      3. Get from Cloud – Select from OneDrive, Dropbox or box, then navigate to the documents and select them.&#x20;
   7. The uploaded file will appear in the list below with its name, size, and the number of pages.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/73feb14f-9ab8-418f-adac-3c9bc31fda83.png)

## Step 4: Add and Assign Signer Roles

1. Scroll down to the Signer Roles section.
2. To Add Role click the Add button at the screen's right corner.
3. It will then give you the option to choose from a list, choose as per your preference
4. Add the Signer Roles: **Tip:** **Add the maximum number of Signer Roles that are appropriate for the document. It’s easier to remove unnecessary tags when creating a signing session. Click the +Add Role button and in the pop-up modal select the signer roles, and click the Select button.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1d5335a5-e855-48b7-ba10-2100316edea7.png)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/597465c9-28f5-4544-9181-3e55e34f6588.png)

## Step 5: Save the Template

1. Once you have completed all the required fields and uploaded the documents, click the blue **Save** button at the top right corner of the screen.
2. Your Template will now be saved and listed on the Templates page.

Congratulations! You have successfully added a new template in eSign. You can now use it for Sessions and Bulk Send.
