
# Set a Subject line with Template variables

*   To setup a default email “Subject” (with “Template Variables”), click on “Preferences” under your account name.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d76743d7-772c-4ba5-93b6-88bf94b3ebd2)

*   On the “Preferences” page, click on the “Change” option, displayed to the right of “Change Email Subject”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6f0b330c-8e27-4a32-bc25-980ed994a32e)

*   Type in the required text, drag and drop the “Template Variables” to the “Subject Line”, and click on “Save”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f72fee2e-cce7-4d00-b8e2-2b8f9edf5de9)

*   The new email “Subject” (including the predefined “Template Variables”) will be displayed on all emails, when sending them out to the recipients.
