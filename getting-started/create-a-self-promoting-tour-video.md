# Create a Self Promoting Tour Video

**Create a Self Promoting Tour Video**&#x20;

**Are you looking to create a tour video to promote yourself? Why the customer should choose you as their agent in assisting buying or selling your home? Want to show your customers what you are capable of? If any of those are you, continue reading to learn more!**&#x20;

**Getting Started**  \
Once you have your Paradym account set up and running, you can begin right away creating your promotional video!  \
**o** Go to your My Tours page from your drop-down menu.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/627bd6ec-f933-4661-8f4d-b36dbd93722f.png)

**o** Now, all you need to do is click the plus sign in the upper right-hand corner of your **My Tours** page. \
**o** You will then hit the Agent / Office Promotion template.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6c82eba0-47fe-4431-860c-2e3812830dfb.png)

**o** The Title can be anything! You can name it “Your Real Estate Agent” or if you have a slogan,  you can use that too! Remember, you can change the Title and Description at any time. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/13986ca2-cd16-40b1-9412-8d05c017b02f.png)

**o** Once you are finished, simply hit the **Next** button.  \
**o** Upload your photos or video clips here! You can add more later too!  \
**o** After you upload your photos, simply hit the **Next** button.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/5a2560bd-5295-4846-834e-00eb194de9e0.png)

**o** Now you will be on a page that looks like this.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/25cdc337-c34e-454f-9498-287ba8ef63ba.png)

**o** First thing that you want to do hit Change and choose to Manually Publish the tour.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/cc8e6e62-7908-4fb9-a626-f96b2bca12d0.png)

**o** And there you have it! You just created yourself a tour!  \
**o** You can always tweak the changes you made at any time! \
**o** The left hand column will have most of your settings.  \
While the five options to the right of each photo have the rest.&#x20;

**Q:** How do I even start? What should be at the beginning of the video? \
**A:** Any way that you want. It can be a still shot, a video clip or text on a photo. &#x20;

**Q:** I am still not sure what to start with. Can you give me an idea to use? \
**A:** Of course! We are here for your real estate needs.&#x20;

&#x20;  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/2ec8a0e5-2cfd-4689-a068-638616700088.png)\

* Got any designations? Add them in a video of you introducing yourself.  \
  If you do not have any, feel free to leave it out.&#x20;
* Let them know how long you have been in the real estate business.  \
  If you are just starting, feel free to leave it out.&#x20;
* Tell them what drives you to help them with their real estate needs. &#x20;
* Tell them a little about yourself.   \
  Do you have any animals, favorite items, etc.?  \
  The part of the city or cities you work in.   \
  What did you used to do before coming into real estate?  \
  Any hobbies you may have.&#x20;
* Reference any relevant information you feel would be appealing to the customer.&#x20;
* Don’t want to make a full video of you talking? You don’t have to! Use pictures and text or even your voice to narrate through our mobile app!&#x20;
