# Accounting - Recurring Charge/Credit Templates

The purpose of this article is to outline the steps needed to enter a recurring credit for an agent.

To enter a recurring credit for an agent to apply on a monthly or annual basis, navigate to **Agents > Setup > Recurring Charge/Credit Templates**.  

*   Click **Add**
*   Enter a unique **Description**
*   Select **Credit** from the **Type** dropdown
*   Select **Monthly** or **Annually** from the **Frequency** dropdown
*   Enter **Amount** of the recurring credit as a positive number
*   Select the GL code from the **Account** dropdown 
*   Select **Project** from the dropdown, if applicable
*   Check the **Subject to late fee?** box, if applicable
*   Check the **Taxable?** box, if applicable
*   Click the **Manage Subscriptions** button, to select the agent or agents that will receive the monthly or annual credit
*   After selections are made, click **OK**
*   Then click **Save**

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/23c4e00b-7ed8-428b-b5a0-5a59ae1f614d)**

Each month when you are ready to create the recurring credit, navigate to **Agents > Processing > Process Recurring Charges/Credits**.

*   Click on the **Agents** tab 
*   Click **Select Active Only**
    *   All active agents will be selected, however, credits will only be generated for subscribed agents 

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6220f953-d3e4-4908-a3aa-6255e77ed5ed)**

*   Click on the **Recurring Charge/Credit Templates** tab
*   Put a check mark in the **Selected** column to the left of the recurring credit

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/da693071-8cb3-4d1f-8eec-201ef5f549af)

*   Enter **Accrual Date** for credit
*   Click **Preview Charges/Credits to Create** button

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0e18247c-6fa8-4224-8b63-04fb6e4bdf6d)

*   Review **Recurring Charges/Credits Preview Report** to ensure credits will be created for the correct agents

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/00ee2507-65d7-4b2b-aeee-6ba7dc83e646)

*   Click **Process Recurring Charges/Credits** button

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/324c430d-7842-4fcd-8b26-5664de5740d7)

The following pop-up will be displayed:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/20844740-6ed7-4dae-930e-1866ea805cda)

*   Click **Yes** to proceed or **No** to cancel the process

If you click **Yes**, you will receive the following pop-up detailing the number of successfully processed items:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f6d1bcbb-b2b7-4e02-9923-be658370c57f)

*   Click **OK**

If you would like to apply the credit to the agent's open charges, navigate to **Agents > Processing > AR Credits/Payments**. 

*   Highlight the line item with the **Unapplied** credit 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/07143709-a456-4633-bfcf-6787ba9fdf5f)

*   Click **Edit**

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/25ffc57c-db1a-4736-bca9-70762892e43f)**

*   Click **Apply Payment**

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6a6eb443-ea55-4cf3-addc-ce09f2cf7912)**

*   Click **Auto-Apply** to apply the credit to the oldest agent charge, or manually apply the credit to any of the open charges
*   Click **OK**

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e7e5fb42-74cc-47ee-a4e1-aadcf757e643)**

*   Click **Save** 

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b0c23d99-0560-46da-82cd-47083768ca12)**

Note:  If the agent does not currently have unpaid charges, you can apply the credit at a later date, when there are agent charges to apply it to.
