# How to View a Contact's Details

To view a contact’s details, click on their name.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/fcc8bc46-8370-4cdd-8403-bf7ea635c9a4)

**Top Section**

The top section shows the following information:

• First & last name.

• Phone number.

• Email address.

• Buying and/or selling.

• Status: Hot, Warm, Cold or other status. This field is set manually.

• Client or Lead.

• Registration Status: Unregistered (lead) or registered (client).

• If they are pre-qualified and if so, for how much.

• Contact Score: The higher the number, the more active this person has been on your website.

• Reassign: Allows you to assign the lead to another agent or user.

**Details Tab**

The details page is organized into the following sections:

• **To Do’s**: To Do’s will show action items that are pending, due or overdue. Click on the “+To Dos” to add a new To Do item.

• **Contact Details**: View and edit specific details regarding the lead, such as name, email address, and phone number. An additional person can be added to the contact record. The additional person could be a spouse, business associate or other interested people.&#x20;

• **Personal Details**: Here you can add the birthdate, home anniversary, nickname, employer and job title for your lead.

• **Website Info**: The website info box only shows on Client records and allows you to manage how the CRM will send saved searches and saved property information. Edit and manage details regarding the client account such as their login name, preferences to receive email and/or text messages for saved properties and saved searches. You can also set the frequency of saved search or saved property emails.

• **Customer Profile**: Here, you can document information regarding your lead’s buying and/or selling activity. Easily create a buying or selling profile with details regarding their purchase or sale.

• **Contact Notes**: Add and view notes and comments submitted by the lead on a contact form. Click the “+Add Note” to add any additional notes. Notes are sorted so the most recent note will show at the top.

• **Recent Activity**: This is a 30-day lookback of actions and activities related to your lead. This displays the number of saved searches, saved properties, action plans and to do items.

• **Groups**: You can manage leads by group assignment. Create your own custom groups and add or remove leads to groups with the click of a check box.

• **Action Plans:** Action plans are drip email marketing campaigns. Here you can add an action plan to your lead.

**Properties Tab:**

* There are two different views of the Properties page.
* This is determined by the contact type either being a lead, or a client.
* If the contact is a lead, this page will show properties they have viewed.
* Click on the property photo to view the property detail page.
* If the contact is a client, this page will have more functionality available such as the ability to view, add, or edit their saved searches and saved properties.
* You can see both properties they have inquired about as well as properties they have viewed.

**History Tab:**

* In this section, you can see Actions, Activities, Lead Routing, Emails sent and more.
* The most recent activity will show at the top.
