# How to Utilize Contact Groups

Groups can be a powerful way to manage your leads and clients. They can be associated with one or more groups. Custom groups can also be created, such as potential buyers, potential sellers, and more.

Assigning action plans to a group can be an easy and efficient way to market to your contacts. When adding new contacts to the group, the action plan will automatically be assigned to that person, and they will start receiving email messages from you. Calendar events can be associated with a group too. The calendar event or To-Do will show on your CRM Dashboard.
