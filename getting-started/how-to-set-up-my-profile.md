# How to Set Up My Profile

Let’s start by setting up your profile in your **My Account** section.  At the top of the left navigation, click on the Services option and select Website Administration.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/35879baf-7ef9-4e2b-9d18-dd69a9746f70)

From within the Website Dashboard, select the drop-down option and select “My Account”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0342fc29-fb59-4bbe-8e01-68c811886590)

Then, on the left navigation, click on **My Profile**. **Note**: To navigate back to your content pages, click Home in the top left corner.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/cff50809-95d3-4229-ae75-97350142c995)

The following items are available to update or edit on the My Profile page:

*   **Profile / Agent photo**
    *   Below the agent photo, click the Change Photo text, then navigate to the photo you would like to show on your website, then select the photo.
*   **Phone numbers**
    *   The Mobile number will show on your website by default. If the mobile number is blank, then the Direct Office number will show.
    *   Add Fax and Toll-Free phone numbers as needed.
*   **Email address**
*   **NAR designations**
*   **License State**
*   **License number**
*   **Languages Spoken**
    *   Select the languages you speak. The selected languages are searchable on the company website, agent search.
*   **Change Login Info**
    *   Update your username and password. This will also change your login information for the CRM.
*   **View office name and details**

Note: Depending on your company configuration, some fields may not be editable. Contact your company website administrator for details.

If you make any changes to this page, be sure to click **Save** at the bottom.

If you require any assistance with your setup or have questions, please contact our support team at support@nestfully.com.
