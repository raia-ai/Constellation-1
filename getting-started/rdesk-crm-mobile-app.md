# RDESK CRM – MOBILE APP

## RDESK CRM – MOBILE APP

rDesk CRM Mobile app puts your leads and clients in the palm of your hand. Designed for the real estate professional who is on the move and needs to stay on top of leads, tasks and client details. The CRM Mobile App is a powerful tool in your arsenal. CRM Mobile, will keep you organized, allow you to accept and contact your leads immediately with a few taps. Improve your efficiency and become more productive all without being tied to your desk.

This guide will describe how to manage leads and clients using the Mobile CRM App.

To view or download a PDF version of this guide, go to:  
[http://redassistsimages.fnistools.com/Uploads/RECos/1180/ContentFiles/red-rdesk-crm-mobileapp-user-guid](http://redassistsimages.fnistools.com/Uploads/RECos/1180/ContentFiles/red-rdesk-crm-mobileapp-user-guide.pdf) 

## System Requirements  

Mobile CRM App will run on Android or iPhone iOS

We recommend a connection that is 3G or better.

## Download and Install

Downloading and installing the mobile app is quick and easy. The app is downloadable from iTunes for iOS and Play Store for Android.

_Note: the steps to download and install rDesk CRM Mobile App may vary depending on your device and version._

## Apple iOS Download and Install Instructions

Open iTunes Play Store and search for ‘REDCRM’

On the results page, touch ‘Download/Install’

Once the app has downloaded, touch ‘Open’

The app will open and present the opening page to the login screen. In the ‘Select Company’ field, enter Real Living, and select it from the list and touch ‘**Next**’.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d2b876d5-9225-495e-980b-d615aa1b74ab.png)

\*\*See the Login section below for detailed instruction to login to your mobile app.

## Android Download and Install Instructions

Open Play Store and search for ‘REDCRM’

On the results page, touch ‘Download/Install’

Once the app has downloaded, touch ‘Open’ or go to the app drawer and touch the app to open

The app will open and present the opening page to the login screen. In the ‘Select Company’ field, enter Real Living, and select it from the list and touch ‘**Next**’.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/fe92c190-d51f-49e2-84a9-00f7a69729ca.png)

\*\*See the Login section below for detailed instruction to login to your mobile app.

_Note: The app may not place an icon on your home screen. From the App Drawer you can touch and hold the icon and drag it to your home screen._

## Sync Contacts

Sync Contacts will allow you to import all your contacts from your mobile device and sync directly with your CRM Mobile app and desktop rDesk CRM with just a few clicks.

Touch Sync Contacts to open the module and begin the process.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b5d00081-1c40-4289-9aea-1e9d30bd1109.png)  

Then touch ‘Next’ 

  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c78c651e-254d-4557-a54c-f2678977994b.png) 

Contacts that are available to sync will display and are selected to sync by default.

Uncheck any contacts you wish to remove from the sync process.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e483892e-c6c8-4cab-9dff-30f3dee3b9b4.png) 

Touch ‘Next’  

On the confirmation screen, touch ‘Tap to Sync’ to begin the sync process.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6870a507-8c0d-4a63-a50c-f9bb74dffe80.png)  

Once the sync process begins, you will see the % complete. Once the process is complete, ‘Sync Complete’ message is displayed. 

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e90fab07-1ebc-4873-80d2-90ace5d97d13.png)      ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/16826b5b-d315-41f7-ac6e-939c4535f3b0.png)         

If you have a large number of contacts to sync, the process may take a while. It is recommended to use a good WiFi connection for first time sync. A large number of contacts may impact your data plan.

You can click “Continue using app” while the syncing process continues to run in the background.

Once the sync is complete, stats are displayed as confirmation.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d367b2de-7b0c-4517-ac47-9df82ff50eef.png)

## Accept A Lead

Once a Lead has been routed and assigned to you, you may need to accept the lead before working with that person.

Depending on the setting of your mobile devise, you may receive a push notification on your home screen. You can touch the notification to be taken into the mobile app where you can accept the lead.

To accept a lead, login to the mobile app. You will notice the bell icon will update to show a number. This number indicates that there is an action for you, to either accept a lead or there is a To-Do item waiting for you.

Touch the bell icon to go to the Leads page. You will notice under ‘Unaccepted Leads’ you have x-number of minutes remaining to accept the lead.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/828270a5-1c99-4299-ab8c-9a850a964872.png)

Touch the lead.  
_Pictured is a lead from a listing inquiry_

In the case where your company has setup lead retraction, on the Accept Lead page. You will see the countdown clock. If the clock reaches 0 minutes, the lead will be retracted and assigned to another agent.

_Note: Time shown in this illustration may vary depending on your company’s retraction rules and settings._

Touch the ‘Accept’ button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b7bd6e46-71f1-4c4b-853f-e34eb0f9bea5.png)

Accepting the lead will take you into the lead follow-up workflow.

## Lead Follow-Up Workflow

To better help you manage your leads, the lead follow-up workflow will step you through the initial contact and follow up with your new lead. This will help you nurture them along to a transaction.  

Once you have accepted a lead or touch on a new lead, you will be taken to a page where you can call or text the lead if they entered a phone number or email them.

Scrolling down the page, you will also see the property the property they inquired about.

Touch the Call Now or Email Now button.

You can also touch the ‘Set Reminder’ to set a push notification to remind you to follow up.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/912aaa74-d28a-4847-95dc-d1a605d6ed0c.png)

After contacting your new lead, you will be taken to the What Happened? page

On the ‘What Happened' you will have the options to select

Made Contact, Left Message/Sent email or Couldn’t Reach.

You can touch the ‘Flag’ icon in the top left of the contact card to flag this lead.

When you touch the ‘**Left Message**’ or ‘**Couldn’t Reach**’ buttons, you will be taken to the What’s Next page, where you can set a follow up item.

Touching ‘**Made Contact**’ will take you into the Opportunity workflow.

_See the ‘Customer Profile’ section of this document._

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d4a7b3bd-23d1-430b-ac59-7636b8c75ac3.png) 

On the What’s Next screen, touch Showing, Listing Appt or To-Do to schedule a follow up item with your lead.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/eb145181-b026-41ae-82aa-1b27b4645d73.png)

Scheduling a follow up is as easy as selecting the date and time of your follow-up. This action item will be tied to your lead.

Touch the **date** on the calendar to select the date of your To-Do item.

Touch the ‘**Type**’ to choose other options such as ‘Follow-Up’, ‘Meeting’, ‘Showing’ or another option.

Select the Start and End times.

Select **Reminder** to receive a push notification reminder of the To-Do.

Add any **notes** if needed.

Touch **Save** to save the To-Do to your lead.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/11a00c79-0a01-480d-819a-0e6e3f713402.png)

## Customer Profile

As part of the lead follow-up workflow you have the option to document the type of opportunity the lead is. Are they Buying, Selling or an opportunity for both.

The Opportunity will allow you to capture important information regarding your leads buying and/or selling preferences.

When you accepted the lead, as shown in the lead acceptance workflow above, and touched ‘Made Contact’ you will be taken to the Opportunity module. Or

On the contact card for the lead, touch ‘**Customer Profile +**’

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/2120f830-6a70-446c-a674-7d7a3d4b0fa7.png) 

Touch the opportunity for the lead, Buying, Selling or Prospect. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6832680e-556d-4f04-b138-4fa67cc92360.png)

On the resulting page, enter the

*   Property location of interest
*   price range
*   Estimated time frame
*   Planning to Sell Y/N
*   Contingency
*   Pre-Qualified, and if so for how much.
*   Enter desired property details
    *   Type
    *   \# Beds
    *   \# Bath
    *   Square Feet
    *   Lot size
    *   Year built

Click ‘**Save**’

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/824576e6-4967-405a-ae24-c415dfbfe1ad.png)

This information will be available to you as reference. 

## Lead Ingestion / Lead Intake

Receive leads from external sources like Zillow, Trulia, Realtor or other lead generation site. Automatically adding the leads to your rDesk CRM and notifying you as soon as they arrive.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/88420425-ef7c-44e3-92b8-5e15d55a0f64.png)

On the Lead Ingestion / Lead Intake page, you will need to copy your unique code. Your code is in the form of an email address. Use this code as the email address where your third-party lead generation site, will send your lead notification.

Copy the email address that shows on the Lead Ingestion page.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1096f975-463f-4206-b8d6-249ef09b4331.png)

On your third-party lead site, use this email address as the email to be notified of new leads.

That system will send a lead notification email to your unique email address. rDesk will do the rest. Our system will receive that email and process the lead and assign it to your account.

Under ‘**Forward Emails**’ enter your email address to have those lead emails forwarded to you as well.

Touch Groups on the flyout menu.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4b88d2a7-f2c7-4555-86e2-685b3f15f463.png)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/57cd616e-9fcf-49f5-a360-e64713205b88.png)

On the Group detail page, you will see your leads and contacts show as a “card” on the page. You can ‘swipe’ the card to the right or left to view the next card in the list. You are NOT removing this person from the list.

Once the contact shows you can call, text or email them right from this screen. You can create a Buying or Selling Opportunity for your client of lead. Or touch the pencil icon to edit the groups the contact is associated to.
