# How to use Account Management tab

The below article reviews Paradym's Account Management tab.

The Account Management tab has a lot of great information for Admins, Brokers, and other leadership members. Learn what account management is and how to use it for your benefit&#x20;

* Administrative Guide &#x20;
* How to Use&#x20;
* Features&#x20;

**Steps Outline Below:**&#x20;

Step 1: What is Account Management used for?&#x20;

Step 2: How many Agents have assigned Paradym accounts; How many Offices do you have&#x20;

Step 3: How to search for an agent and view their profile&#x20;

Step 4: How to Log-In as an agent and/or view their log in credentials&#x20;

Step 5: How to change your Account Management views – There are THREE views to select from&#x20;

**See below for steps 1 - 5**

**Step 1:** What is Account Management used for?&#x20;

* Manage agent accounts and assigned offices&#x20;
* Add/Remove agents&#x20;
* Agent activity reporting &#x20;
* Turning on Listing-Sync for agents&#x20;
* Communicating with agents via email , text message, or mobile app notifications&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/bb1d0c6f-3236-483a-9053-43e1d0bfc592.png)

NOTES: &#x20;

* Click on the + symbol (in the green box in the image above) at the top right to Add an Agent to your Paradym account brokerage account (A separate Tutorial / Article has been created that will provide specific instructions how to add and delete agents)&#x20;

**Step 2:** How many Agents have assigned Paradym accounts; How many Offices do you have&#x20;

To Determine how many agents have an assigned Paradym account, locate the “ All (#) “ to the left. This number should also reflect the “ All Agents (#) “ located near the center of the page as well. However, the number of agents will change according to the office you have selected (see below).&#x20;

To Determine how many offices, locate the “ Offices (#) “ on the left&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/fbfb2fa0-ec90-4f5c-9bbc-a4283772a58d.png)

Notes: &#x20;

* To add offices, you will just click the + sign beside “ Offices (#) “ and begin set up&#x20;
* To view the profile of an office, click on the name of the office&#x20;
* To view an individual office’s agent list, click on the number of agents which is located to the right of its assigned office name. The number of agents will change as you select each office&#x20;

**Step 3:** How to search for an agent and view their profile&#x20;

There is an easy tool for you that will help you search for a particular agent. Click on the search icon which will direct you to a search bar.   For advanced option, select “Advanced.” &#x20;

To view an Agent Profile, click on the name of the agent.   Your next window will be the agent profile (See Step 4)   Quick note: For a larger view of all the agents, select the 3 small horizontal lines beside All Agents.&#x20;

**Step 5:** How to change Account Management views&#x20;

There are multiple types of views (3) in your Account Management tab.   Let’s take a closer look at each view.&#x20;

1. Gallery View will have a 4 small squares icon and will reveal agent profile photos (See below)&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6df982a7-6bd8-4d10-ab96-152582fc4920.png)

&#x20;       2\. List View will have a 5 horizontal lines icon and will reveal Agent activity (See below)&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/cac3a734-fe45-45fb-869d-49b416b36398.png)

NOTES:&#x20;

* Use the scroll bar on the right that will help you go vertically up and down on the page&#x20;
* Use the scroll bar at the bottom that will help you go left to right on the page&#x20;
* As you scroll left to right, the entitled columns will shift as well as you scroll&#x20;
* You can choose a list of agents and check mark in the box to the left, THEN use the “Select an Action” at the top right to directly send a message to the group of agents you have selected&#x20;
* If the agent has a colored (not greyed out) social media icon under their name, this means the agent has certain social media set up and is currently active&#x20;
* If the agent has a greyed out icon, this means that for that particular social media platform for the agent, somehow the social media for the greyed out icon has been disconnected. There are several variables to why social media has been disconnected. Please partner with the agent and your Paradym team to see why they are no longer connected.&#x20;
