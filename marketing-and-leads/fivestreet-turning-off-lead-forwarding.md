
# FiveStreet: Turning Off Lead Forwarding

If you would like to stop leads from flowing into FiveStreet from a particular source, follow these steps:Â 

1\. Go to the Lead Sources tab of your account.

2\. Click Stop Processing \_\_\_\_ Leads next to the lead source.

3\. Change your @fivestreet.me email address back to your preferred email address in the lead source. (Zillow, Trulia, Realtor.com)
