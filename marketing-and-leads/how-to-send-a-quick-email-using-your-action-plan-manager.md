# How to Send a Quick Email Using Your Action Plan Manager

Click the **Quick Email** link to launch **Quick Email** functionality.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/4faad2c5-73f9-4125-b088-d65d54c298bc)

Within **Quick Email**, you can select a single Action Plan activity to email to your contacts, groups, or to any email address you manually enter by clicking the Import from APM Email.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2bb36c6f-6032-4202-92d4-e874c4ea00fc)

_Note: The Birthday and Home Anniversary email activities will be available to be sent via **Quick Email.**_
