# How to use Brand Builder to Share local content

**Brief Description/Summary:** 

Paradym’s Brand Builder feature can be a very beneficial tool to use from Brokerages’ perspective, so what are some common ways you can utilize Brand Builder? What is Brand Builder? Paradym wants to ensure that Brokerage's are  excited to use Brand Builder, know how to use the tool, and ways to find it beneficial and useful in their daily routines.  

**Steps Outline Below:** 

Step 1: What is Brand Builder and Why is it important? 

Step 2: What are some common ways to utilize Brand Builder? 

Step 3: Step-by-Step Guide 

Sneak Peek 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/11271db4-0a34-4b29-9f3a-88ee62610e10.png) 

**Step 1: What is Brand Builder and Why is it important?** 

Brand Builder (Which is displayed and shown in a Brokerage account and known as “My Content” ) is an easy way to post local content onto your Agent’s social media pages for them, on their behalf. It can be very beneficial to a brokerage that wants to post their own local content and “broadcast” or publish content for an agent onto their social media pages. Marketing director’s will love the tool! Why?  You can post content on your agents’ pages for them!  It is very important as it will save a ton of time for marketing teams and admins.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6f8f0050-9413-4c56-9506-5002d83c7aab.png) 

**Step 2: What are some common ways to utilize Brand Builder?** 

General Ideas, types of posts, tips: 

*   Promoting local partnerships and foundations 
*   Giveaways 
*   Promoting local contests and events 
*   Supporting other local community businesses 
*   Testimonials and Appreciations 

*   Promoting SOLD properties  
*   Celebrating Birthdays  
*   Celebrating New agents 
*   Celebrating Awards or Agent promotions 
*   Local articles or links from the web 

**Step 3: Step-by-Step Guide     (13 steps < 1 – 13  are in order below)** 

(1) Log into your CMA broker account and locate the Broker name at the top right and click on their name. 

(2) Select the down arrow to view a drop down menu with a list of several options to choose from; select “My Content” from your menu of options. 

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4d7ddf26-7009-42de-b487-e5ea6f3689a3.png)

(3) Your next window you will come to has several areas to point out (see below picture and further notes)  

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/ce588f62-d833-4577-980d-897ae0e3c547.png)

Notes:  

You will notice:

     (A) The + symbol to start adding and posting new content 

     (B) My Content (##) which will show you total number of posts you have created 

     (C) A drop down list of posts you have created 

     (D) The date you created the post 

     (E) The number of agents that will receive the scheduled post 

     (F) A way you can preview the post, edit your post, or broadcast your post 

NEXT > (see step 4) 

(4) Let’s begin adding and creating a new post, first Click on the + symbol as shown in the picture above. Next, you will see a space to add your new post. (see below and further notes)  

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b3ca179b-07d8-40e3-aaa9-004a1a9e7e8d.png)

Notes: 

*   Choose what you are wanting to add 
*   You can add a photo or video 
*   You can insert (copy and paste) a link you are wanting to post 
*   Insert your choice of verbiage in the space provided which will be displayed in the description area of your social media post that introduces your item 

(5) After making your edits and you’re ready to move forward, click “Save”  (see above image)  

(6) You should now see your post at the top of your list of created Posts (see below image)  

(7) You can either Preview of Edit your post if you need or if you are ready to finish and schedule your post for your agents, please select the “Broadcast” option  (see below image)  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/995bd839-b15e-4785-9062-2e827c27ed23.png) 

Next Image below

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c2aa0ff1-1e88-429f-98db-3aa97d648c16.png) 

(8) Make your selections on which agents and which offices will receive the post (see image above)  

(9) Then, select the day and time you wish to schedule your post (see image above)  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1cd9a266-7330-4672-9f91-428df8d9eeb0.png) 

(10) Choose the option or not if you would like to send a review notification to your agents to let them know a post has been scheduled on their behalf  

(11) You’re now ready to FINISH your Post!   Select “Broadcast” and then your next screen you will need to confirm the details saying you want to Broadcast to your selected group of agents. 

(12) You are Finished!   (see below image and further details) 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d6e52362-67b1-4a6b-a167-8303c6259571.png) 

(13) Scheduled our post for X date and to X amount of agents. I can always Preview if I need 

Notes: 

*   Since you have already scheduled your post, you are no longer able to go back and make edits in the broker account. If you’ve made a mistake or need to remove the post, you would need to log into your agent accounts individually and locate the Dashboard menu, and select “Social Schedule” to make any changes or remove the post.  
*   How do I know my post was successful?  You can log into your agent accounts and locate the Dashboard menu, and select “Social Analytics” to review and find the post. Or, you can spot check a few agents and go to their social media accounts to see if the post went through
