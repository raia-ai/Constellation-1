
# How to locate your Leads

Please Log into your Paradym account.

1\. Select your NAME at the top right of the page for a drop down menu

2\. Select "Dashboard" from the menu of options 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/9b10da57-0051-46ed-bd13-6bfa3f609088.png)

3\. On the left side of your screen, you will see a menu of options running vertically on your page.  Please select "Leads" from your options.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dd4b3560-6039-4034-aef7-ac37f34f46eb.png)

4\. The Next screen you see should be displaying your Leads page

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/440767a8-29df-4b26-8a88-3beab0062952.png)

5\. Next, Select "Edit Lead" to see the details 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e000b413-275b-4528-bfcc-4b0b532ad205.png)

Finished
