# How to Edit the Design of Your Website

The design is the overall color scheme and look of the website.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6461328c-c208-420f-95b9-ea9759e203bd)

To change the design, mouse over a design to show the **Select** and **Preview** buttons.

Click Preview to see a preview of the design.  Here is how a preview will appear:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/50dc111b-c3d0-45a9-b4ec-9506432bebd9)

  
Click the Select to apply the design to your website.

Some companies will have the option to select a color palette for the design. Click the **Color Options** dropdown to see the available colors. The colors will be applied as accent colors to buttons, links or background colors.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/81209e5f-3b5a-4567-af1a-69712f645e97)
