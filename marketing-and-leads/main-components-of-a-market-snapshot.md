# Main Components of a Market Snapshot

Once a Market Snapshot has been generated for a contact, an email will be sent with a link to the Market Snapshot. When they click the link, the snapshot will open directly in their internet browser.

Below is a sample of a Market Snapshot. We can use this to review the main components of a Market Snapshot.

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/bf117990-0028-4c88-b3e9-13211e16aa77)**

  
**Agent Photo and Contact Information**

The information that appears here is taken from the information you enter in the Market Snapshot **Agent Information** section.

**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9c8cc3a8-7ff5-4234-80f4-3e1b072b1f32)**  
Click the **Change Report** or **Change location** link to change the report schedule and the property information used to generate the report data.

**Market Trends**

Below the agent information section are info boxes displaying selected market trend data. This data is based over the last 90 days.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/10d4ca78-a96b-4206-b8fb-46b8820ecab7)  
To view more details, click anywhere in the info box.

**Map and Property Information**

The Map and Property Information section is displayed next to the market trend info boxes. Click the map to display the relevant properties on a map as well as next to the map in a list format.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1f4aea84-54ac-43fa-b4bd-96f7872d8aef)

Clicking on a property on the map will highlight it in the list, clicking on a property in the list or grid will display the Listing Details Page, with detailed property information about the listing.
