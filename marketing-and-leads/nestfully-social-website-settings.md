
# Nestfully Social Website Settings | Constellation1 Customer Hub

On the Website Settings page, you will find the following.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0b34b49a-32ab-483c-a915-5a5867da3211)

**Website / Marketing Name:** Depending on the selected website design, this may show at the top of your website.

  
**Website URL:** This is the URL or the primary address for your website.  
   - **Pro Tip**: You can connect a custom domain to your website. Example, AgentName.com.  Contact your website administrator for details.

Place a check mark next to the following items to display them on your website:  
• **Preferred Phone.**  
**• Email.**  
**• Personal Fax.**  
**• NAR Designations.**  
**• License Number.**

**Note:** Some items may be grayed out and are managed at the company level.

  
**Client Login Notifications:** Receive an email each time one of your clients log into their account on your website.

**Social Networks:** Add the URL to your social media next to the corresponding social network. An icon will display on your homepage and linked to your social media.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/154b6ab7-a915-486e-ac4e-7a4be8ad1d78)

If you make any changes to this page, be sure to click **Save** at the bottom.

If you require any assistance with this aspect of your Nestfully website or have questions, please contact our support team at [support@nestfully.com](mailto:support@nestfully.com).
