# Managing Email Activities Within Action Plan Manager

From the Action Plan Manager grid, click **Manage Email Activities**. You will see all activities displayed.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/bd77c035-8c40-472e-bb2f-c777599ffde7)

**Show** will allow you to choose from a dropdown list how many activities display on the screen, the default is 10. If you change the default, your selection will “stick” if you navigate away and back.

**View** allows you to filter activities by the owner from a dropdown list. If you change the default, your selection will “stick” if you navigate away and back. Under Action Plans, a number will display that shows how many plans currently uses this activity.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b48ae6aa-dd3f-43b5-b1d6-9cb4c07468ff)

**Under Actions**, the following icons can display for each action plan.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/55d44993-d468-4f25-8faa-0168e7157060)

To edit an activity, click the edit icon. The **Edit Email Activity** overlay will display.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/58be87d3-daf1-4987-9f3e-9a97f6776a9a)

To review changes made, select the **Preview** option.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f5d0ec89-7450-4873-8b6d-bd25834d6f7d)

Click **Save** OR Click **Save As** to save the activity with another name OR click Cancel to close and go back to the Manage Email Activities list. To copy an activity, click the copy icon.

The Edit Email Activity overlay will display. The title will default to “Copy of ..”which you can revise to a new name.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c1f35159-d490-4663-ae94-1545abd1180e)

Revise the content if desired and click **Preview** to view the changes made.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/62405614-5727-43f1-a6f2-3e7f621eae00)

Once you are satisfied with your changes, click the **Save** button.
