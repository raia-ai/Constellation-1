# How to Manage Who Receives Your Action Plans

To add contacts/groups to your Action Plan, go to **Manage Recipients** and click **Add Recipients**. The Add Recipients overlay will display.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e7f948d5-e49c-47bf-a5cc-92e15bb7a965)

On the Contacts tab, you can check the box next to the contacts. Check the top box for all contacts.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ca1b2462-3bd7-420b-8ab7-aaac2182bfdb)

You can enter a partial name to search for a contact.

Click the **Group** tab and check the box next to the group. Check the top box for all groups. You can enter a partial name to search groups.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ccb7e1d5-65d8-4516-ae18-df440d67a2be)

Click **Save** OR click **Cancel** to return to the Action Plan.

Under the Manage Recipients section, the Contact/Group Name will display on the page along with:&#x20;

**Start Date:** This is the Start Date for that Contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead to begin receiving the Action Plan emails.

**End Date:** This is the End Date for that Contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead to begin receiving the Action Plan emails.

**Status:** This is the status of the Action Plan.

**The definitions are noted below.**

* **Scheduled** – Contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead is on the plan, email activities have not yet been sent.
* **In Progress** – Contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead is on the plan, at least one email activity has been sent and at least one email activity is remaining to be sent.
* **Done** – Contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead is on plan, all email activities have been sent.
* **On-going** – This applies to groups, as the contacts contained in the group can be in any of the above states.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/548aaced-5c7e-43c6-9f5b-fb432537d291)

To remove recipients from the Action Plan, click the remove icon next to the contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead name. A confirmation message will display, click Yes OR the contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead will be removed from the Action Plan but will remain in the system. Click No and the message will close and the contact, group, and/or unassigned Lead will remain on the Action Plan.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5601be19-c6ee-4c79-a3ed-144c0317fee4)

_Note: You can also add/remove a contact, group or Lead to/from an Action Plan from the individual record or contact groups. You can also have incoming leads automatically added to an Action Plan._
