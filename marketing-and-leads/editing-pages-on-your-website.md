# Editing Pages on Your Website

Click **Pages** on the left navigation to view the pages on your website. The page titled “Welcome” is your Homepage.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a14ebc14-5dc2-48b1-b570-3db7fccf4fb3)

To add a new page, click the **Plus icon (+)** or click the **Add Page** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/faf61f66-b927-4492-8092-df78fd88ebe7)

When adding a new page, select the page layout, there are several to choose from. Click on the layout you would like to use for your page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/da9bc24f-9147-4ccc-bb60-b6d4c3f77bea)

Once you select the layout, you are taken to the settings for this page. Enter a **Page Title** and **Description**. This will help with Search Engine Optimization.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c3ed5f48-d623-484a-82d9-f230fb4cad33)

Then click **Content**. This will take you to the tile view of your page content.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/67c0f91d-e460-4e28-b6e8-ec0c409d1c59)

See the **Content Editor** section to edit and customize your content.

**Pro Tip:** When a new content page is created, link it to the top navigation.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f86d3d28-5179-4250-9029-b05e30f4a368)
