# How to Enable Consumer Registration on Your Website

With our Nestfully website product, you can compel a consumer to register on your website after viewing a certain number of property detail pages.

Enter the number of properties a consumer can view before being presented with a Login/Register screen.

To turn this off or disable this feature, enter a number 0.
