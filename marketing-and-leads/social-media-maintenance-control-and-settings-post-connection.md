# Social Media Maintenance, Control and Settings (Post-Connection)

The below article reviews how to effectively maintain and control your Paradym social media connections and their general settings menu. Social Media Maintenance, Control and Settings (Post-Connection).  Steps 1-15 will take you on a training tutorial making sure your Social Media settings are correct. 

Step 1: Go to [www.Paradym.com](https://word-edit.officeapps.live.com/we/www.Paradym.com) and Click on "My Account" to get signed into your Paradym account.

 ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage1.png) 

Step 2:  Sign into your Paradym account using your Log-In credentials: username and password. Click Sign In    

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/HTPTIFPimage2.png)

Step 3: Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Settings.”    

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage3.png)

Step 4: On your lower left-hand panel, you will see your “Content” section. This section is for users of the Reach Social platform only and will include Steps 4-6. If you do not have Reach Social, please go to Step 7.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage4.png) 

Step 5: Locate and click on your "Categories" tab option.  Your Categories page allows you to choose the topics (i.e. Categories) that appeal to you and your audience. Make your selections. You will see one automated Reach Social post per day based on your selected categories. 

NOTE:  Below, we’ve compiled a list of general topics, holidays, and seasons. This section allows you to choose your customized interests and topics so that our Marketing team can get to work for you. Once you’ve chosen your topics, please make sure to select the “Save” option in the upper-right of your screen.  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage5.png)

Step 6: Referring back to Step 4, please now select the "Schedule" option this time. Choose a schedule for your Reach Social content to be shared onto your social media platforms. Paradym will post One Reach Social content based on your Categories you've selected and in accordance with the days of the week you have identified as well. After making your selections, select “Save.” 

*   **Next Calendar Fill:** This is a helpful reminder of your monthly pre-created content and schedule. You will receive an email monthly advising that your calendar is ready for review. 
*   **Best Practice:** Statistics show that social media traffic is highest on weekend days. Keep this in mind for scheduled social media marketing strategy. 
*   **Pause automatic sharing:** Indicates turning on or off your Reach Social content feature.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage6.png)

Step 7: Referring back to Step 3, make sure you are in your settings menu and Click on "Social Signature"

*   Like an email signature, this is your social media signature for your Paradym account.
*   Use Hashtags, slogans, tag lines, names, phone numbers, addresses, etc.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage17.png)

Step 8:  Review your Social Schedule. Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Dashboard.” You will see an “Upcoming Social Posts” section towards the middle to bottom of your screen. Select “View All” in the upper-right corner of this section.  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage8.png)

Step 9: You will see an a preview of your “Upcoming Social Posts” towards the middle to bottom area of your screen. Select “View All” in the upper-right corner of this Section.   OR, you can also select "Social Schedule" on the left hand column menu giving you the same result.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage9.png)

Step 10: Here, you will see a preview of your upcoming social posts. You also have the ability to reschedule, preview, and edit your content. The screenshot below highlights where you will see these options to change the post. You will need to hover your mouse over the platform icon for these options to appear. 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage10.png)

NOTE: The types of posts you will see determines which Paradym platform you have, for example, you may see just your tours in your schedule. This means you have Paradym's Marketing platform. If you see other types of content and posts then you have our Reach Social platform. In addition, there is an option to “Create New Post” which is also a capability for users of Paradym's Reach Social platform. (Reach Social is a premium platform offered by Paradym).

Step 11: Select "Create New Post" (Reach Social platform only, Opt-In upgrade optional).  Here you can type or insert an attachment to create your own post. Click on the social icons to indicate which social platform you'd like to send, and click on the "Post Now" drop down menu to schedule your post for a different day and time. 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage11.png)

NOTES: 

*   You can add a photo or video

*   With the “Post Now” option in the lower-right corner, you can utilize the drop-down arrow to post immediately, schedule for later, or add to your schedule that has been set in place. **This will appear in green** once you’ve typed in the “What would you like to say?” section, or added a photo/video. 

Step 12: View your Social Analytics - Make sure you are still in your Dashboard menu, select "Social Analytics"

 ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage13.png)

Step 13: Now we are moving on to your Tour sharing settings. Go back to your "Settings" menu. Locate and Click "Change" beside your Social Connections.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage14.png)

Step 14: Click on the gear icon shown below.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage15.png)

Step 15: Here you will have a menu of options for Tour Sharing only. If you hover over the small i icon you will receive further details and definitions.

*   Smart Schedule: Paradym will post your tour for you at optimal times.
*   Instant Posting: Paradym will post your tour for you instantly upon a tour being created. 
*   Post manually: Paradym will not post a tour for you on your behalf. With this option you will post a tour manually which gives you 100% control. To Post a tour manually: Go to your "Tours" menu, select the tour of choice, and then choose your social media platforms you'd like to post your tour, type your comment, and post.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-SocialMediaMaintenanceAndSettings_Post-Connection_/SMMASimage16.png)

 Finished. You Have went through a thorough maintenance, control, and settings tutorial on Paradym's Social Media Connections.
