# Teams | Constellation1 Customer Hub

This article details the steps required to configure Teams. 

_A few notes before setting up a Team._

*   _A Team Commission Plan will be created when all members of the team will jointly contribute to a single commission plan_
    *   Click here for details on setting up [Team Commission Plan](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/17485430) 
*   _The plan can be assigned to all members of the Team via the Commission Details tab of the Agent's profile_
*   _Team members do not need to be assigned the same plan, but the plan MUST be a team plan_
*   _The plan will calculate the commission using the Team Leaders' Anniversary timeline_
*   _The plan can be overwritten and/or excluded from the team commission history on a per transaction basis_

On the left navigation bar select **Agents** / Teams tab and select Add.

Complete the details as required:

*   Team Name - the name of the team
*   Effective Date - the date the team was formed
*   Assigned Office - select the applicable office from the drop down
*   Status - Active or Inactive (used when team is no longer a team)
*   Deactivate Date - date the team dissolved and will be enabled when Status is Inactive
*   Team Number - enter the applicable number if required, ex. 1, 2, 3 (to manage the number of teams you have)
*   Team Franchise ID - enter the applicable number as required by the franchise

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17637568)

In the Team Members section, start typing the agent's name who is part of the team. When displayed, select Team Leader or Team Member from the Member Type drop down, select the applicable "Team" commission plan and enter the allocation percentage. When you have added all team members, select Save.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17636530)

At the transaction level, on the Commission tab user will select the +Add Agent/Team link and search for the name of the team created. Once the team name is selected, all team members will display with the allocation percentage described above.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17636531)

To set up Team Leader permissions refer to this link for further details, [Viewing and Editing Team Leader Permissions](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/21841766)
