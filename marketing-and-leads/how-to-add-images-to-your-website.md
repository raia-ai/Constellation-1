# How to Add Images to Your Website

**Images** is where you can upload and manage photos, logos and banners.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/19c31ada-2fe3-4f50-8e2f-52fe6af180dd)

Click on the type of image you would like to upload.

*   **Images** are photos that may be added to a web page.
*   **Banners** are images that will show at the top of the homepage.
*   **Logos** are images that show in the top left corner of the website. _This option may not be available for some companies._

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e080a15b-1eec-4b1c-a78c-60309b78fd9b)

Each image type has a size requirement. Minimum size requirements:

*   **Content images** must be 300 pixels wide and 200 pixels tall.
*   **Banner images** must be 925 pixels wide and 400 pixels tall.
*   **Logo images** much be 300 pixels wide and 30 pixels tall.

To upload an image, drag and drop the image to the **‘Add a Photo’** box or click the ‘Add a Photo’ box. Navigate to the image on your computer.

The image will show in a cropping tool. Drag the blue lines to crop the image or click **Skip Resizing** to upload the full image, then click **Save**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/12fedf37-5701-4ca1-a9ee-bc54af0198ca)

The image will show in the gallery. Click on the image you would like to add to the page, then click the **Insert into Page** button.
