# How to add cell phone video to your Single Property Site

**Brief Description/Summary:** &#x20;

The Single Property Site can be customized to the agents’ preference to a degree.  An agent can customize the viewing experience by adding additional content of their choice to the Property Site. &#x20;

**What are some examples of things and types of content you can add to your Single Property Site viewer?** &#x20;

* Enhanced videography, cell phone video footage, drone video footage, Matterport links, Documents, PDF’s, floor plans, surveys, links to your website or the company website, links to partnership websites i.e. preferred mortgage lender etc., links to local media outlets or other various websites, links to your active inventory, links to a lead generation page such as “Find Your Dream Home” etc. &#x20;

&#x20;**Today’s Tutorial will be on “How to add cell phone video to your Single Property Site”**

Step 1: Download and Open your Paradym Mobile Agent App on your Cell Phone.&#x20;

(Please see separate KB-Articles on the Mobile Agent App for downloading instructions and additional training)&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/db2bee44-6931-403e-8c14-8116ecc52351.png)

Step 2: Select the listing Tour of your choice you wish to add video

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/606982d2-1750-479c-a093-93020276d875.png)

Step 3: Select “Scenes”  &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/8ffd574b-f122-42ae-b765-ba628120a2ac.png)

Step 4: Select the + symbol at the top right corner to add a scene to your tour (photo, video, panoramic) &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4809c669-53f6-4fa7-9163-b5a6e61b6f7f.png)

Step 5: If you already have a video uploaded to your camera, you may choice library and follow the prompts on your phone. Or you can choose to record a video by selecting “Take Photo or Video” or “Make Intro Video.” &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/220e9509-5dfc-4340-ae46-cf4d7e41a073.png)

Note: After completion, the video is now part of your tour. If you’re using the Fusion Viewer the video will appear more than likely at the end of your tour. It may also appear at the beginning as well so you may want to check there as well. You can re-order the scenes if you’d like to rearrange the order. Same also goes for the Single Property Site. The video will appear in the Gallery area. &#x20;

Step 6: Switch to your PC and log into your Paradym account. Click on your name at the top right for a drop down menu. Select “My Tours.” Then, select the correct Tour that you are uploading video too. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/ea515204-fbce-4f4f-a04a-c98dc3a3c75d.png)

Step 7: You should be seeing a menu of options for your selected tour of choice. In the middle, right side you should see a running vertical list of your tour’s scenes. Scroll down and locate your video you just uploaded. (note: it may take a few minutes to finish processing). Next, click on “Details” located just the right of the video. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/afcf8afc-1e21-4f1c-b5b0-af17569d3469.png)

Step 8: Next, right click anywhere in the video square on your screen. Note: But Do NOT right click inside the Circular / Play area.  Then, select “Open video in new tab” from your menu of options.  &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dd5edbee-5317-44f5-b2ae-940d85b056c3.png)

Step 9: Go to your new internet tab for the video.  Next, right click and copy the full URL Internet page address. You should now have copied the URL and ready to paste.  &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/2da4e0a1-70e0-49f9-b33c-d4a7f195682b.png)

Step 10: You should still be logged into your Paradym account. Go back to the tour. Click on your name at the top right for a drop down menu. Select “My Tours.” Then, select the correct Tour that you are uploading video too. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/fda4bd31-41ee-4b28-8bae-2cee82e4f228.png)

Step 11: On the left side of your screen you will see a menu of options. Locate and select “Additional Content”  &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/acac9631-459c-4d24-8da4-e276df187c89.png)

Step 12: Notice the Four areas you can add your new cell phone video too.  Next, click on the + sign located at the top right.  Then, select the “video” option when prompted in your next screen. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/af726eee-6130-4035-8587-2088712fb000.png)

Step 13: See screen shot below for the menu of options for you to fill out &#x20;

(A) Edit and Name the Title of the Video (customer facing, except when using as the Focal) &#x20;

(B) You will Paste in the URL you previously copied in Step 9 above into the URL tab space &#x20;

(C) Choose the Placement of your new video (Focal, Feature Content, Tab, or More Info area) &#x20;

(D) If the newly added video has any branded information included in it, please check the IDX box located at the bottom &#x20;

(E) Select “Save” when you are finished making your edits &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/22c89ccb-7d4d-4f9e-8b70-e44ba402184e.png)

Step 14: The screen just took you back to the Additional Content menu if you’d like to add anything else to your Property Site you can do so or if you’d like to make any changes to the current information.  &#x20;

NOTE (A): You can add the same video in your additional content area multiple times, to each of the four main areas: Focal, Featured, Tab, More Info if you’d like. Also, you can move the video up or down between the Four areas using the parallel, vertical arrows on the right. &#x20;

NOTE (B): To learn more about our Single Property Site viewer or if you’d like a tutorial on Paradym’s Additional Content feature, please read through and review further KB articles featuring these Topics.   We also have a KB Article on our Paradym Mobile Agent App if you'd like additional training information.
