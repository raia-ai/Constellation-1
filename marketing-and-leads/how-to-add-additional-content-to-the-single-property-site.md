
# How to Add Additional Content to the Single Property Site

The below article reviews how to Add Additional Content to the Single Property Site.

**Brief Description:**

Most real estate pros have seen of and/or are using a traditional virtual tour template to showcase an active listing they have on the market. Paradym refers to the traditional tour option, known as our Fusion Viewer. However, agents have another optional viewer to use, the Single Property Site.

*   The Property Site takes on a totally different viewing experience; However, it can also be customized to the agents’ preference to a degree.  While the Fusion has it’s own benefits and customization tools, the Property Site has the ability to take it to the next level for the real estate agent when it comes to showcasing their listing. 
*   Using the Property Site viewer, an agent can NOW customize the viewing experience by: **Adding additional content of their choice to their tour template.**

**Step 1:** Why is adding additional content important, and what are some examples of things and types of content you can add to your Property Site?  How can you utilize additional content with the Fusion viewer? 

*   Adding additional content to your tour template can be very beneficial to an agent in helping them win the attention of a customer and to hopefully turn the customer into a new lead for the agent! Adding additional content will make your Property Site stand out from the rest. It will turn your “template” into an elegant true story telling experience and a true personalized website for each of your listings! 

*   What are some examples of things and types of content you can add to your Property Site? 

Enhanced videography, video, cell phone video footage, drone video footage, Matterport links, Documents, PDF’s, floor plans, surveys, links to your website or the company website, links to partnership websites i.e. preferred mortgage lender etc., links to local media outlets or other various websites, links to your active inventory, links to a lead generation page such as “Find Your Dream Home” etc. 

*   How can you utilize additional content with the Fusion viewer? 

There is an area within your Fusion viewer called “More Information.”  It will be located on the left side menu of options in your tour. You can add additional content of your choice in this area as well.

**Step 3:** Step by Step Guide 

A) You will need to first Login into your Paradym account

B) Then, please Locate your name at the top right and use the drop down menu to select “My Tours” 

C) Make your selection to which listing tour you’d like to begin adding content too 

D) From the left column menu, locate and click on “Additional Content” (see image below) 

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/3f1a3c4a-3703-4f85-ba35-41c2b5096d9e.png) 

E) Next, Click on the + sign near the top right hand corner of your screen (see image below). The drop down menu will provide options for you and will be asking if you would like to add 3D Model, Video, Document, or Link.  Here you will Make your selection.   

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/02af488b-8907-4f95-bf95-d15c90464d9d.png) 

F) Next, you will need to name and identify your content. What you type in the Name space will show and be visible to your customer when viewing your Property Site (excluding Focal). \[See Image below\]  

G) URL – or – File.  Copy and paste the URL to the video or link that you are adding. i.e. website source of the video or website.  Or you can choose a file from your computer to upload your video, documents, pdf’s, etc. \[See Image below\]  

H) Placement.  Choose where you’d like the content to appear: Focal, Featured, Tab, or the More Info area \[See Image below\]  

I) SAVE your work!  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/81f3c272-182b-4f42-9803-47478527b4b2.png) 

J) You can always add the same piece of content to your Property Site by adding it twice and be used separately to the multiple areas. With that in mind, you can add a video to your Focal section and the same video added to your Featured Content section as well.   

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6002d073-2a77-4c0a-9fe8-5bb224540d02.png) 

K) You can Always make additional edits and/or view your content after you have added your content to your account. You can also use the up and down arrows to Transfer content from one area to another  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/78c23145-7e41-406c-8956-297aaa645356.png)
