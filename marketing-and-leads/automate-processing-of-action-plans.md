# Automate Processing of Action plans

*   In order to automate the Action plans click on “Preferences” under the drop-down icon having your account name.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/dbfc0011-efe7-4ae9-9914-7779aba1c1c3)

*   You can turn all the Action plans On/Off by simply clicking on the button next to “Automate Task processing”. If selected “Off” the action plans will be stopped and if selected “On” the action plans will be sent with one click.

**  
Note**: Initially Action plans are turned on by default it’s your preference to turn them off or on.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/84c27df8-b418-4980-9537-2b6633677253)

*   A confirmation dialog box appears once any option either “Off” or “On” is selected.
