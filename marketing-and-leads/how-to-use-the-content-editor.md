# How to Use the Content Editor

Whether you create a new page or are editing an existing page, this section will walk you through the details to customize your content. To begin, click the **pencil icon** next to the page you wish to edit.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/22d5afa5-2379-4fa0-86ec-0fa1d60a231b)

On the content edit page, content elements will show as tiles along the left navigation.

  
Click on the tile to edit or modify the content or feature.

**Content Title and Sub-Title**

  
Click on the ‘Content Title’ or Sub-Title tile to edit the title and sub-title of the page.

  
**Pro Tip:** Enter a meaningful title and sub-title on your content pages. This helps with SEO.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ad550aae-2a53-47e3-8f5e-2399c14ce329)

**Text**

  
Add text to your page. The Text tile is a text editing tool, to help you format text and create hyperlinks.

  
On your Welcome page, this is where you will find the Welcome message that shows on your website.

**Plus Icon**

  
Click on the Plus icon to add additional content elements to your website. A flyout menu will open, click on the content element to be added to the page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/4ca6cc9b-a706-4fc2-8c75-a5f35fcf2c26)

**Image Tile – Add a Photo**

  
Click the **Select Image** button. Select an image from the gallery or upload an image to use on the webpage.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1b6be17d-eae0-4b1d-bf11-9404bd6bcdfb)

To select an image from the gallery, click on the image, then click the **Insert Into Page** button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3ff7ca26-dea8-4151-bf24-d4780e9438d0)

To upload an image, drag the image to the **Add a Photo** box or click the **Add a Photo** box, and navigate to the image on your computer. The image will show in a cropping tool. Drag the blue lines to crop the image as needed or click **Skip Resizing** to upload the full image, then click **Save**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9f7f1182-7539-4bdb-ada4-75ac16a89e28)

The image will show in the gallery, click on the image you would like to add to the page, then click the **Insert Into Page** button. The photo will be added to your web page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/364c414f-a128-4309-ac3f-159f83eb51e8)

  
**Embed Video**

  
Add a video to your website. Videos must be hosted on YouTube or Vimeo.

  
In the URL box, enter the URL of the Video. The URL of the video can be found in the browser address line on the website where the video is hosted. Alternately, go to Share on the video host site to find the URL.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d0c48551-d213-486c-81d1-d9cc7b906875)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ce5cf638-6099-4540-939a-c22b5627c7c4)

**Add Testimonials**

  
Testimonials can be added to your site with the **Testimonials tile**. In the Testimonials tile, enter the name of the person who gave you the testimonial and type their testimonial in the text box.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0bceff3a-7e32-437d-8158-e0548875557d)

**Add a Listing Carousel**

  
Display listings in a carousel format on your website.

  
**Note:** You will need to create a jump search before adding a listing carousel to the page.

  
See “Creating a Jump Search” below.

  
Give the carousel a Title. This will show on the page, above the carousel. In the Jump Search ID box, select the jump search from the dropdown.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d90550e1-3780-4449-b041-2c690467c6e2)

**Create a Jump Search**

  
A jump search is a property search where you define the search criteria. An example of a jump search may be Open House listings or New to Market listings.

  
To create a jump search, click on **Jump Searches** at the bottom of the left navigation.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a3c2b912-8674-41f0-80b7-9f652dcdea0f)

  
The Jump Search interface will open in a pop-up window.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/61c5d448-e18c-4f3c-96d5-d64755457f16)

Click on the **New Listing Jump Search** button. The property search will open. Enter the search name and add a browser title. Create the search criteria. Then click **Save Jump Search**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/280cabb7-7fc2-4e4f-8559-cb2959795733)

The newly created jump search will show in the list of jump searches. The jump searches will be available to select when creating a listing carousel and adding a link to your top navigation.

  
**Pro Tip:** Use the jump search URL in your social media to drive traffic to your website.
