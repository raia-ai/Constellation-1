# Paradym: Reconnecting LinkedIn to Paradym

**RECONNECTING LINKEDIN TO PARADYM**

**Step 1:** Login into your Paradym account at my.paradym.com, Once in, click your name in the upper right corner area of the browser screen. Choose Settings from the drop-down.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1287167e-2d82-4d97-96d4-9b62ba943526.png)

**Step 2:** On the Settings page, look to the left and downward for a link called Connections.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/70628b52-9017-4075-bb18-89f290766810.png)

**Step 3:** Click on the gear-shaped icon to the right of Linkedin.  Notice the ! symbol over-top of it.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c4a5b2be-be83-4bfc-bfd3-83625bf21a92.png)

Step 4: Click the word Reconnect on the left side of the screen.  Notice the text below that reads, “Your access token has expired.  Please reconnect.”

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/32396fe8-52f1-4da4-8030-3073a078070b.png)

**Step 5:** Select LinkedIn Page if you have created a business page through LinkedIn.  Otherwise, simply select LinkedIn Profile.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/cfe10cf0-e3e3-4d4f-bdb5-264bc58e90df.png)

**Step 6:** You’ll be directed to Linkedin and may need to sign in. You will be directed back to the Tour Sharing screen in Paradym you were on previously, meaning the reconnection was successful, **as long as the (!) is now gone.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/56632870-7507-4794-acca-c07f1531dc7f.png)
