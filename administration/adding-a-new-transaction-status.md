# Adding a new Transaction Status

*   Log in to your Transactions Site Admin account.
*   Under "Site Settings", click on "Transaction Status".

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f3279e4e-be00-4ba8-bbbb-67961d7b54d2)

*   On the following page, Fill in the new "Transaction Status" and "Transaction Description", and click on "Add" to add the new Transaction Status
