# How to Delete an Agent Account

The below article reviews how to delete an agent account. 

  Step 1: Log into your company Paradym account  

Log into your company Paradym account with your broker or admin credentials. Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Account Management.” 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image1.png)

Step 2: Find and Select the agent profile you wish to delete.  Once you have selected the agent, use the drop-down menu option at the top right using the "Select an Action" method. Select "Delete Agent."  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image6.png)

Step 3: You will be asked if you'd like to transfer any tours to an active agent account before deleting permanently. (Hint: will the brokerage keep any remaining active listing tours that you will need to transfer to someone else before deleting the agent account). To continue, you can follow the process provided and simply choose the agent’s account you’d like the tours transferred too.  

**Keep in mind, once an agent’s profile has been deleted, all of their tours and additional content will be** **_permanently_** **deleted from the tour list.**
