# Reconnecting Your Social Media | Constellation1 Customer Hub

How To Reconnect Your Social Media 

**Got an Expired Token?**   
**No Problem!**

First, sign in to your Paradym Account  
Click on your name in the upper right and choose Settings

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB_Reconnect_SM/1.jpg)

 ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB_Reconnect_SM/2.jpg)

Look on the bottom left hand column > Hit Connections  
Click on the gear with a red exclamation point on it

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/ac366c25-a515-40f5-ba46-76125f3f56fd.png)

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB_Reconnect_SM/4.jpg)

Hit Reconnect - Located below your name on the left

Follow any prompts it may provide - Such as Logging In and Allowing Permissions

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB_Reconnect_SM/5.jpg)

Once finished, you will review your social media connection options  

Afterward - Hit Save!

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB_Reconnect_SM/7.jpg)
