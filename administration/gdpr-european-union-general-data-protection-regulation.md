# GDPR (European Union General Data Protection Regulation)

If your organization receives a request to delete a customer as part of GDPR (European Union General Data Protection Regulation), admins can remove the customer record in the website admin by going to the client detail page and selecting "remove".

To remove the record for rDesk CRM, please open a maintenance ticket with the customer name and email and your Account Manager or Enterprise Solutions member will handle shortly.
