# How to Transfer Tours | Constellation1 Customer Hub

The below article reviews how to Transfer a Tour from one agent account to another.&#x20;

&#x20;Step 1: Log into your company Paradym account

Log into your company Paradym account with your broker or admin credentials. Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Tours.”&#x20;

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image7.png)

Step 2:  Find and locate in the upper right corner the icon that looks like two arrows in opposite directions.

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image8.png)

Step 3: You will be prompted to choose which agent account you’re transferring tours from. &#x20;

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image9.png)

Step 4: Select the tours you wish to transfer, then select “continue.” &#x20;

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image10.png)

Step 5: Select the target agent account you want the tours you selected transferred to. Select continue. &#x20;

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-AddingRemovingAgentsAndTransferTours/image11.png)

Step 6: You will have a notification that states “Transfer tours now?” Select “yes,” or “ok.” &#x20;

Finished
