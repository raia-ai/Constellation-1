# Printing Your Invoice | Constellation1 Customer Hub

**Printing Your Invoice** 

First, click on your name in the upper right   
Choose Settings 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/665732d4-1ac8-49a5-9811-f928ae6ec0b1.png)

Inside Settings, click on Billing Information on the left

 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/ce7672fd-0a86-4b4e-8c18-83772cfacd11.png)

Once inside of Billing Information, it looks as follows: 

  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b9e8ee94-4ba8-4d8a-9f10-5bee14345e8a.png)

Down on the bottom left, hit **VIEW** next to History.   
Another window will pop up or open, as seen below: 

You can filter it as needed, by using Type, From, and To Dates.    
There are multiple ways to print.    
You can right click on the screen and choose Print or click on the three dot menu and choose   
Print Screen. You can also take a screenshot and print it to use the snipping tool to print it.
