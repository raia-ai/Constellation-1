
# Paradym: Reconnecting YouTube to Paradym

**RECONNECTING YOUTUBE TO PARADYM**

Step 1: Login into your Paradym account at my.paradym.com, Once in, click your name in the upper right corner area of the browser screen. Choose Settings from the drop-down.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18562524)

Step 2: On the Settings page, look to the left and downward for a link called Connections.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18562525)

Step 3: Click on the gear-shaped icon to the right of YouTube.  Notice the ! symbol over it.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18562526)

Step 4: Click the word Reconnect on the left side of the screen.  Notice the text below that reads, “Your access token has expired.  Please reconnect.”

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/18562527)

Step 5: You’ll be directed to YouTube and may need to sign in. You will be directed back to the Tour Sharing screen in Paradym you were on previously, meaning the reconnection was successful, **as long as the (!) is now gone.**
