# How to Assign a User to a Group

eSign offers you the option to Group accounts together. Here is how you can assign a User to a Group in eSign**.**

1.  Login into your eSign AdminÂ account.
2.  Navigate to the top right and click on your profile icon, and Select Administration or Group Tools (depending on the type of your Admin account).
    1.  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d9e6c229-3613-4a7e-8034-3b791d042139.png)
3.  From the navigation panel on the left, click on Users . On the search bar you can look up your User by entering a Name, Company, Email, or User Type to look up the User information. Once you have the user information, click on the three dots under the Actions column.
    1.  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/3d253b0e-aa26-4f37-96e5-57b1f3502ac5.png)
4.  Click Assign Group, choose your Group from the drop-down list, once assigned click the Update button.

If you need any assistance in assigning Users to a Group, please reach out to the support team at esign@constellation1.com.
