# Website News And Advice Articles

**rDesk Website News Articles**

rDesk website News Articles is an easy and friendly way to feature news and advice articles in a professional looking blog format. The blog format will also allow you to take advantage of the SEO power of a blog site. This is a blog within your website. The news articles feature is responsive and fully optimized for search engines. Editing and managing news articles has never been easier.

This step by step guide will help you with updating and personalize your website.

**Logging In**

Login to your website admin as a company admin.

      
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c6ca76a8-2ee4-4298-9b4d-e1b5d3606643.png)

On the left nav, click on ‘**Content**’ In the sub-nav you will find ‘News Article Categories’, ‘News Article Landing’ and ‘News Articles’.

   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/722c6a53-fe45-4500-a248-ee6195b9d2fb.png) 

**Description of Available Options**

**News Article Landing** – Think of the Landing page as the homepage for your news articles.  
You can feature specific articles on this page. This page will also show the most recent articles published. Categories are also displayed for easy access to other articles and is good for SEO.

**News Article Categories** – News can be divided up by Categories, such as Information for Buyers, Sellers, Moving Tips, etc.  
You can create different categories, then associate an article to one or more of the categories.

**News Articles** – Is just what it says. In this area, you will see a list of articles that have been created. You can create new articles and update existing articles.

**News Article Landing page**

The landing page is the Homepage for your news and advice articles. Published articles will show on the landing page. Any categories that you have created will be available on the landing page too. There’s also a search tool, allowing consumers to search for keywords in an article.

Using the News Article Landing page admin, you can customize the top section of the landing page. Feature specific articles and content. This will show above the published articles that show on the page.

   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c83b2886-d031-437c-b5aa-d4b68543164c.png)

                    _Pictured, News Article Landing page admin_

In the ‘**Heading**’ enter a name for your news articles. This name also becomes part of the URL for the landing page. https://\[CompanyWebsite\].com/\[HeadingName\]

In the **content editor**, you can enter your content in a design view or html view. Use the formatting tools to format the text and upload images. This shows in the top section of the landing page, just above the articles.

In the ‘**Article Extract**’ field, enter descriptive text about this page. Search engines will use this text to help catalog the article. This is good for SEO.

When you’re ready, click the ‘**publish**’ button to save and publish your news article homepage.

    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/70380218-d3d1-41ea-8676-c40b0e80e04e.png)

                          _Pictured, Public facing News and Advice landing page._

**News Article Categories**

Categories will help you organize your articles into logical groups. You can define the category names and the articles that will be associated to a category.

   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/35e46050-f355-445e-85b7-618a21c747c1.png)

On the **Article Category** page, you will see your list of available categories.

To add a new Category, click the ‘**Add New**’ button.

In the popup, enter the name of the category and the sequence number.  
Check the ‘**Is Visible in Search**’ box to feature the articles in this category on the Landing Page. 

   ![6.jpg](https://support.realestatedigital.com/hc/article_attachments/360040282253/6.jpg)

**News Articles**

Create and manage all news and advice articles right here. On this page you will see a list of the articles that have been created. You can edit existing articles or create new ones.

In the ‘Name’ field, you have the options to ‘**Edit**’ or ‘**Delete**’ an article.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4093fdc5-bac7-4059-bc84-22adb93e70c9.png) 

To add a new article, click on the “**Add New**” button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4b05536e-3188-46c2-8196-79b36ca50f6e.png)

At the top of the page, give your article a **Heading**. This is the title of your article and will show on the landing page.

Give your article a **URL**. The URL will use your company website as the domain. The public facing URL will look something like; https://CompanyWebsite.com/pages/\[**NewsArticle-URL**\]**.**

**Post Date** – gives you the option to set the date when this article will post to your site. Create an article in spring time, and have it post on a specific date in summer.

In the content editor, you can enter your content in a design view and/or html view. Use the formatting tools to format the text and upload images.

**_Pro Tip:_** _If you have an html editor that you are more comfortable using, you can use that tool to create the content. Then just copy the html from your editor and paste it into the html view in your news article. You will still need to upload any images to the article._

**Article Extract** – Enter a description of the article. This will show on the landing page as an overview or description of what this article is about. Search engines will also use this to help them catalog the article for the search engines.

**Photo** – Add a cover photo to your article. In the photo section on the right side, enter the URL to link the photo to the article.

**Photo URL** – There are two ways to upload an image to use in your article.

1.  You can upload the image to the article in the editor tool. Once the photo has been uploaded and shows in the library of images, click the thumbnail to view the photo in the preview window. In the preview window, click the ‘Properties’ button.  
    In the ‘Choose Image’ field, copy the URL.  
    Then paste the URL into the ‘Photo URL’ field on the right side.
2.   You can also upload an image to the content files. Then copy the URL from there and paste it into the ‘Photo URL’ field._  
    See below for instructions to upload images to the content files and copy the URL._

**_Pro Tip:_** _Before uploading a photo, give it a meaningful name that relates to the article. Search engines will see the name of the photos._

**Caption** – Give your photo a caption. This will not show in the article however, search engines will see this.

**Categories** – In the drop down, select the primary category. In the check boxes below, add secondary categories where this article will show.

You have the option to ‘**Save as Draft**’ or ‘**Publish**’, both will save your changes.

## Upload images to content files

Uploading images and other content to the Content Files module is fast and easy.  
To upload an image (or other content file such as a work doc or pdf);  
On the left nav, click on ‘**Content**’ then ‘**Content Files**’

     ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/31b5ab09-2828-4f98-b729-712e13c54916.png)

On the right side of the page, click on ‘**Add a New Content File**’

     ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c50a24aa-22e6-4b2e-831c-702f0ad5f0ff.png)

On the ‘Content File Edit’ page, enter a description of the file you are uploading. The description does not show on the file or on the website. This is for your information to help you manage the content files.

Click the ‘**Browse**’ button and navigate to and select the image you would like to upload.

The name of the file will show next to the Browse button.

Click the ‘**Submit**’ to upload the image to the system.

You will notice a new content file is now showing in your list.

    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/bd76b2fa-0f3f-4ae4-9f62-8952db02c586.png)

In the ‘Actions’ column, click on ‘**Edit**’

     ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/29f4504b-5d1d-4d72-9ce1-d922d029bc21.png)

On the Content File Edit page, copy the URL and paste it into the Photo URL’ field on your News Article. The image will link to your article.

    ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e78dac7d-1bef-4b97-b48f-d3a6a1a7c62d.png)

**The News and Advice** module will help you put relevant and meaningful content on your website in a blog format. This will help make your website more powerful with consumers and the search engines.
