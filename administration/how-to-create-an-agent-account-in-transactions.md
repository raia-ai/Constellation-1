# How to create an Agent Account in Transactions

*   Log in to your Transactions Admin account.
*   Under the "Organizations" tab, locate the Main office (it will be a "Realty" Type office) and click on "Branch".![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2d3944ee-24b1-44b4-a998-76aa0f22f178)

*   Under the "Branch Office List", locate the office where the Agent account needs to be created, and click on the corresponding "Staff" option.

        ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2b02549d-ee6c-43ad-a8ea-1f91370ad285)

*   Agent accounts can only be created under a Transaction Coordinator's account, so select the Transaction Coordinator that you want to assign to that Agent, and click on it's respective "Add Agent" option.![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f4ebd24b-c66d-4563-a8be-ca54d184dbb0)

*   On the following page, fill in the fields highlighted in red, and click on "Finished" at the bottom of the screen.![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/093dfcc3-684f-4c7f-bdd5-869d0783931f)

*   An Agent account will be created and an email will be sent to the Agent on the designated email address, containing their log in credentials.
