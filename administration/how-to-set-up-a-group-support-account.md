
# How to set up a Group Support Account

Site Admins can create Group Support accounts to manage individual or multiple Groups. They have the same level of access as a Site Amin account, but limited to the Group they are assigned to. Here is how you can create a Group Support account in eSign.

**Note: This feature is only available to eSign Enterprise Clients**

1.  Login into your eSign Admin account.
2.  Navigate to the top right and click on your profile icon, and Select Administration or Group Tools (depending on the type of your Admin account).
    1.  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/d9e6c229-3613-4a7e-8034-3b791d042139.png)
3.  From the navigation panel on the left, click on Groups then click on the Add button.
    1.  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/af973e37-c2a3-4400-b6bb-968785369fac.png)
4.  Enter all the information to create the Group (Group Name, Customer Name, Address line 1, Customer support phone number, Customer email address are mandatory). Once all the information is complete, make sure that the Make Active? toggle is set to Yes and click on the Save button.
    1.  ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4cb7c603-653e-483a-baf7-deeaf62bc4f2.png)

Your Group has now been created and you can start assigning Users to it!
