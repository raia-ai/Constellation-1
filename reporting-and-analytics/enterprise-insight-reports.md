# Enterprise Insight-Reports | Constellation1 Customer Hub

Currently within Enterprise Insights we have several reports available for your use to aid you in analyzing your data

To access, from the left navigation bar, select **Reports** and the following options will appear

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3be11b11-a22a-4e70-bb7e-0e68a84b98f8)

**All Transactions**

Filters can be applied to aid in analysis by selecting/multi-selecting if applicable:

*   Groups
*   States/Provinces
*   Cities
*   Brokerages
*   Offices
*   Agents
*   Transaction Status
*   Basis Date-Close Date, Offer Acceptance Date or Process Date
*   Report Range-Last 3/6//12 Months, Month to Date, Year to Date or Custom Date Range

A preview of a maximum of 15 records will display in the bottom of the screen to give you a quick snapshot of data

By Selecting **Save**, it will retain your settings for the next time this report is generated.

Report can then be exported in CSV or PDF format depending on how you wish to use the data
