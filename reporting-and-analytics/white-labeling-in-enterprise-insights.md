# White Labeling in Enterprise Insights

Enterprise Insights users may have the option for White Labeling which will be displayed on the left navigation bar.  This functionality allows each Company to customize their logo, email template, email address and color/colour. &#x20;

If you would like to add Enterprise Insights with White Labeling to your account, please email [commissions@constellation1.com](mailto:commissions@constellation1.com) and a member of our Support Team will be happy to assist.

From the left navigation bar, select **White Labeling**

**Custom Branding card**

Allows users to upload a logo and/or favicon, set left navigation bar color/colour and use a customized URL vs the default of [https://commissions.constellation1.com/.](https://commissions.constellation1.com/)

The Click to upload custom logo option, select the camera icon and navigate to an appropriately sized PNG file.&#x20;

The Upload custom favicon for Browser option, you can Drag file or Click to select the appropriately sized PNG or ICO file. &#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/55de343e-0bb2-4581-99bf-884a36727fe8)

Once the logo and/or favicon files have been uploaded, a preview will be displayed. If satisfied, click the check mark to right of the preview to accept the new logo/favicon and select **Save.**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/95ed293b-d579-463f-a6d9-4dc8e584c05f)

If you decide that you do not want to upload your own logo, select **Clear Logo** and the default Constellation1 logo will be used.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/041ab907-6bb4-422c-ae71-e077ea8390bb)

The Select custom color for the left menu option, click on the checkered box to select from the color/colour wheel and **Save.** To revert to the default color/colour, select the Reset to default color icon and **Save.**

The Custom URL for Commissions option, please email [commissions@constellation1.com](mailto:commissions@constellation1.com) and a member of our Support Team will be happy to assist in getting this configured for you.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/df9d924d-37a2-40b9-8baa-f79db79879ee)

**Email Template card**

If you have opted to customize branding as detailed above, the logo will display at the top of all emailed templates such as invitations, statement delivery, scheduled reports, e-signature signing sessions, etc.&#x20;

The information listed in the footer of the template can be customized and will be used for user/agent invitations.&#x20;

Once edits are completed, select **Save.**![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/35ade3ec-bd5c-4e64-b709-e80da0f58b68)

**Custom Email Address card**

Allows users to configure a specific email address and domain that will be used vs the Constellation1 defaults provided. For more information, please email [commissions@constellation1.com](mailto:commissions@constellation1.com) and a member of our Support Team will be happy to assist.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/06bb9b1d-86d6-4157-b552-2b1d35066fd0)

**Brokerage Override card**

Allows Master Users of each Enterprise Insights company the ability to customize their logo, email template, system email address and color/colour on left menu bar if enabled.&#x20;

Make your selections by toggling ON each option as needed.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a995d4d3-29ca-4504-92ac-6271251fc685)

When Brokerage Overrides are enabled in Enterprise Insights, the Master User will log into the appropriate Commissions Online company.

From the left navigation bar, select **System Settings / Company**.

Expand each card and configure as needed.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/86aaf8d4-ed80-4678-957a-24564fc9bb52)
