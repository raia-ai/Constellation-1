
# How to Check Company Account Invoices

The article below reviews how to check your CMA company account invoices. 

Step 1: Log-into your company Paradym account by going to www.Paradym.com, selecting "My Account,"  and inserting your log-in credentials. Note, you should automatically see your Dashboard menu of options. In you are not in your Dashboard settings, locate the broker/owner's name at the top right, select the down arrow for a menu of options, and select "Dashboard."

Step 2:  Next, Select “Company Charges” on your dashboard left-hand panel of options.  

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/20a9fea7-c786-4598-8f0b-39e0019b0e37.png)

Step 3: You will be asked for your broker password again for confirmation.  

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/8f2b4a5e-ff24-42e0-872c-2160f289bf95.png)

Step 4: Select the invoice date in which you wish to view. Select your type as well. Select "Go" on the right.

 ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/5b411887-a498-4942-a52b-29a127da63f0.png)

Finished!
