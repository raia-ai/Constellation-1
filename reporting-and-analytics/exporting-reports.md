# Exporting Reports | Constellation1 Customer Hub

This article highlights how to generate and export reports and from C1 Commissions.

*   Generate a Preview of the required report by selecting the preview icon:

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ExportingReport1/exportingreport1.png)

*    From left navigation select **Export** and select the desired output file type: 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ExportingReport1/exportingreport2.png)

*   The file will be saved to the location selected to view and edit. 
*   If you are on our hosted environment, we suggest saving to the desktop and then on the desktop you can right click to copy and paste on your own desktop for use in your files or to email.
