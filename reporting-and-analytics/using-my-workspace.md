# Using My Workspace | Constellation1 Customer Hub

Commissions Online has a feature referred to as **My Workspace** which is a high level overview of key reminders and items of importance, similar to the **Start of Day report** from Commissions Desktop.

Note: if your My Workspace icon looks like this ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6da5735d-1757-4523-bc8f-079eb48c1628), it is indicating that you have action items such as upcoming closings in the next seven days or overdue or possibly tasks/checklists that require your attention

When you access the **My Workspace** icon in the top right hand corner of the screen ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/089cb862-fefa-4f38-bdd1-fb5a931a088f) you will see a page appear similar to the following

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2bf5f3a8-1ba8-461c-a558-0b08bf3aac62)

The following sections are expandable:

**Upcoming Closings**

Once expanded this section will display:

* Number of transactions Pending TODAY, Next Seven Days and Overdue.
* Upcoming and overdue closings can be filtered based on ALL, 7 Days or 30 days.
* Once filtered, list will display Address, Side, Agent and Days Until Close.

By selecting an item from the displayed list, it will automatically take you out to the transaction selected.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/83cae28d-6de2-45a6-ac6b-f45d016e7b3b)

Should you wish the system to generate and email a report of upcoming closings to specified users, select the following icon

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/28837699-10cd-4d0f-8a1c-468b8aa37667)

Enter the EXACT number of days before closing you wish the report to be based on, enter the email recipient(s), the time and Time Zone you wish the report to and select Save.  The report will automatically be generated and sent to the requested party(ies) based on your selection

Should you wish to pause the email notification for any reason, select **Pause Email Notification**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/aae52074-cac1-4901-8248-c0c78af896af)

**Submitted Approvals**

* Should your office use Tasks or Checklists on your transactions, once expanded this section will display any outstanding items submitted for approval
* By selecting the address from the list, it will navigate you directly to the transaction at which point you can navigate to the Checklists tab and address any outstanding items

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a4e9d108-b4b9-4d98-955c-a6270650722e)

**Conditions Due**

Should your office track conditions on transactions, once expanded this section will display:

* Number of Conditions Due TODAY, Next Seven Days and Overdue.
* Upcoming and overdue conditions can be filtered based on ALL, 7 Days or 30 days.
* Once filtered, list will display Address, Name of Condition, Description and Days Until Due.

By selecting an item from the displayed list, it will automatically take you out to the transaction selected.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/64b67402-b0c2-4c63-b46e-c858c301dcd8)

**Agent License Expiration**

Should your office track your Agent License expiration dates in their Agent Profile, once expanded this section will display:

* Number of Agent Licenses expiring TODAY, Next Seven Days and Overdue.
* Upcoming and overdue Agent Licenses can be filtered based on ALL, 7 Days or 30 days.
* Once filtered, list will display Agent Name, License Number and Days Until Expiration.

By selecting an agent from the displayed list, it will automatically take you out to their Agent Profile.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f013a159-9198-4f39-9e80-d4a2571f222c)

**Notes Reminders**

Should your office use Notes on your Agent Profiles or Notes on Transactions, once expanded this section will display:

* Number of Notes by date based on TODAY, Next Seven Days and Overdue.
* Upcoming and overdue Agent and/or Transaction Notes can be filtered based on ALL, 7 Days or 30 days.
* Once filtered, list will display Note Type, Description, Importance, Note Text and # of days until Reminder.

By selecting an item from the displayed list, it will automatically take you out to the transaction or profile of the agent selected.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d495be65-2158-4df7-95f7-1783e00540c4)
