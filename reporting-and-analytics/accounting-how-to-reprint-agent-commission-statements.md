# Accounting - How to Reprint Agent Commission Statements

The purpose of this article is to provide steps for reprinting an agent's Commission Statement.

To reprint an agent's Commission Statement, navigate to **Sales > Reports > Commission Statements**.

*   Highlight the **Agent** line item, with the applicable **Check/ACH** # 
*   Click **Process Report** button
