
# Accounting - How to reprint 2021 1099-NEC forms

The purpose of this article is to show how to reprint 1099-NEC forms for 2021.

To reprint 1099-NEC forms for 2021, navigate to **Agents>>Processing>>1099s**.

*   Select **2021** from the **Tax Year** dropdown.  

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17864434)

*   Navigate to the **Reporting** tab.
*   Select **Official 1099s** from the **Report** dropdown.
*   Make certain **1099-NEC** is the form selected.  
*   Click **Process Report**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17864435)

*   Print on official 1099-NEC forms. 
    *   You may want to print one page to plain paper, to make certain the values print in each box correctly.  
    *   If you need to adjust the placement of the values in any of the boxes, navigate to the **1099-NEC Printing Layout** tab to make adjustments.
