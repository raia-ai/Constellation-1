# Build Customized Report | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   In order to generate a customized report having selective columns click on the “Clients” page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c2055b18-7318-482b-a424-56c943b0cac7)

*   Select a date range from initial to final date for fetching “Referral records” from the database. Then click on “Build Report” button to make a customized report as per your requirement.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/effac36b-c980-45ed-a048-3d7a7dd67b60)

*   On “Build Report” page wait for some time while data gets loaded and updated from the database.
    
    **Note:** The record gets available once “Total Records” and “Filters” row is displayed on the screen. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0a0e39c0-2d37-452a-9662-8c12e6cbde06)

*   Click on radio button and type the name of the report and then click on fields for selcting the required columns.

     ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/31392f43-ca7c-4404-9c6b-1ad198c55503)

*   A Report field table will be displayed. Select the required columns/fields by clicking on the tabs, once selected close this table.
    
    **Note:** The tab highlighted in blue color are the selected columns/fields.
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/80be218e-582e-4a8d-8c5a-bd8143ec9ea1)

*   Click on “Save” button to save this report for any future prospect. Then click on “Generate Report” button to view the customized report having your required columns/fields.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5b8c392f-4ec2-4fda-b633-f43fa458dba1)

*   This customized report can also be downloaded in CSV or PDF format by clicking on “Export CSV” and “Export PDF” button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/7e48859f-36da-424d-a509-0fa13b79dbae)
