# How to Post to Instagram from Paradym

The below article reviews how to post to Instagram from Paradym.

Step 1: Go to www.Paradym.com and Click on "My Account" to get signed into your Paradym account. 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage1.png)

Step 2:  Sign into your Paradym account using your Log-In credentials: username and password. Click Sign In  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage2.png)

Step 3: Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Settings.”  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage11.png)

Step 4: In Order to Post to Instagram, you will first need to make sure your other Social Media Connections are connected and set up (Very Important - Please make sure at least your Facebook account is connected before moving forward). You will see a green check mark beside each social icon if you are connected. If you have not set up your Social Connections yet, please go here to learn how and get started:  https://www.paradym.com/blog/resources/social-automation-manager-basics/?portfolioCats=73. Once complete, you may continue to the next step. 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage12.png)

Step 5: Next, Utilize the drop-down arrow located in the upper-right corner and navigate to “Dashboard.”

   ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage3.png)

Step 6: Select "Social Schedule" on the Column on the left.   

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage4.png)

Step 7: Next, hover over a Facebook icon post and select “Share to Instagram.”  

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage5.png)

Step 8: It will prompt you to enter your cell phone number in "phone tab." Once complete, click "send"

   ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage6.png)

 Step 9:  You will now want to check your phone. You should have received a text message on your phone. Please check and open the message by tapping on the link. (Android users will more than likely need to first Select Browser to open link).

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage7.png) 

Step 10:  Follow the Clearly stated instructions to post to Instagram.

  ![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage8.png)

Next - Look below for (2. Create Your Caption)

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage9.png)

Next - Look below for (3. Create Your Post) 

![](https://app.na3.teamsupport.com/Wiki/WikiDocs/2472652/images/KB-ParadymArticles/KB-HowToPostToInstagramFromParadym/HTPTIFPimage10.png)

Step 11: Once you have switched over to Instagram and shared your post you are finished!
