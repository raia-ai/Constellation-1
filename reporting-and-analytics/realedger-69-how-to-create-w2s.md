# REALedger 6.9 - How to create W2's

The purpose of this article is to show how to print W2s in REALedger 6.9.

Prior to printing W2s, you must first close the calendar year.  Then follow the steps below.  Note:  The screenshots referenced in this article were created using Calendar Year 2022 as an example.  Be sure to adjust the dates you input, to reflect the relevant calendar year.   

To print W2s, navigate to **Payroll>>All>>W-2s**.  The **Enter Yearend Date** field will default to the calendar year just closed.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17685783)

Click **Print Pre-W2 Report**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17685784)

Review **Pre-W2 Report** for accuracy.  

Click **Print W2's**.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/17685785)

A preview of the W-2 data will appear in report view, with two employee W-2s per page. 

*   Insert Copy A of the W-2 forms into printer and print.
*   Insert Copy B of the W-2 forms into printer and print.
*   Insert Copy C of the W-2 forms into printer and print.
*   Insert Copy D/1 of the W-2 forms into printer and print.
