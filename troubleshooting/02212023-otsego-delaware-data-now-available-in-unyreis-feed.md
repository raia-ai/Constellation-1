# 02/21/2023 - Otsego-Delaware data now available in UNYREIS feed

Please note that as of Feb 21, 2023, the listings from Otsego-Delaware is joining Upstate New York Real Estate Information Services, LLC (Rochester).

Existing listings will show up with MLS number prefixed with “OD” followed by 7 digits. New listings added starting on February 28, 2023, will be prefixed with “R” (i.e., R1234567).

Agent IDs are prefixed with an "OD" (i.e., UserID “1234” as “OD1234”). Office IDs are now prefixed with an "OD" from (i.e., Office “ABC” as “ODABC”).
