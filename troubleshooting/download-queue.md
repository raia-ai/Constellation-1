# Download Queue | Constellation1 Customer Hub

The Download Queue in Documents is a feature designed to manage the downloading of multiple documents efficiently. It is particularly useful when dealing with a large number of documents, ensuring that the system remains responsive and that users can continue working while the documents are being processed.

*   **Key Features:**  
    *   **Batch Processing:** Allows users to download multiple documents at once.
    *   **Queue Management:** Documents are processed in the background, freeing up the user to perform other tasks.
    *   **Notification:** Users are notified when the document package is ready for download.

*   *   **Tips and Best Practices:**
        *   **Limit the Number of Documents:** For optimal performance, it is recommended to limit the number of documents sent to the queue to no more than 25.
        *   **Naming Conventions:** Use clear and descriptive names for your document packages to easily identify them later.
        *   **Check Document Sizes:** Ensure that the total size of the documents does not exceed system limits to avoid errors.
        *   **Missing Download Link:** If the download link does not appear, refresh the page or check back after a few minutes.
        *   **Error Messages:** If you encounter an error message, verify that the total size of the documents does not exceed the allowed limit and that all documents are correctly selected.
