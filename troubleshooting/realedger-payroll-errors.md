# REALedger – Payroll Errors | Constellation1 Customer Hub

The purpose of this article is to provide step-by-step instructions on how to get past the errors when moving from the **Unpaid** view to the **Paid** view, from the **Edit Payroll Transactions** screen, in the **Payroll** module.

When moving from the **Unpaid** to **Paid** view …

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/756faefe-2426-48d7-98fc-c39566c8e3cd.png) 

…the following alert pops up:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/200fa4bd-5dcd-45a9-abab-278f8391aa73.png) 

When you click **OK** the following error pops up:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/9698e468-884e-4e07-a0a3-6520ec26f2e0.png) 

Click **OK** to the error pop-up.

Then click **Esc**, **Esc** on your keyboard.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/4e26d2cc-308f-4db7-92cb-b1593b3de60a.png) 

You can then click **Close** without error.

  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/e0261eb3-3a6a-4f49-bd65-691864a01890.png) 

To avoid this error, click **Edit Payroll Transactions…**

 **![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/23376e11-b6c9-451c-80d8-92ee91ec5e31.png)**

…then immediately click **Esc** twice on your keyboard…

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/df1cb966-362f-4ff5-a197-593e21f8eb8e.png) 

…then click **Undo**. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/53efadd2-e866-4434-ac08-8e86ffaa5185.png)  

Once you do this, you will be able to navigate to the **Paid** view without error.
