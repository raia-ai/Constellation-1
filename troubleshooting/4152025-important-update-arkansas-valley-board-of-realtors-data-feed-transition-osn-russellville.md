# 4/15/2025 - Important Update: Arkansas Valley Board of REALTORS Data Feed Transition - OSN Russellville

We are writing to inform you that the **Arkansas Valley Board of REALTORS** data feed (**OSN Russellville**) has been dissolved. According to our recent updates, the data previously available in this feed is now accessible through the **Northwest Arkansas Board of REALTORS** data feed (**OSN NorthwestAR**).

How to find Arkansas Valley Board of REALTORS data in Northwest Arkansas Board of REALTORS data feed:

*   Existing listings from Arkansas Valley Board of Realtors will show up **prefixed with “AV”** followed by 7 digits
    
*   Agent IDs (Agent.MLSID field) are prefixed with an "AV" from Arkansas Valley Board of Realtors to Matrix (i.e., UserID “1234” as “AV1234”)
    
*   Office IDs (Office.MLSID field) are now prefixed with an "AV" from Arkansas Valley Board of Realtors to Matrix (i.e., Office “ABC” as “AVABC”)
