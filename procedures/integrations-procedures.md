## Procedure 1: Broker Guide: How to Use Office Accounts

**Steps**:

1. In the top-right menu, go to **Account Management**.
2. Select the office you want to manage.
3. Choose **Select an Action → Send Credentials**.
4. Confirm the email address and click **Proceed**.
5. A **Welcome Email** and a **separate credentials email** with login information are sent automatically to the office manager. (You will not receive copies.)
1. In the top-right menu, go to **Account Management**.
2. Click the **\+ (plus)** button in the Offices section to add a new office.
3. Enter office details (title, address, contact info, etc.).
4. Upload the office logo if you have one. (You can also allow the office manager to upload it later, unless your Broker settings are set to **override** the logo - if so, your broker logo will be applied across all offices and agent accounts.)
5. _(Optional)_ Check **Send Welcome Email** and **Send Login Credentials** to immediately provide the office manager with access. If you prefer to wait, leave these unchecked and send credentials later as described above.
6. **Save** the office record.
1. In the top-right menu, go to **Account Management**.
2. Select the office.
3. Choose **Login As**.
4. You’ll enter the Office Account exactly as the office manager would see it.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28587458

## Procedure 5: RDesk CRM Contact Detail Page - User Guide

**Steps**:

1. In the upper left-hand header section of the Contact Details Page resides the “Text Avatar,” which will display the first initial and last initial of the primary Contact’s first and last name, respectively.
2. Directly underneath the contact’s name is the Contact’s primary phone number on file, which the user can tap on and select while accessing this page on a mobile device to call the contact
3. Underneath the primary phone number is the primary email address for the Contact, which can also be clicked while on desktop to open your default email client (e.g. Microsoft Outlook) or tapped on when on a mobile device to email the contact using your mobile device’s default email client (e.g. Gmail, Yahoo! Mail)
1. Whenever a customer contact is added to the “group” of “buyer” he/she will displayed to this top identifier section
2. Example: Sam Agent manually adds Joe Buyer to the “Buyer” group via the “Groups” module on this Contact Details page, which will then dynamically display the “Buying” icon
1. Hovering the “Unregistered” button will display quick instructions on how to register your contact should you desire ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/73290abd-05b8-43b8-b982-b90b9bae389e.png)
2. Selecting the “Unregistered” button hyperlink will pop a message overlay for you to confirm whether you would like to Register the contact
1. NOTE: the Unregistered button can appear for the contact Types of “Lead” or “Contact”![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dcda65d8-27e9-45c4-8762-ad50fc663aa0.png)
3. Selecting “Yes, Register” will ping our registration service, which will register your customer contact using your customer’s “primary” email address as their username - AND - a random, auto-generated password will be sent to your customer’s primary email address
1. When you register your customer your customer will turn into a Registered Client, and the respective buttons in the top-right bar will indicate that (as seen in below)
1. You are now able to save properties and searches for your customer as a result!![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/111d1369-4439-4974-8a1c-80759a8c1637.png)
1. Creating a To Do
2. Select a the “+ To Dos” link and a To Do modal will spawn, allowing you to select from various tasks (drop-down selection), selecting a date, start and end time, inputting a note, and a reminder option
3. Selecting a “Reminder” option (e.g. selecting “5 mins before”)

2. Click on “Settings” next to the “Sign out” button
3. In the “E-mail” box change the email address to match the email address associated with rDesk CRM account
4. Click on “Update” button to save the changes
