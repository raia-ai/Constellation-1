## Procedure 2: Top Producer 8i: Microsoft Edge Configuration for Top Producer Editor

**Steps**:

1. Open ![Edge-1](https://support.topproducer.com/wp-content/uploads/Edge-1.png) Microsoft Edge.
2. Click on the 3-dots in the upper right.
3. Click _Settings_.
4. From the Settings menu on the left side, click _Default browser_.
5. Under _Internet Explorer compatibility, set Allow sites to be reloaded in Internet Explorer mode (IE mode)_ to **Allow**.
6. Under _Internet Explorer mode pages_, click **Add a page**.
7. From the _Add a page_ box, type in [https://www.topproducer8i.com,](https://www.topproducer8i.com,/) then click **Add**.
8. Top Producer 8i ([https://www.topproducer8i.com](https://www.topproducer8i.com/)) will now open in Internet Explorer mode for 30 days from the date it was added. You will need to repeat steps 6 & 7 once the date under _Expires_ has passed, if you intend on editing any of the Flyer, Presentation, Envelope, Label, or Postcard templates again beyond that date.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28289388

## Procedure 3: Changing Your Top Producer CRM Password

**Steps**:

1. Go to [https://crm.topproducer.com](https://crm.topproducer.com/). If logged in, click **Sign Out** in the top right.
2. Click **Forgot Password**? on the login screen.
3. Enter your username and click **Send Password Link**.
4. A password reset email will be sent to your registered email address.
5. Click **RESET PASSWORD** in the password reset email.
6. Set your new password as prompted and click **Reset Password**.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28613094

## Procedure 4: We’re having a hard time verifying that you aren’t a robot error on login

**Steps**:

1. Log into Top Producer 8i at [https://www.topproducer8i.com/](https://www.topproducer8i.com/) or if you have the Top Producer X login page open, click the **Top Producer 8i** button at the top of the screen to open the 8i login page.
2. Once you are logged in to Top Producer 8i, click the **TPX** button from the main menu, this will open Top Producer X in a new tab/window.
3. Once you are in Top Producer X, hover over your agent photo in the upper right corner and click **Sign Out**.
4. Log back into Top Producer X.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28195899
