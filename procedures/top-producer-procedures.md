## Procedure 1: Set the Listings Page to Display Your MLS Listings

**Steps**:

1. Log in to Top Producer® Websites at [https://www.topproducerwebsite.com](https://www.topproducerwebsite.com/)
2. Click My Account
3. Click Third-Party Integrations
4. Under 'Enter your credentials to display your MLS listings' enter your Agent ID.
5. Under Board Name/City, click the drop list and select your MLS.
6. Click Save Integrations.
1. From the main menu click Edit Site, or choose Edit My Pages from the Quick Tools section of the main page. The Website Manager window opens.
2. Click Manage My Pages. The Manage My Pages window opens.
3. Hover over the Listings Page from the menu. Click the pen icon to edit the page. The Edit Page window opens.
4. In the Types of Listings section, select _MLS Property_ from the Select the type of listings to show dropdown menu. If you have not already integrated your MLS, you’ll be prompted to do so here (Step 1).
5. Click the Preview button if you’d like to preview the page in a new window.
6. Click Save Page to save and publish the page.

## Procedure 2: Adding IDX Pages to Your Top Producer Website

**Steps**:

1. Log in to Top Producer® Websites at [https://www.topproducerwebsite.com](https://www.topproducerwebsite.com/)
2. Click Edit My Site.
3. Click Manage My Pages.
4. Click Add Main Page.
5. Click 3rd Party IDX. The New IDX or New 3rd Party IDX screen appears.
6. Enter the Page Name, SEO Title, SEO Description, and SEO Keywords.
7. In the 3rd Party IDX Link section, paste your frameable IDX URL.
8. When done, click Preview to take a look, then click Save Page.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28543983

## Procedure 3: Performing a Label Mail Out

**Steps**:

1. Hover over the _Contacts_ icon in the Main Menu, and click **Search for Contacts**.
2. Perform a search using the criteria necessary to generate the list of contacts you wish to include in the label mail out.
3. In the _Search Results_ list, select the contacts you wish to include, or select them all by placing a check in the box at the top. At the bottom, click **Schedule Activity**.
4. At the top of the _Add Activity_ page, click the **Type** drop-down list and select **Label**.
5. In the _Activity Details_ section, enter a **Description** of the activity. If you wish to schedule this activity for a later date, enter the date in the field. Otherwise, leave it as today’s date.
6. The _Contacts_ section will include the contacts you selected from the search results. If you would like to include additional contacts, locate them in the list on the right and drag them into the Contacts section on the left, or click **Search & Attach Multiple Contacts** to perform an additional search.
7. In the _Label Template_ section, select the label template you wish to use by choosing the **Category** and the **Template**. You can also choose to sort the labels in a specific order.
8. Once the above is completed, if you wish to schedule this activity for later, click **Add Activity**. If you would like to print the labels now, click **Print Preview** at the bottom of the page.
9. In the _Print Labels_ popup, choose the starting position, which allows you to re-use a label sheet that already has some labels missing. The default is to start printing at the first label on the sheet. If you wish to print more than one label per contact, select the desired number from the Copies Per Contact list. Click **Print Preview**.
10. The labels will be displayed in a new window or tab. Use the PDF Toolbar to print the labels.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/27849059

## Procedure 4: Using Your Domain Name With Top Producer Websites

**Steps**:

1. Log into Godaddy at [https://www.godaddy.com/](https://www.godaddy.com/) and click Sign in (top right) or to go [https://sso.godaddy.com/](https://sso.godaddy.com/)
2. Click My Account in the upper left area, then click Domains.
3. From the Domain Portfolio, click on the domain name used for TWS
4. Click the DNS tab to access the DNS settings.
5. From the DNS/Advanced DNS section, we will need to add a new A records, CNAMEs and TXT files. Click Add New Record button, we’ll start by adding the A Records which includes 3 sets of IP addresses:
6. Click Save.
7. Click Add New Record again, this time we are adding the CNAME type record.
8. Click Save All Records.
9. Look for a CNAME with the name www. It will likely exist already and have the Value using the domain name.
10. Click Save.
11. Look for a TXT record type that has a Value that begins with v=spf1 include
12. Click Save.
13. This concludes the DNS changes needed for the domain in Godaddy or domain registrar control panel. You can now log out of the domain registrar control panel.
14. Once these steps have been completed, please contact Top Producer Support and advise them your domain name is now pointing to the Top Producer Website servers, as there is an additional step that needs to be done to complete the domain setup. Your domain name will then start to display your Top Producer Website within 24-48 hours.
