## Procedure 1: Sending a Market Snapshot Report

**Steps**:

1. Click the **Compose** button and select Send Snapshot, or click the **Send Snapshot** button in the _Snapshots_ dashboard or the _Snapshot Reports_ tab of a contact record.
2. Enter _Contact details, Address, Buyer preferences_, and choose the _Snapshot settings_.
3. Click **Send Market Snapshot**.

## Procedure 2: Top Producer X CRM: Creating and Sending Email Templates

**Steps**:

1. Go to **Marketing** > **Template Library** > **Email**. You will see a list of all available email templates, organized by category. Click on a template to view the contents.
2. If you would like to start with a blank email template, click **Add from Scratch**.
3. Enter a name for your template, select a template category, enter an email subject, and the body of the email.
4. To enter a merge code, such as customer name, click the **Template** button tpxtemplatebutton. Search in the template list for the merge code you’d like to add, and click to add it to the email.
5. Once you’re done editing your template, click **Save Template**.
1. Click the **Compose** button compose and select **Send Email** sendemail1, or click the Compose Email button in the Email tab of a contact record.
2. Click the **Template** button to view merge codes and email templates. Your personal templates will appear at the top. Click the one you wish to apply.
3. The template will be automatically applied to the email, and any merge codes will be auto-populated.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28410633

## Procedure 3: Top Producer X CRM: Creating a Task Plan

**Steps**:

1. In the Task Plan Manager, click **Add Plan**.
2. Choose the type of plan (contact/transaction), enter a name and description, and click **Save Plan**.
3. Click **Add Task** to begin adding tasks to the plan.
4. On the _Add Task_ pop-up, set the task type, details, additional information, and choose the team member to which the task should be assigned. If creating an email task, be sure to select an email template to send.
5. Once you’ve finished setting up the task, click Save and Add Another Task to save the task and add another.
6. Click Save Task when you’ve finished adding tasks to return to the task list.
1. In the Task Plan Manager, click **Browse Templates**.
2. Search for the desired template and click the name.
3. Click **Customize Plan**.
4. Edit the name and description, then click **Save Plan**.

**Source**: https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/knowledgeBase/28408274

## Procedure 5: Email Integration - Needs reauthorization

**Steps**:

1. Start by clicking the trash can icon to remove your email from the integration settings. When asked 'Are you sure you want to delete this email integration?', click Delete.
2. Enter your email address into the Email field, then click Authorize Email
3. You will be prompted to sign in or choose your email account to continue to Top Producer CRM. Here you will provide your updated email account password if it has been changed.
4. Follow the prompts and select all access permissions to continue with the email integration setup. Below is an example of what you will see when selecting access permissions from a Google or Gmail account.
5. Your email account has now been reauthorized. A 'Please wait a few minutes while your email integration is being set up.' message will appear.
