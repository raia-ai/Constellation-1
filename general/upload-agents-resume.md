# Upload Agent’s Resume | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   To add an agent's resume to their profile, click on the "Offices/Agents" page.

                                                                             ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/154332bc-d2ef-4ca0-9603-5c97aefd4564)

*   On the Agent’s page, enter the name of the agent in the search bar and select the "edit" option by clicking on the … three dots under the Action column.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/4a9f022d-eb57-410b-8b13-8ff7d835c615)

*   On the Agent edit page, scroll down the screen find the Resume option and click on it. Then upload the Agent’s resume from your system.
    
    **Note:** The format of the upload file should be in PDF or Word.
    

                   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/4c0062cf-9129-4d24-9988-b021a7ad9e14)

*   Once the resume is loaded click on the “Upload Resume” button.

.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5aff4926-170c-4350-9fab-db07c3f37077)

*   After uploading the resume you can click on "View Resume" to view it. or click on the Remove file option to upload a new one if needed.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/252a1af5-8cc6-4a5b-8e49-ab82d988ea7b)
