# Referral Upload Workflow | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   In order to upload multiple referrals to your account, click the “Add” button on the “Clients page”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f3128a67-4c57-45b4-89ef-a5e71028f492)

*   Select the “Bulk Upload Referrals” option from the drop-down menu.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/3bde6b60-dced-4660-80e1-80675103773b)

*   A modal box will open, now click on the “Download Template” button to download it  
    to your computer system.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/be32ba4b-cb8c-49b4-82ef-b99d9305ba1c)

*   Open the downloaded template file and fill in the required fields as per your  
    requirements and save it.

  
**Note:** Make sure to complete the Date Received, Client First Name and Client Last Name columns for all records, as they are required to upload the template.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e1feb03b-c9a3-4659-a658-c6523de84e59)

*   To upload a referral template, click the text "Click here to upload file" in the modal  
    box and select the file from your computer system.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/dd501ada-0455-476e-a95f-efd55f9c4f6a)

*   Preview the data and then click the "Save" button to upload all records into your account database table.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6f3518f4-9f57-459c-965e-7a5be808ae16)
