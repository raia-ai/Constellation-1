# 4/9/2024 - Changes in FargoMoorhead Area Association of REALTORS data feed

We are pleased to inform you that the FargoMoorhead Area Association of REALTORS data feed will be live tomorrow on April 9th, 2024 through a vendor system conversion. As a direct result of this feed conversion, you will be noticing several changes in the existing feed:

*   Increase in listing counts due to a considerable amount of shared data from the Northstar MLS data that is now available through this feed
*   Values in the following fields will also be changing:

*   OfficeMlsIds
*   ListingsIds
*   MemberMlsIds

To facilitate a seamless transition for your operations, we have attached detailed information outlining the upcoming changes we were able to identify.
