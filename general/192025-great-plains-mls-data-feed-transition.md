
# 1/9/2025 - Great Plains MLS Data Feed Transition

We are writing to inform you that the Great Plains MLS data feed (OSN SalinaMLS) has been dissolved. According to our recent updates, the data previously available in this feed is now accessible through the South Central Kansas MLS data feed (OSN SouthCentralKansas).

Please note that while current records are available in the new feed, historical records have not yet been incorporated.
