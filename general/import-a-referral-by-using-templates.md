
# Import a Referral by using Templates

*   In order to import a referral from another source received through an email,  
    click on the “Add” button on the right corner of the “Clients” page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/36a541de-1f8e-4b3c-8518-a135544124b1)

*   Select a template as shown in the drop-down menu (OpCity, e-relo, Veterans United, HomeLight, Broker Center).
    
    **Note:** OpCity has been selected as an example in this article to show the workflow.
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/b39c5d14-abbc-4424-83d7-a1e46f36cedd)

*   A modal box will be opened, just copy the referral information as mentioned in the email and paste it into the box, then click on the “Post” button.

                   ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e4c67ca2-1ebb-461c-82ed-63badd580227)

      ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2272425d-2eb5-414b-b23c-5f544d927448)

*   When you click the “Post” button, the page will refresh and the reference will be added to the table.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/0e526484-2091-4875-8e34-212dec3e0128)
