# Adding Custom Music | Constellation1 Customer Hub

Adding Custom Music&#x20;

Paradym has a plethora of songs already available for use for your visual tours.

However, if you have a specific song in mind that you prefer that we do not have, you can import one to use!

**Step 1)** Sign into your Paradym account.&#x20;

**Step 2)** Click on your name in the upper right and go to My Tours

**Step 3)** Click on the tour you want to change the music on.&#x20;

**Step 4)** Look on the left hand column and click on Music.&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/ace117c1-3879-4205-ae5c-3e22349610d2.png)

**Step 5)** Click on Import&#x20;

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/28f4b65c-5fb3-4040-ac8a-91b3fa047115.png)

**Step 6)** Below is what you will see next.&#x20;

&#x20;           Keep in mind, you must own the rights to use this music. You are responsible for obtaining the necessary permission before permitting any copyrighted material that belongs to others to be placed on the Paradym service.  \*You must click the check box stating, "I accept the responsibility for the music file I am uploading."  prior uploading the music.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c2100c63-a143-494d-835f-baec947fd93d.png)

**Step 7)** Click on the Choose File button. You will next locate and select the song you wish to upload.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/cd9a855e-a2c8-46fe-b0f6-7112f8adbe2d.png)

**Step 8)** Hit Upload in the upper right.&#x20;

**Step 9)** Scroll to the very bottom of the music list and the song will be there. Select it.&#x20;

**Step 10)** Hit Save in the upper right.&#x20;

The song has now been added to your Paradym visual tour.&#x20;
