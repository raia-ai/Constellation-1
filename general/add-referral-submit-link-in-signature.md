# Add referral submit link in signature

*   In order to add a referral submit link, click on the “Clients” page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6144fbed-19f2-4862-8e93-b11975f46164)

*   Click on the Add button and select “Referral Submit”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c3f7c4cb-2c3a-44fb-b7fc-133272a558d6)

*   Copy the complete URL link mentioned in the address bar.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a85629d9-0366-45ed-aafa-18a2d56bfb47)

*   Now click on "Preferences" section under the bell icon having name of your account.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c17dd40c-efb2-467d-be5e-2cf65aa08962)

*   Scroll down this page and find the signature section then click on Link button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/239670da-f083-4668-b5bb-d75a0f6fe23a)

*   Enter the text in “Text to Display” which you want the receiver to see i.e "Click here to submit a referral" and then paste the copied link under this field and click on "Insert Link" button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9581d994-6f3c-4d73-bb3c-cfac2420e677)

*   In order to save this link in your signature email click on "Save" button under signature section.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f0080a71-2f3a-49b3-a1e9-fea95aa81efe)

**Note:** When a receiver clicks the link, a page opens and when they SUBMIT the page it goes to your Import table. To view it click on “CLIENTS” page, click “IMPORTS” which opens the Import page, then if you need to click column heading ‘How Old?’ which will sort the records showing the newest ones first.
