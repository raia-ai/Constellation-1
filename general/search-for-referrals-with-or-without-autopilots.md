# Search for referrals with or without Autopilots

*   To check if referrals have any Autopilots assigned to them, click on the “Clients” page.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/57f56a4b-8c95-4e8b-bf97-f1ed925014d2)

*   On the “Clients” page, click on the “Filter” button.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/36645b57-fe34-4b5f-a0d6-a97b79ea2a00)

*   Scroll down until you find the “Current Tasks” field. Select the required option from the drop-down menu and click on “Search” button.

**Note:** The “Yes” option will display a list of all referrals that have Autopilots scheduled, while the “No” option will display all referrals that have no Autopilots scheduled.
