
# REALedger - How to Change Password

The purpose of this article is to show how to change your password in REALedger.Â 

To change your password in REALedger, click on **Tools**\>>**Change Password**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/69031d7c-849c-4a4a-af90-3659afe8a5e3.png)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/153e2ca6-5cee-4c31-9291-0bc16c35ade9.png)

*   Enter **Current Password**
*   Enter your new password into the **New Password** & **Confirm New Password** field
    *   Password must be 5 or more characters
*   Click **Apply New Password**

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/b6f66537-5c6d-4bb7-8b47-ed3156147fe7.png)

*   Click **OK** to the **Password Changed!** pop-up

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/f2b9195d-a039-4313-aead-32c38606b0ba.png)

*   Click **X** to close **Change Password** pop-up

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/aa22dcba-21fb-449e-ad04-ecd3c6e82ecb.png)
