# 05/02/2023 - CRMLS and CRISNet

On 05/02/2023 members of the Southland Regional Association of Realtors (SRAR), also known as CRISNet MLS, joined CRMLS as a participating Association. CRISNet data is now available in the CRMLS feed. At this point, we have detected no roster ID or MLS number changes.
