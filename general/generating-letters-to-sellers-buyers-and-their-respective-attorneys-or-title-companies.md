# Generating Letters to Sellers, Buyers and their Respective Attorney's or Title Companies

The  letter templates may be customized and once done you can then either email or print these Letters.

*   Navigate to Transaction > Main tab  
*   Do not EDIT the Transaction 
*   Double click on the Seller/Buyer name (or their representation) and select the WORD icon to the right of the displayed default letter template.
