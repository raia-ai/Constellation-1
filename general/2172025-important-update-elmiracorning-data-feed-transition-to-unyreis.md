
# 2/17/2025 - Important Update: ElmiraCorning Data Feed Transition to UNYREIS

We want to inform you that the **ElmiraCorning Regional Association of REALTORS** data feed (OSN **ElmiraCorning**) is merging into the **Upstate New York Real Estate Information Services (UNYREIS)** data feed (OSN **UNYREIS**).

**Key Dates**:

*   **February 17, 2024** – ElmiraCorning data will begin appearing in the UNYREIS feed.
    
*   **February 19, 2024** – The ElmiraCorning feed will become stale and no longer receive updates.
    

To ensure continued access to the latest data, please transition to **OSN UNYREIS** for ElmiraCorning listings.
