# BMA Form | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   In order to use BMA form, click on drop down menu icon just next to your Account name on the top right corner of the screen and select “Preferences” page from the menu.

  
![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d412757a-060a-4a03-966c-29e20cc25874)

*   Scroll down the page and find “Forms” section button and click on it.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/f982f1a8-d255-48d7-a841-e78ab3f118dc)

*   Click on “All Forms” section, search the form by typing “BMA Form” in the search bar and click on it. A new modal window will be opened, select the client’s name and fill all fields before sending it to the recipient.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/572474a7-a7b7-412b-9485-d72b7ef455d1)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/1f8e3300-0175-4893-8b8d-1c144dcca0f8)

*   BMA form can also be sent from the individual client record directly. For this  purpose, we need to go to

“Clients page” -> Client Referral -> Tasks -> Add Tasks”

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5e907422-7dcb-4180-a86f-d043b9b0b5f5)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ca580840-5a3c-4cdf-8d8b-0b47f58050d9)

*   Select the “BMA Form” from the “Form” drop down menu and fill in all the required information in fields before sending it to the recipient.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/32d34300-0024-4901-a856-cb39b3c2ef63)

*   Once the form is sent, the recipient on the other end will receive an email containing a link to the “BMA Form”.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/aae1b519-9977-4964-890e-437d1aed256f)

*   Click on the given link in the email, it will open the BMA form in a new window.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/695138da-e81f-42e6-a44b-5cc9529c9b38)

*   In the left, a list of form sections is given, the recipient can click on any section to get to a part of the form directly.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/465dc483-4294-43f7-93cc-bc86e0b22dd8)

*   The recipient can **Submit** the form by filling in all the required information in the form and clicking on “Submit” button which will ask for confirmation before submission.

**Note:** A form once saved can’t be edited/ retrieved again.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/ef49315a-cded-4da0-96fb-35718759d9d1)

*   Recipient can also **Save & Close** the form if he/she wants to fill any portion of the form later by clicking on “Save” button.

**Note:** In order to retrieve the saved form, the recipient should click on the BMA form link again in the email sent to him/her, to get it back.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/65813e3b-3fe6-47e8-a0ac-067cd8ada4c5)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/588c28a2-5d2b-43df-9ba0-6d67006e4e90)

*   Recipient can also **Cancel** the form by clicking on “Cancel” button. A new modal will be opened, select the reason and type the comments before clicking on “Submit” button for cancellation.

**Note**: A form once canceled can’t be retrieved back.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d01b301b-c5ef-4dd1-bdfa-c5a68e0da94a)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/666bd339-5f0d-4d8d-97da-1e18cb2f8357)

*   In order to choose and save picture for the document you need to scroll down the webpage till the last and find "Uploads" section.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c16de35d-7cf6-4bc8-b87e-4a046c1cc3cb)

*   Status of the form can be seen in “Notes” section of any individual client record. For this you need to go to

  
“Clients page -> Client Referral -> Notes”

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/61b3ed8c-4b97-4077-8b29-cc9bcd9d7b72)

*   Files of the form either submitted or canceled can be seen in “Files” section of any individual client record. For this you need to go to

“Clients page -> Client Referral -> Files”

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/57497f2f-a57c-478f-b4ff-f6f048f47662)
