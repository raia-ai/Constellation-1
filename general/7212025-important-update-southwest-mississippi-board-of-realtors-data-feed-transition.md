
# 7/21/2025 - Important Update: Southwest Mississippi Board of REALTORS Data Feed Transition

We’re reaching out to inform you that the **Southwest Mississippi Board of REALTORS data feed (OSN SouthwestMS)** will be discontinued. **Starting Monday, July 21, 2025**, the data previously available through this feed will be accessible via the **MLS United data feed (OSN MLSUnited)**.
