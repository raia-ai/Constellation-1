# 1/3/2025 - New Canaan MLS Operations Ending

We want to inform you that the New Canaan MLS system has suspended operations. As a result, the data feed is no longer available.

We recommend making alternative arrangements with your MLS subscriber and participant users to access the data you require. Many brokers and agents have already transitioned, or are in the process of transitioning, to SmartMLS or other MLS systems.
