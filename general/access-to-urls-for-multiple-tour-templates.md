# Access to URL's for Multiple Tour Templates

By following the simple steps outlined below, you will learn how to create a link for various types of Tour templates, applicable to both Branded and Unbranded Fusion Viewer Tours and Single Property Site Tours.

After completing the steps above, you'll have access to a variety of Tour templates, both Branded and Unbranded, for the Fusion Viewer and Single Property Site, all with just a few clicks.
