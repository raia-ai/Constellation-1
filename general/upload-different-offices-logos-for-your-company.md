# Upload different offices logos for your company

*   To upload different offices logos for your company click on the Offices/Agents page and edit the office details by clicking on the 3 dots menu … under the Action column.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/af0fb029-549d-4bfb-8ae0-35885e7587f7)

*   On the Office edit page scroll the page, find the “Upload new logo” option, and click on it to upload a logo. Once the logo is uploaded click on the “Save” button to update the record.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/935a6599-c23a-4af9-bf24-e5e3fc7624e9)

*   Click on the "Clients" page and edit the required referral.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/520e4b14-7958-4107-8989-5afb51ffd416)

*   Select the Agents tab to add an agent associated with the office and click on “Save”  
    button to update the referral record.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c7206822-66cf-45ce-8e29-79669b4fb27b)

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/90e7129f-ec49-4a7d-af9c-1453cde865a3)

*   Go to the “Tasks” tab and click on the “Add Task” button to send a form to a recipient.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/81ec4a28-e9cc-46b0-95ef-41b664d9e25b)

*   In the dialog box, select a form from the drop-down menu. Enter the required information, including the recipient's email address in the email field, and click send.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/5634e05e-86d1-417f-9145-028bee9fa813)

*   The recipient on the other hand will receive an email that will consist of the uploaded logo for the office.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c7ba3954-4cd5-4692-932d-8a155c5ca96b)
