# 1/6/2025 - Upcoming System Conversion for Greater Baton Rouge Association of REALTORS Data Feed

We would like to notify you of an upcoming change that may impact your use of the **Greater Baton Rouge Association of REALTORS** data feed.

On **Monday January 6th, 2025**, a vendor system conversion will be implemented for this data feed. To ensure a smooth transition, we’ve made the updated data available in advance. You can access this data by adding the **$reconvert=true** flag in your queries.

**Key values in all resources will be changing (ListingKey, MemberKey, OfficeKey, etc.)** as a result of this conversion.
