
# Attachment removal in email responses

*   In order to remove attachments in the email responses click on “Activities” and then select “Email Responses”.  
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e7905592-9ba3-4b29-a8f6-c55dd00e3132)

*   Click on “Email Responses” and select the emails having attachments in them which you want to remove before importing into any client.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/bdb37d08-5c18-42a9-9b70-91ac62239323)

*   Click on … dots under the Actions column and select view/import.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/d55f1b8b-cad9-4215-a7af-fc27e8c6f5bb)

*   Now view and click on x (Cross icon) next to each attachment that you want to remove before importing it to any client.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/92e47a12-2077-4bfb-b781-5f43ab15bd08)
