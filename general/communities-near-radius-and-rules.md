# "Communities Near" Radius And Rules

In the Communities Near section, you’ll find communities that are located within a 30-mile radius “as the crow flies” of the city you’re viewing. The communities are sorted to display custom communities first and communities nearest to farthest second. If no communities are returned then the carousel will not be displayed.
