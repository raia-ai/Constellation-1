
# Change the Status of multiple referrals at once

**Change the Status of multiple referrals at once**

In the Clients section, select the entries that require their status changed (first column on the left) and press “Filter”:

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/3f8fd7b9-4a3d-4836-aa8a-080f9bfa4032.png)

Once the Filter section is open at the top, press “Status Update”:  
![Graphical user interface, text, applicationDescription automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/353d97ed-8fd9-4735-b233-056ca8b9ef5b.png)

Select the necessary status from the drop-down menu (e.g.: Closed) and press “Change”:

![Graphical user interface, applicationDescription automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/1578c88e-997d-4ea2-b26c-2e8b01d6d082.png)![Graphical user interface, applicationDescription automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/373b7188-42d2-4f3f-bf1f-1fe6694208b3.png)

Upon seeing the “Are you sure?” pop-up – press “Ok”:

![Graphical user interface, text, applicationDescription automatically generated](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c918e8f6-908c-4d66-82de-05940276bdab.png)

The status of the selected referrals has been updated.
