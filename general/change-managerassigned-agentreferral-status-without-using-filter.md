# Change Manager/Assigned Agent/Referral Status without using Filter

*   To change either the “Manager”, the “Assigned Agent”, or the referral “Status”, click on “Clients” and type the name of the client in the search bar.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/c8ea8f31-424c-48ab-a3f1-ed7247e66921)

*   A list of referral(s) associated to that client will be displayed. Select the required referral(s) by clicking on their respective checkbox. Once selected, 3 buttons (**“M” “A” “S”**) will be shown above the “Add” button.
    
    **Note:  
    “M”** is for changing the “Manager” of the selected referral(s).  
    **“A”** is for changing the “Assigned Agent” of the selected referral(s)  
    **“S”** is for changing the current “Status” of the selected referral(s).
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/eb165a26-d3b6-4ea6-b50e-4db2a234e12f)

*   To change the "Manager" of the selected referral(s), click on the **“M”** button, select the name of the new “Manager” from the drop-down menu, and click on "Change".

                                               ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/e8fc3c47-70d1-4f77-9364-c0a31f1104bc)

*   To change the "Assigned Agent" of the selected referral(s), click on the **“A”** button, select the name of the new Agent from the drop-down menu, and then click on "Change".

                                          ![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/6e965882-9729-4745-a8fd-343fbffec52d)

*   To change the "Status" of the selected referral(s), click on the **“S”** button, select the new Status from the drop-down menu, and then click on "Change".
