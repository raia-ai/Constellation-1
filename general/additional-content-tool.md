# Additional Content Tool | Constellation1 Customer Hub

**Additional Content Tool Option**

Do you have a link, video clip, 3-D Model or perhaps a document you want to add to your Paradym tour?  
You've come to the right article!

Log into your Paradym account.

Click on your name in the upper right and hit **My Tours**. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/2b80136f-4d62-4bf1-aa38-f0b9d9cc7541.png)

Choose the tour you wish to add additional content to. 

The left hand column, under the **Options** section, you will find **Additional Content**. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/0f29ed10-282c-4c84-b57a-2c48441dcc2a.png)

Once inside this feature, click the plus sign in the upper right to choose what kind of content you wish to add. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/a477b2e0-eb51-444b-8327-ff502047001a.png)

To give a better idea of what differences are between **Focal**, **Featured**, **Tab**, and **More Info**, see the photo below. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c4ac86e2-41e0-4996-bcae-27002540a340.png)

Once you know which of those four you want to use, hit that plus sign again. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/6c5b2808-a445-47d1-83f3-de770ce195f0.png)

Click on the item you wish to add. Below are all photos of what each look like.   
They all are similar but you will see the key differences between them. 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/dbb928ec-7037-485f-81a5-b71416fd3354.png) 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/5369070a-7a38-43f8-8e48-3cd2699e8c8b.png) 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/c2894e55-2191-4dd0-91c0-a1879f42dcd6.png) 

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/image/2472652/0e75c20e-4151-41c2-b381-58f1c8f56bd7.png)
