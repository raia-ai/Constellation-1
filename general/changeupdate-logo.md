# Change/Update Logo | Constellation1 Customer Hub

*   Click on “Preferences” under the drop-down icon having the name of your account.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/2a53f9c8-2d7b-4232-b0ed-4d616d016aa1)

*   On preferences page, scroll down till last and click on “Drop files here to "Upload" button under Logo.  
    

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/a09f65c8-b545-4cb0-b0da-8ba978f774a7)

*   Click on “Upload” button after updating the logo and refresh the page to see your new logo.

![](https://constellation1.na3.teamsupport.com/api/attachments/action/1/9c55e43a-e8bc-49a4-baff-57ce4964b5d8)
